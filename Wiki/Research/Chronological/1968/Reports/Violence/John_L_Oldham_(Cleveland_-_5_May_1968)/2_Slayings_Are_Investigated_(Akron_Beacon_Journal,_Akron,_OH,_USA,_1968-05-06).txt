===== 2 Slayings Are Investigated =====

CLEVELAND (AP) — Detectives were investigating two shooting deaths Sunday on Cleveland's East Side. Minnie Stevenson, 27, was shot at a party in the apartment of 29-year-old Rosetta Wilcox. A 32-year-old man was being questioned. John L. Oldham, 30, identified by police as a female impersonator, was killed Sunday morning in an alley. Police said a relative of the victim was being questioned.
