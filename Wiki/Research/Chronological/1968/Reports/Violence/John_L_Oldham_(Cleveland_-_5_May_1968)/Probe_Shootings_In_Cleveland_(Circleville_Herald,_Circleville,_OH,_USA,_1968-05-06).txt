===== Probe Shootings In Cleveland =====

CLEVELAND (AP)—Homicide detectives were investigating two shooting deaths Sunday on Cleveland’s East Side. Miss Minnie Stevenson, 27, was shot to death at a party in the apartment of Rosetta Wilcox, 29, on Union Avenue. A 32-year-old man was being questioned.

John L. Oldham, 30, was shot in the head and killed Sunday morning in an alley. Police said a relative of the victim was being questioned.
