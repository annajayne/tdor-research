On the night of 2 July, 1961, farmer worker Rudy Vicks, 33, was stabbed several times by George Simmons, 34, in Simmons' shack on a farm on the road connecting Allentown and Crosswicks in the Hamilton Township, New Jersey. 

Rudy fled, and their partially decomposed body, wearing women's clothing, was discovered on the farm on 11 July, 1961.
