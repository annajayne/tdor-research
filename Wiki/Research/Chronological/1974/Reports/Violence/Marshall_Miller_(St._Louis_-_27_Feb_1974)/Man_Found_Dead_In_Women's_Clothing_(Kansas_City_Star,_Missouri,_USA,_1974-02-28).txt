===== Man Found Dead In Women's Clothing =====

St Louis (AP)— A 28-year-old man dressed in women’s clothing was found stabbed to death yesterday in his south St Louis apartment.

A neighbor discovered the body of Marshall Miller in the apartment kitchen. He had been stabbed in the chest and was wearing a blue skirt and a red-and-white blouse, police said

The stabbing apparently followed a quarrel between Miller and another man, who had been asked to leave the apartment police said
