===== Youth Is Charged In Fatal Stabbing =====

William Keed Jr., 18 years old, of the 2200 block of Benson Hurst Drive, St. Louis County, was charged yesterday with murder in the fatal stabbing of Marshall Miller, 28, at his home at 3601 Shenandoah Avenue, police reported.

The victim, whose body was dressed in woman s clothing, was found by a neighbor. Police said Miller had called them about midnight from a nearby home to complain that a woman was trying to break, into his apartment. When he returned home he found a radio-phonograph had been stolen.

A warrant charging murder was being sought from the circuit attorney's office, police said.
