Transgender woman Francis Nelson, 75, was stabbed to death in their room at 580 O'Farrel Street, San Francisco, on 11 November, 1974. Their killing was one of multiple related knife murders of transgender women in their rooms that happened in 1974 and 1975 in the Tenderloin District of San Francisco.

On 30 April, 1975, Raushee Onez, 32, was arrested and charged in the murder of Francis Nelson, and also in the murder of transgender woman Roger Carey, 42, who was found stabbed to death in their room on 25 April, 1975.
