===== Disease, scorn follow denizens of 6th Street =====

[...]

The female Impersonators believe they are abandoned by police and public alike and could "drop dead" and no one would care, Loretta said.

"The police are apparently not handling Miss Carol's case properly ("Carol" was a female impersonator who was murdered last year — a suspect has never been arrested or identified)," explained Loretta. "I heard some of the cops say after Miss Carol died that it was just one more queen they didn't have to mess with. They don't care what happens to us."

Austin, said Loretta, "is just a sorry place."
