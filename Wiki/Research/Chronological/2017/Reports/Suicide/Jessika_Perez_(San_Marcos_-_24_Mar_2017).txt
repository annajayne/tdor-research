> A member of the LGBT community was found dead in the municipality of San Marcos, inside his house where a hair salon operated, in the El Prado neighborhood of San Marcos.
> 
> The discovery was made on Friday afternoon. The victim responded to the name of Nelson Vergara Pérez and was a transsexual known as 'Jessika'.
> 
> He was 42 years old and was well known in the town for his work as a stylist. His death has surprised the entire population of the San Jorge region.
> 
> The community notified the authorities and after the respective inspection, they inspected the body.
> 
> The cause of his death is still unknown, but it is known that he allegedly took his own life. Authorities have not ruled out homicide.
