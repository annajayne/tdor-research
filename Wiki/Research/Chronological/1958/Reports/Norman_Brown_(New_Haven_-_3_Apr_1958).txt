Brown, a reported female impersonator, was found in their apartment stabbed-and-strangled to death on Friday 4 Apr, 1958. They were stabbed in the abdomen with a large carving knife, and a sheet was knotted about their neck in a crime described by the polices as "a crime of passion."

There were no known suspects.
