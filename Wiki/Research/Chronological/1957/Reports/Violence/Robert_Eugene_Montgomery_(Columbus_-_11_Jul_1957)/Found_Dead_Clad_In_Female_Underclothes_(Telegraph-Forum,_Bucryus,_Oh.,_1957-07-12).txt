Found Dead Clad In Female Underclothes

COLUMBUS (UP)— Robert E. Montgomery, 24, was found dead Thursday night in a bedroom of his home He was clad in feminine underclothing.

Coroner Robert Evans said the cause of the death would not be known until further studies have been made. He said Montgomery had been dead for several days.

The body was attired in a woman's negligee A girdle waa pulled over the head and twisted at the back of the neck. Also in the room were pieces of radio and electrical equipment.
