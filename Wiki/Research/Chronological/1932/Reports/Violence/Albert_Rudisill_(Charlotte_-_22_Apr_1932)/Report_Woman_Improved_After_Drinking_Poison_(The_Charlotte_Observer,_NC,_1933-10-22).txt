===== REPORT WOMAN IMPROVED AFTER DRINKING POISON =====

Nellie Long, 230 North Brevard street, was reported last night to be somewhat improved from an illness caused by the drinking of a poison She was taken to St Peter's hospital.

The young woman several months ago figured in the mysterious Rudisill case. She is alleged to have informed the police that two men attempted to give her a poisonous drink because she "knew too much about the death of Albert Rudisill”. Officers thereupon renewed their investigation but all clues petered out and the case remains as much a mystery today as on the day the death occurred. Rudisill's bodv was found ayjear ago in a Charlotte warehouse. The body was dressed in woman’s apparel.
