===== Jury Selected In Slaying Case =====

NEBRASKA CITY, (AP), A Jury was being selected Monday to hear the case of George O. Wells, jr., charged with first degree murder in the death of a circus performer.

Wells, of St. Joseph, Mo., is charged with murder in the death of Jesse Charles Dillard, also known as Jack Lopez, a female impersonator in a Rogers Brothers circus sideshow attraction.

Dillard's body was found In a box car July 15 by Milo Longwell. Wells was arrested at St. Joseph. 

Wells' case will be the first for the September term of district court.
