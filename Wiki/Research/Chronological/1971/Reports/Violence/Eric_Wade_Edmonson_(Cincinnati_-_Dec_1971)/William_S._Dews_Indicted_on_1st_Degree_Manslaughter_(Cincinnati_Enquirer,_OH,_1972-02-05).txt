William Sonny Dews' 30, 1334 Linn St., was indicted for first degree manslaughter by the Hamilton County Grand Jury Friday on charges of killing a man described by police as a female impersonator.

The body of Eric Wade Edmonson, 28, 748 Ridgeway Ave., dressed in woman's clothes, was found near 515 Poplar St., on December 3. He had been shot once in his head.
