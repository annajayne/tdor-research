===== Man Indicted In Fatal Beating =====

A Paterson man charged with fatally beating a woman with whom he resided was indicted by the Passaic County Grand Jury on charges of murder.

James King, 46, of 71 Tyler St., was indicted for the murder of Miss Hazel Savage, 47, last Dec 6. Police at the time of the arrest said King fatally beat the woman with a piece of wood in the bedroom of their apartment. Miss Savage died the next day.

King will be arraigned in Passiac County Superior Court on Tuesday.
