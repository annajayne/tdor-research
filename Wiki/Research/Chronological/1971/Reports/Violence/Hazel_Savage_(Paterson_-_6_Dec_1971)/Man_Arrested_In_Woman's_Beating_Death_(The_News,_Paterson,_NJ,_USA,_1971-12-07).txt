===== Man Arrested In Woman's Beating Death =====

Police have charged James King, 46, of 71 Tyler St., with murder following the death early this morning of Hazel Savage, whose semi-nude body was found in a pool of blood in the bedroom at the Tyler St. Address.

Called to the scene at 11:25 p.m. to investigate an "injured person," police said they met King who appearred incoherent and led them to the bedroom.

The victim died at 3 a.m. in St. Joseph's Hospital where authorities said she had a fractured skull, fractures of both arms, a fractured leg and internal injuries.

Police said they found a heavy wooden oard and a broken hammer in King's bedroom.

Det. Charles Council is in charge of the investigation.
