At 3:15 am on the 20th of November, 1971, the body of Frank Monroe Shideler was dumped out of a maroom late-model Olsdmobile on the southbound 10th Street exit ramp of the North Expressway. They were dressed in women's clothes complete with makeup, wig, and purse. They had been shot in the left side of their neck multiple times. The police said they believed Shideler was a female impersonator who was in Atlanta looking for work.
