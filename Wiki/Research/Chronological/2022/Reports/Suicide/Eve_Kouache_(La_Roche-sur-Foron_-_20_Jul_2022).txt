> TDoR - 1981-2022 - Tribute to Eve, Trans Woman
> 
> Her name was Eve Kouache who died by suicide on July 20, 2022 in La Roche-sur-Foron (74) at the age of 41, and was born in Lyon 7th arrondissement (69) on July 7, 1981.



> Call for witnesses: Arrest of Eve KOUACHE, a transgender homeless woman
> So that it doesn't happen to anyone else...
> 
> August 13, 2023
> Eve KOUACHE case (7/7/81-20/07/22) Call for witnesses: Arrest of a transgender homeless woman on 10/07/2022 at Evian Les Bains station (74)
> Reminder of the facts
> 
> On 10/07/22 Eve KOUACHE, 7/7/81-20/07/22, transgender of 1.50m, Disabled Adult who lived in Nantes, was arrested at Evian station, while she had taken refuge there to escape from attackers. Stolen of all her identity papers and means of communication, no one believed her female identity, certified by the TGI. Hospitalized under X, she was found hanged in EPSM on 20/07/22. No autopsy. Family notified 6 days after death. Let's look for witnesses or videos of this arrest. We do not believe in suicide.
> 
> If you were passing through or on holiday in July 2022 in Haute-Savoie and if you have video, visual or audio testimonies, help me understand why Eve, who lived in Nantes and was recognised as a disabled person, was the victim of several discriminations and inhuman treatment.
> Contact: merle.oberon(at)laposte.net
> 
> Thank you all!
