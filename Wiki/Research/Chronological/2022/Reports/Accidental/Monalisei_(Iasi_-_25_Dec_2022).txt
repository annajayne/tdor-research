> Monalisei, a young woman from the transgender community, known from Iasi, was burned alive, live, on Facebook . The cat knocked over a lighted candle on the bed, and everything was alight in a few minutes. Online followers called 112 and reported the tragedy. The rescuers arrived too late and could not save her. The young woman's family believes that a criminal hand is behind the tragedy.
