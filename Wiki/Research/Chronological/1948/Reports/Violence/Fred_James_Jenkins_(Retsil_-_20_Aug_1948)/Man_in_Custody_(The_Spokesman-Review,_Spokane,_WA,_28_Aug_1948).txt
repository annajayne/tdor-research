===== MAN IN CUSTODY =====
PORT ORCHARD, Wash., Aug. (AP)—Sheriff Pete Henderson said a man was taken into custody for questioning in the strangulation death of a 14-year-old Retsil boy.

The boy, Fred James Jenkins, was found dead at his home last Friday. The youth's body was found hanging from a closet rafter. The boy, Fred James Jenkins, was found dead at his home last Friday. The youth's body was found hanging from a closet rafter, with a silk scarf knotted around his neck. The boy was completely dressed in his mother's clothing when found.

A coroner's jury found his death was caused by "a person or persons unknown."
