Note Found On Boy, Hanged, Disappears

BREMERTON, Wash. (AP)—Police disclosed Wednesday that a note which said only: "Tell me tonight," was found in the woman's suit which Fred James Jenkins, 14, was wearing when found hanged in the family home Aug. 21.

Sheriff's deputies made the disclosure in reporting that the note had disappeared. , They could offer no explanation for the disappearance of the note, pointing out that it had been kept in the hands of officials since the body was discovered.
