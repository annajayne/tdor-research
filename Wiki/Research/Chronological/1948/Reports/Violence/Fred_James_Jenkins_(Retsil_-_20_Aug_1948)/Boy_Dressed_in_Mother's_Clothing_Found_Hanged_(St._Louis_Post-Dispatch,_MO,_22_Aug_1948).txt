===== BOY DRESSED IN MOTHER'S CLOTHING FOUND HANGED =====
PORT ORCHARD, Wash., Aug. 21 (AP)—Completely dressed in his mother's clothing, a 14-year-old boy was found hanged here last night.

Coroner Al H. Segerquist said the boy, Fred James Jenkins, died of strangulation. Authorities are investigating possibilities of suicide and of foul play.

The parents, Mr. and Mrs. James C. Jenkins, told authorities they were attending a veterans' organization meeting at the time of their son's death. Thev said they could not shed any light on the tragedy. The son had never been known to dress in women's clothing at any time, they said.
