> 
> Maryuri would have left her hometown in search of better job opportunities in the aforementioned country. However, she began to experience health problems and this would have led to her suffering cardiorespiratory arrest.
> 
> Regarding the trans woman, it was established that in the capital of Magdalena she lived in the Once de Noviembre neighborhood, where she was widely known for her work as a model for the LGBTI community.
