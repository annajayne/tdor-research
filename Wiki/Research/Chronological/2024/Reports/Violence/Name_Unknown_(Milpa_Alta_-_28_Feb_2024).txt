> The body of a transgender woman was located in an area located in the Pueblo San Pablo Oztotepec neighborhood, in the Milpa Alta mayor's office, while in Tláhuac, a head was found. The Attorney General's Office of Mexico City (FGJCDMX) is working to determine the identity of the remains, as well as whether both limbs correspond to the same person.
> 
> The first investigations indicate that the victim was found by local residents in an open area, on the edge of a local road.
> 
> After receiving notification of the facts, the agent from the Femicide Crime Investigation Prosecutor's Office began the investigations and ordered the forensic service to carry out the removal of the body.
> 
> At the same time, detectives from the Investigative Police (PDI) are searching for family members and possible witnesses, in addition to locating video surveillance cameras that will clarify the facts.

> They find the body of a trans woman in Milpa Alta; she was found in terrible conditions
> With signs of violence and in terrible conditions, the body of a trans woman was located in Milpa Alta; authorities investigate the terrifying discovery
> 
> CDMX authorities received a report about the discovery of the body of a trans woman in the Milpa Alta mayor's office and upon arriving at the scene, they located her in terrible conditions.
> 
> According to the first testimonies, the deceased showed signs of violence, in addition to the fact that a weapon was near the Milpa Alta region where it was located.
> 
> Thus, due to the terrible conditions in which her body was found and because her identity is still unknown, they transferred the trans woman to the ministerial amphitheater .
> 
> With the latter, the authorities seek to clarify the causes of death and how the terrifying crime occurred in Milpa Alta that could be classified as a hate crime .


> This Wednesday, February 28, they found the body of a trans woman in the town of San Pablo Oztotepec in the capital's mayor's office of Milpa Alta , but her death was not the most unfortunate thing about the case.
> 
> And, according to testimonies and reports from the authorities, the terrible conditions in which she was located included that the trans woman had been decapitated and physically attacked.
> 
> In addition to this, security forces that attended the case assure that next to the victim was the knife with which she could have been attacked and slit her throat.
> 
> While the head of the trans woman, which was no longer attached to her body, could not be located in said region of Milpa Alta , thus causing difficulties in finding her identity.
> 
> Due to the terrible conditions in which the body was found, the CDMX Prosecutor's Office will carry out an investigation into the case , as in this way they estimate to solve and punish the shocking crime.
> 
> “Investigative Police detectives are searching for family members and possible witnesses, in addition to locating public and private video surveillance cameras that will clarify the facts.”
> 
> Hate crimes are crimes that are committed motivated by prejudice or bias against vulnerable groups due to factors of race, sexual orientation and identity, gender or religious belief .
> 
> Therefore, upon determining that the body found in Milpa Alta in terrible conditions corresponds to a trans woman, the possibility of analyzing the crime under this definition has been raised.
> 
> However, in the first instance, it is planned to find the identity of the victim in order to have more information elements that can unearth the possible triggers of the crime.
