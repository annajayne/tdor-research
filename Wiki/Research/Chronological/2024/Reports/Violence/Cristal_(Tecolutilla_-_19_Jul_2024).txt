> Trans woman executed in Tecolutilla
> A trans woman was executed during the early hours of this Friday while she was walking in public in Ciudad Tecolutilla, Comalcalco.
> 
> The events occurred when the person was at the corner of Gregorio Méndez and Matamoros streets in Sector 2, and according to witnesses, armed subjects traveling on a motorcycle shot him at point-blank range.
> 
> Due to the multiple projectiles that hit her body, the person identified as Cristel "N" died instantly.
> 
> The victim's lifeless body was transferred to the Forensic Medical Service, while the State Attorney General's Office is already investigating the events.
