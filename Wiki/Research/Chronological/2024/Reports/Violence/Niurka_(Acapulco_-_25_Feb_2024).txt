>

>

> ACAPULCO, Gro., February 25, 2024.- A transgender woman was shot and wounded on Avenida Costera Miguel Alemán in the Condesa area.
> 
> According to police reports, the attack occurred at 5 a.m. this Sunday; According to witnesses, armed men arrived at the scene and attacked their victim, who was about to get into a March-type Nissan vehicle.

> Paramedics from the Red Cross came to the scene, who provided first aid and took her to a hospital for medical attention; her state of health is unknown at this time.
