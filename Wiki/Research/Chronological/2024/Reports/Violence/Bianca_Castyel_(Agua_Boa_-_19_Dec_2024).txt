> The young woman identified as Bianca Castyel, 20 years old, found dead in Água Boa (631 km from Cuiabá), this Thursday (19), had perforations and was lying naked in bed. The information was confirmed by the Official Technical Identification Expertise (Politec) which reported that it is still waiting for the results of the complementary exams for the "complete definition of the mechanism of death". So far, no suspect has been identified or arrested for the crime. The city's delegate reported that the case is still being investigated. 
> 
> Bianca Castyel was a transsexual and blogger, with over ten thousand followers on Instagram alone. On the social network, she shared photos of her "looks" and makeup. 
> 
> As reported by HiperNotícias,  Bianca was found dead inside the studio apartment where she lived in the Operário neighborhood of the city. The case is being treated as intentional homicide. 



> The body of a young transsexual woman identified as Bia Castyel, 20 years old, was found on the afternoon of this Thursday (12/19), victim of homicide in the municipality of Água Boa (620 km from Cuiabá). The young woman was found dead in a kitchenette room.
> 
> Bia, the name by which she was known in the city, worked in a beauty salon and was found dead on top of the bed, in a room in the studio apartment where she lived.
> 
> The case, which is still being investigated by the Civil Police, is being handled as intentional homicide, when the perpetrator intends to kill, due to the fact that the victim has bodily injuries resulting from physical force.
> 
> A team from the Official Expertise and Technical Identification (POLITEC) is already on site and is carrying out all necessary procedures to identify the causes of death.



> A 20-year-old transsexual woman identified as Bia Castyel was found dead inside a studio apartment in the city of Água Boa on the morning of this Thursday (19).
> 
> According to initial information, Bia's body was found around 1 pm in the Operário neighborhood. There is still no information about the circumstances of her death.
> 
> It was reported that the door to the young woman's house had been broken down, and she was found on top of the bed. The body was sent to the Forensic Medical Institute (IML) for an autopsy.
