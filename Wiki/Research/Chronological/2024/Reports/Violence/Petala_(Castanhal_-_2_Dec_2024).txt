> Trans woman shot dead by motorcycle duo in Castanhal
> The victim already had a history of drug trafficking.
> The Liberal
> 12/3/24 1:42 PM
> 
> A trans woman, identified as Pétala, 20 years old, was shot dead on Rua Pedro Ulisses, in the Propira neighborhood, in Castanhal, in the northeast of Pará. The crime occurred on Monday night (2/12), when the victim was in front of a residence. The suspects of the crime are two men who arrived on a motorcycle.
> 
> According to initial information, the crime occurred around 9 pm. The victim had gone out to buy a snack when he was surprised by the criminals. The men shot the victim and fled. Pétala died instantly. People who were passing by reported the death to a Military Police vehicle from the 5th PM Battalion.
> 
> The Civil Police were at the scene to investigate the crime and gather information that could help solve the case. According to police information, Pétala was a well-known figure in the area due to her involvement in no crimes. She had already been detained seven times by the police, three of them for drug trafficking. However, there is no confirmation as to whether her past life was linked to the murderer.  
> 
> In a statement, the Civil Police reported that the Castanhal police station is investigating the homicide. “Forensic examinations have been requested,” they said.
