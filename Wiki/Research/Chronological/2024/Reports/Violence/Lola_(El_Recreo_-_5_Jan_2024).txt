> Social organizations reported this Monday, January 8, 2024, the first transfemicide perpetrated in the El Recreo sector, south of Quito. Collectives ask national authorities to take action to find him or those guilty of the alleged murder.
> 
> Lola (protected name), 25 years old, was originally from El Carmen, province of Manabí, but lived in the capital . On January 3, 2024, she left her home with the purpose of meeting with a client, but she did not return home .
> 
> The young woman was reported missing. However, on January 5, she was found dead in the El Recreo sector .
> 
> This happened after an encounter with a client, so it is presumed that the murderer could be that subject.
> 
> Asociacíon Silueta X reported that there is no data that can determine the identity of the client and alleged criminal.
> 
> This social organization and other LGBT+ groups call on the State Attorney General, Diana Salazar , and the General Police Commander, César Zapata , to take action to find the culprit and ensure that the case does not remain unpunished.
> 
> They also request the intervention of the Ministry of Women and Human Rights, the Undersecretary of Diversity and the Ombudsman's Office.
