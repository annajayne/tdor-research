> Man stabs transvestite to death and tries to dismember her body
> December 2, 2024
> 
> The Municipal Civil Guard (GCM) of Rio Verde arrested around 1 am this Monday (2) a man accused of stabbing Elson Nascimento de Lima, 34, to death inside a residence on Rua Otoni Carvalho, in the Jacutinga neighborhood, in Jataí. The information is from the Rota Policial Notícias profile.
> 
> The GCM agents were informed by a team from the Fire Department (CBMGO), which was already on site, that there was a naked and injured man inside the residence and that he was refusing help.
> 
> The perpetrator, aged 37, was approached by the GCMs and reported that he had hired Elson, a transvestite, to deliver drugs to his home and invited the victim to use the drug together.
> 
> According to him, the two had a disagreement over the price charged for the drug, they got into a physical fight and the victim was stabbed in the neck, dying at the scene.
> 
> The residence showed signs of extreme violence, with a large amount of blood in the rooms. The GCM found Elson's body in the external area of ​​the house. The accused even tried to dismember the victim before the police arrived.
> 
> The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML)
> 
> The victim
> 
> Elson Nascimento de Lima was born in Rio de Janeiro, dark-skinned with short, curly hair.
> 
> The body will be buried this afternoon in Jataí. The family, who lives in another city, preferred not to hold a wake.
> 
> The Civil Police are investigating the case.
