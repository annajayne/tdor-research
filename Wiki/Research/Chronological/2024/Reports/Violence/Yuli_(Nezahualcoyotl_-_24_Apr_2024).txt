> (Photo: Valente Rosas)
> The Red 04/24/2024 15:20 Sergio Trejo Updated15:20
> NEZAHUALCÓYOTL, STATE OF MEXICO.- “Do not trust and put up with paunches” says the sign placed at the entrance to the Yuli Beauty Studio and which an armed man observed before murdering its owner.
> 
> Minutes before 10:00 on Tuesday night, a trans woman whom everyone in the Las Flores neighborhood, in Ciudad Nezahualcóyotl, knows as “La Yuli” was shot. 
> 
> Neighbors on Azahar Street, between Girasol and Geranio, heard the explosions and then saw the murderer leave and left the lifeless body of his victim lying among the hair that was on the floor of the establishment. 
> 
> Read also : “We never imagined it was a monster”: Sister of the Iztacalco Chemist breaks the silence
> 
> Although they immediately asked for the support of the paramedics, they could do little for her since she “couldn't stand” the beatings they gave her. 
> 
> Municipal police arrived at the scene and cordoned off the area, although they combed the surrounding streets to find the criminal's whereabouts, he had already taken off. 
> 
> Yuli was 35 years old, she was a stylist and worked late at night. Witnesses point out that it was common for several men to visit her.
> 
> Some to require a haircut and others poorly thought out pointed out that several of them arrived smelling of marijuana, so they do not rule out that drugs were sold there. 
> 
> The body that was not visible to the public was lifted and taken to the local Forensic Medical Service to perform the legal tests. 
> 
> While outside the aesthetics, family and friends lamented the violent death of “La Yuli.”
> 
> Another version claims that it was not just one gunman but three who murdered the stylist.
> 
> Given this, the authorities of the Attorney General's Office of the State of Mexico are investigating, from a gender perspective, whether it was a direct attack or some issue derived from the crime of extortion that caused this unfortunate transfemicide.




> In the municipality of Nezahualcóyotl, Estado de México, a terrible attack was recorded against the trans community, where a woman was shot to death inside her own establishment, which was a beauty salon.
> 
> This tragic event happened specifically in the Las Flores neighborhood, prompting nearby residents to alert law enforcement after hearing gunshots inside the premises located at the intersection of calles Azahar and Geranio.
> 
> Upon arriving at the scene of the event, the police found the sad scene of the body of the victim identified as Yuli, 35 years old, lying on the ground with several wounds caused by bullets, confirming her death on the spot. .
> 
> This led to the intervention of the Attorney General's Office of the State of Mexico (FGJEM), which proceeded to carry out the relevant investigations and remove her body.
> 
> The witnesses mentioned that the owner of the business was approached by two individuals, who entered the business and fired their weapons repeatedly, and then fled.
> 
> Despite a security operation deployed in the area after the incident, authorities have so far reported no arrests in connection with the homicide.
> 
> For their part, the staff of the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) based in the aforementioned Mexican town, were in charge of removing the body in order to take it to the morgue, where they will perform the legal autopsy, in addition to all the protocols for the terrible murder.
> 
> This case has generated consternation in the community and highlights the importance of continuing to work for the safety and rights of trans individuals in society.
