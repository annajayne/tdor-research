> Early in the afternoon of Tuesday (24), a 31-year-old trans woman was killed with several gunshots in the neighborhood of Lourdes, in the municipality of Itaúna. She was delivering saints for a candidate for city councilor in the city when she was discovered by the shooters. The victim was identified as 'Bruninha'. According to the PM, she was already known to the police. Witnesses said that she was delivering the saints of her sister, who is a candidate - After the crime, military personnel carried out investigations and managed to identify one of the perpetrators of the crime. The suspect was arrested and taken to a police station. So far, the police have not reported the motive for the murder of the trans woman. The occurrence is still ongoing…



> A homicide was recorded on Tuesday afternoon (September 24) in the Lourdes neighborhood, in Itaúna. According to the Military Police, the victim, a 31-year-old trans woman, was found lying on the ground, shot. The SAMU team was at the scene and confirmed the death.
> 
> According to the police, the victim was already known to the police, with records for theft, bodily harm and slander. During the investigation, one of the suspects, an 18-year-old, was identified. He also has a criminal record, including homicide, bodily harm, threats and damage, all of which occurred when he was a minor. The suspect was arrested and taken to the police station.
> 
> The teams continue to patrol to locate the second person involved in the crime. The incident is still ongoing.




> A 31-year-old trans woman was killed in the early afternoon of this Tuesday, around 2:20 pm, in the Lourdes neighborhood, in Itaúna. 
> 
> According to initial information, the victim, who is registered as a man, was dressed in women's clothing while walking along Rua Eduardo de Morais, known as Rua 7, at which point he was approached by individuals on a motorcycle and hit by several shots, where he died at the scene.
> 
> The victim, who is known to police, was found on the ground with head injuries. The individual is the sister of a Podemos candidate and was distributing leaflets for her.
> 
> The Military Police were called and attended the scene along with the forensic team. During investigations, one of the perpetrators was identified, approached and arrested. The teams continue to investigate to gather more information.
