> Andreína, a 50-year-old trans woman, was murdered in Medellín on December 31.


> In the early hours of December 31, a violent attack took the life of a person belonging to the LGBTIQ+ community.
> In the El Playón de los Comuneros neighborhood, in commune 2 of Medellín, Andreína García, a 50-year-old woman, was murdered with a sharp, screwdriver-type weapon inside a barbershop by a man of approximately 35 years old. 
> 
> According to the court report, the incident occurred around 1:40 a.m., when an individual entered the establishment and attacked the victim from behind, inserting the screwdriver. Andreina, seriously injured, was rushed by taxi to the Polyclinic, where she underwent surgery, but died due to the severity of her injuries.
> 
> The Metropolitan Police acted quickly and managed to capture the alleged aggressor, who was placed at the disposal of the Attorney General's Office. In addition, the authorities continue to investigate the motives for the murder.


> The latest case occurred at 1:42 am in the Playón de los Comuneros neighborhood, Santa Cruz commune. Andreína García García, a 26-year-old trans woman, was attacked with a screwdriver by a man who reportedly arrived at her house and stabbed her without saying a word . According to statements, the woman tried to flee when she was attacked but fell when she arrived at the CAI, where her lifeless body was found. The subject turned himself in to the authorities and the case is currently under investigation.
