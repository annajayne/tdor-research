> Trans activist Valentina Sody murdered in Temixco, Morelos
> by Megamedia GroupJune 27, 2024, 10:10 amJune 27, 2024, 10:10 am
> 
> MORELOS.- Yesterday morning, Wednesday, Valentina Sody , an LGBT+ activist from Temixco , died in the hospital where she was after admitted being attacked by armed subjects.
> 
> It was reported that on Tuesday night, two men on a motorcycle shot her in a bar in the Rubén Jaramillo neighborhood. The victim was preparing to participate in the show that was offered at the venue.
> 
> The murderers arrived at the Alebrijes bar, owned by the victim , they asked about Valentina, she came out of the dressing room without finishing dressing and they shot her twice .
> 
> Her body fell to the floor of the club and paramedics found her with vital signs, so they transferred her to the "José G. Parres" hospital in Cuernavaca, however, she died hours later .
> 
> In her daily life, Valentina identified herself as José Miguel and was an activist defending the rights of the Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Transvestite, Transsexual, Intersexual, Queer and Asexual (LGBTTTIQA+) community.
> 
> Prosecutor Uriel Carmona Gándara confirmed that Valentina's murderers fired their weapons from a moving motorcycle.
> 
> "Not even the two people riding the motorcycle got off. The passenger who was riding the motorcycle fired a shot, causing serious injuries," he said.
> 
> Uriel Carmona reported that the possible motive for the murder could be the payment for a flat, so they will continue with the investigations.




> Apartment fee would be the motive for the murder of Valentina Sody, trans activist
> Trans activist Valentina Sody is presumed to have been murdered in Morelos as a result of a floor fee
> 
> JUNE 27, 2024
> Morelos authorities are investigating the floor fee as a possible motive for the murder of trans activist Valentina Sody .
> 
> On the night of June 25, armed individuals broke into the Alebrijes bar in Temixco and asked for Valentina , who was the owner.
> 
> When leaving her dressing room to answer the call, the woman was the victim of two gunshot wounds.
> 
> Those responsible escaped while Valentina Sody was taken to a hospital in Cuernavaca where she died in the early hours of June 26.
> 
> The murder is believed to be a result of the extortion that has spread across several municipalities in Morelos.
> 
> With information from Teresa Flores
