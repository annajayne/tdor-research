Laila Ketellen was beaten to death by her ex-partner in Fortaleza, Ceará (Brazil) on 3 Jan 2024.

She was 23, and lived on the street.



> A 25-year-old man was arrested this Friday (5), in the Carlito Pamplona neighborhood, in Fortaleza, on suspicion of murdering his ex-partner, transvestite Laila Ketellen, 23, during an argument.
> 
> The victim was found dead last Wednesday (3), in the Jacarecanga neighborhood, with marks of violence and punctures with pieces of glass. The night before the crime, a witness filmed Laila trying to protect herself from the man's attacks.
> 
> As the investigations progressed, the Civil Police elucidated the case and identified the victim's ex-partner, Marcos Brandow Ribeiro, as the perpetrator of the crime. He has a criminal record for homicide, drug trafficking, theft and damage.
> 
> The man was taken to the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP) of the Civil Police, where he was charged with feminicide. The 4th DHPP police station is investigating whether the suspect was involved in other criminal actions in the region.





> A 23-year-old transvestite was murdered in Fortaleza , this Wednesday (3). The young woman, identified as Laila Ketellen, was found dead in Bairro Jacarecanga.
> 
> The night before the crime, the victim was filmed fighting with a man, who would be her ex-partner ( see the video above ). The information was passed on by a friend of the victim.
> 
> Laila was homeless and got involved with this man, who also lived on the street and worked as a garbage collector. She was killed in the early hours of the morning with a blow to the neck.
> 
> The Civil Police reported that the young woman was found with signs of violence. The Military Police and Forensic Experts were called to the location where the body was found. The Civil Police investigate the case as a homicide.
> 
> Regarding the video and the suspicion of the ex-partner involved in the case, the Security Secretariat said it does not comment on details of an ongoing investigation. The agency also said that other information will be provided later so as not to hinder the progress of the investigation.




> A transvestite was beaten to death in the Jacareganga neighborhood, in Fortaleza, this Wednesday (3). The body of  Laila Ketellen,  23, was found with signs of violence on a public road, according to the Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS).
> 
> According to TV Verdes Mares, a friend of the transvestite said that the death occurred moments after she fought with a man who was believed to be her ex-boyfriend. They both live on the streets. 
> 
> A video recorded the moment of the fight. Laila appears arguing with her ex, who is a collector of recyclable materials. They shout at each other, and at one point, the transvestite pushes the recycling cart. The man even picks up a piece of wood and points it at her. 
> 
> In a statement, the SSPDS reported that the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP), of the Civil Police of the State of Ceará (PCCE), went to the scene of the crime on Wednesday night to carry out the first surveys. 
> 
> The Military Police of Ceará (PMCE) and the Forensic Expertise of the State of Ceará (Pefoce) also responded to the incident. The 4th DHPP Precinct is investigating the case as a homicide.
