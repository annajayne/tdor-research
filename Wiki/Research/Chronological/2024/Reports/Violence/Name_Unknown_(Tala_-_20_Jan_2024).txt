> Guadalajara
> 01-20-2024
> The Jalisco prosecutor's office is investigating the murder of a person, allegedly trans, in the municipality of Tala.
> 
> According to the state agency's file, the crime was discovered this Friday at the confluence of calle Mirador Arenitas, in a property known as Loma de la Cuenta or Arroyo Seco. There the body of a man was discovered with several wounds apparently caused by a knife.
> 
> The investigation file was initiated under the specialized protocol due to versions that suggest that the victim used to dress as a woman and belonged to the LGBT community.
