> Transvestite who tried to break into building was killed by janitor, suffocated with a 'naked chokehold', says police report
> A condominium employee in São Carlos (SP) was arrested, but was released after a custody hearing. The homicide took place in the Parque Faber neighborhood on Monday (21).
> By g1 Sao Carlos and Araraquara
> 10/22/2024 3:18 PM  Updated3 hours ago
> 
> Transvestite Amanda Eduarda, 28, was killed with a "naked chokehold" in São Carlos — Photo: Reproduction of social networks
> 
> Transvestite Amanda Eduarda, 28, killed while trying to break into a building in the Parque Faber neighborhood , in São Carlos (SP), on Monday morning (21), died as a result of asphyxiation caused by a 'mata-leão' chokehold .
> 
> The information is contained in the death certificate issued by the on-duty forensic doctor and which was attached to the case's police report.
> 
> The building's caretaker was arrested, but was released after a custody hearing and will respond to the case in freedom. G1 was unable to contact his defense.
> 
> He must follow precautionary measures, such as appearing monthly in court and at all acts of the process, not changing address without notifying the court and keeping the address updated, under penalty of revocation of the benefit.
> 
> According to witnesses interviewed by the police, Amanda was behaving aggressively and had tried to enter the Iguatemi shopping mall, where she was stopped. She then went to the building located nearby and entered through the window.
> 
> In an attempt to restrain her, the building's caretaker applied a "rear naked chokehold", knocking her to the ground and holding her with his knee on her chest . The action was filmed by surveillance cameras.
