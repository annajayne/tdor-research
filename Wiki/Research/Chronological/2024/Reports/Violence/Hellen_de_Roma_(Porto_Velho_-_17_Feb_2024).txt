> HELLEN DE ROME: Transvestite brutally murdered in prostitution spot identified
> Anyone who has information that could lead to the arrest of the killer can make an anonymous report to the Civil Police number 197
> 
> Rondoniaovivo
> February 17, 2024 at 3:18 pm
> Updated on: February 17, 2024 at 3:48 pm
> 
> The transvestite brutally killed with a machete was identified by her family as Hellen from Rome.
> The crime took place in the early hours of Saturday morning (17) in a prostitution area on Rua Caparari, Nova Porto Velho neighborhood, in the capital of Rondônia.
> 
> Agents from the 1st Homicide Precinct are investigating the case. A man was reportedly seen attacking the victim with a machete and then fled in a car.
> 
> Clebson Silva Sá, 37, as the transvestite's name was on identification documents, there were several incidents of domestic violence against her mother and stepfather.
> 
> At the end of 2020, Clebson was even arrested after holding his mother hostage with a knife, threatening to kill her in a house in the Tancredo Neves neighborhood, east of Porto Velho.
> 
> Anyone who has information that could lead to the arrest of the transvestite's killer can make an anonymous report to the Civil Police number 197.



> The brutal murder took place in the early hours of this Saturday morning (17) on Rua Caparari, Nova Porto Velho neighborhood, in the capital of Rondônia.
> 
> The transvestite was killed with a machete. According to what Rondoniaovivo found at the scene, a man who arrived in a car was seen attacking the transvestite who worked in the area known as a prostitution spot with a machete.
> 
> After the crime, the killer fled the scene. A SAMU team was called and confirmed the victim's death. Agents from the Homicide Division will investigate the murder.
