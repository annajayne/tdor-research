> Trans woman stabbed to death in Constitución
> The victim was allegedly stabbed in the back during an alleged argument over the purchase of drugs. She lived there and was allegedly thrown from the first floor of the building.
> January 20, 2024·5:12 p.m.
> 
> A trans woman was murdered this morning in a violent and mysterious episode in the Buenos Aires neighborhood of Constitución . The victim reportedly suffered cutting injuries from an attacker who allegedly resided in the same building. The neighbors pointed out the alleged perpetrator of the crime to the police and she was arrested.
> 
> The dramatic episode took place in the early hours of this Saturday, on the sidewalk of a passenger hotel located at calle 1400 Santiago del Estero, in the aforementioned neighborhood of the City of Buenos Aires. It should be noted that the site was targeted as a drug " bunker " by the residents of the area.
> 
> It all started around 7 in the morning, when  Diva Reina de los Santos Contreras , originally from the Dominican Republic and with Argentine nationality, received the fatal attack.
> 
> The first versions indicate that the crime occurred inside a hotel room, but the victim was thrown from the first floor onto the sidewalk.
> 
> In this context, the aggressor would have been the woman identified as Miguelina García Díaz , another Dominican, who was accused by the residents of the area as the perpetrator of the crime.
> 
> Meanwhile, a team from Crónica HD approached the scene to find out all the details and talk to the people who witnessed the event.
> 
> As could be reconstructed, the first call to 911 alerted about an incident at the location, and although the police officers arrived in a few minutes, unfortunately they discovered the lifeless body of the woman on the sidewalk of the building.
> 
> Then, upon the arrival of SAME personnel, doctors confirmed that the victim had died a few moments before.
> 
> It is being investigated whether she was thrown from the first floor of the building, and the results of the tests and the preliminary autopsy are awaited to determine if she has any stab wounds on her body.
