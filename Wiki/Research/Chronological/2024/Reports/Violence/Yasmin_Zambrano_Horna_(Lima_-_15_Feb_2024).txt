> In the district of Independencia , another criminal act related to the mafias that control sex workers has been recorded during the early hours of this Thursday, February 15.
> 
> A 32-year-old transgender woman  , known as Yasmín, was murdered in front of her friends while she was at the door of a lodging drinking liquor.
> 
> According to witnesses, a hitman approached and shot her in the head, causing his death. The victim, a native of Loreto, had been threatened for refusing to pay the criminals who control the area. Her friends and neighbors tried to help her, but unfortunately they could not save her life.
> 
> The Police are already investigating the case and analyzing the security cameras at the scene to identify the person responsible for the murder.
