> Trans woman is found stabbed to death in Mogi Guaçu
> According to the Civil Police, Luma Ferrari was found dead on Rua Benedito Franco Maldonado, with deep wounds on the left side of her chest.
> The case is being investigated by the Mogi Guaçu General Investigations Department
> 
> A trans woman was found stabbed to death in Jardim Santa Teresinha, in Mogi Guaçu, on the morning of this Wednesday, 1st.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, Luma Ferrari was found dead on Rua Benedito Franco Maldonado, with deep wounds on the left side of her chest.
> 
> Samu (Mobile Emergency Care Service) was called and confirmed the death at the scene.
> 
> The homicide is being investigated by agents from the DIG (General Investigations Department) of Mogi Guaçu. To date, the suspect for the crime has not been located.
