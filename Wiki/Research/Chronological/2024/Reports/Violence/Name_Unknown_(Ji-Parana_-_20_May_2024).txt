> Transvestite's body is found in Beira de Mata in Ji-Paraná
> The circumstances of the death appeared to be a homicide carried out by stabbing.
> Transvestite's body is found in Beira de Mata in Ji-Paraná
> By rondoniatual.com
> Published on 05/20/2024 at 1:55 pm
> 
> On the morning of this Monday, May 20, 2024, the body of a transvestite was found on the edge of a forest located in the Barcelona residential complex, on the banks of RO-135, in front of UNIJIPA.
> 
> The discovery mobilized teams from the Military Police, Civil Police and the Technical-Scientific Police (POLITEC), who were called to the location, carrying out surveys.
> 
>  The body initially identified as that of a male person, “a transvestite”, was found by workers who were clearing the area and smelled the bad smell.
> 
> There is still no information about the identity of the victim; Due to the circumstances of the death, it was apparently a homicide carried out by stabbing, and then the body was carried about 30 meters and thrown into a forest.
> 
> The body has tattoos of stars on the leg, among others that are difficult to identify, as it is in a state of decomposition.
> 
> The area was isolated to carry out forensic examinations and collect evidence, which will help in the investigation of the case.
> 
> A transvestite reportedly reported the disappearance of a friend she works with on the 11th of this month. The case will now continue to be investigated by the Ji-Paraná Homicide Police Station.
