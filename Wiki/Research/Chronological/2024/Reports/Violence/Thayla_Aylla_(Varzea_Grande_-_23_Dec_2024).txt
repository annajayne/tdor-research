> Body of young transsexual found with signs of strangulation in undergrowth
> The causes of death are still being investigated, but according to police, the victim showed signs of strangulation.
> The body of transsexual Thayla Aylla, 24, was found in a thicket on Monday afternoon (23), in the Zero KM region, in Várzea Grande (MT). The causes of death are still being investigated, but according to the police, the victim had signs of strangulation.
> 
> The young woman had been missing since last Friday (20), when she left with a friend to go to a party in the Nova Switzerland neighborhood and since then she has not been heard from again.
> 
> Due to the lack of information about the victim, a relative of Thayla contacted the Civil Police's Missing Persons Unit, which began the search for the young woman.
> 
> On Monday, a resident who was passing through the region, near a distributor, found Thayla's body and called the police.
> 
> At the scene, the Official Expertise and Identification (Politec) and Civil Police teams found the body and identified that it belonged to the missing young woman.




> Missing trans teen was beheaded and dumped naked in VG
> Body was found in a wooded area, behind a distributor
> 
> Thayla, the 24-year-old transsexual who had been missing since Friday (20), was found dead in Zero KM, a region known as the main prostitution point in Várzea Grande, on the afternoon of this Monday (23). The victim's body was behind a distributor, in a wooded area, until the exit of Zero.
> 
> In addition to having her throat slit, Thayla was naked and had a bag around her neck. The victim's registered name is Thiago Henrique Santos de Souza. 
> 
> Preliminary information indicated that she had disappeared after getting into a client's car, also in the Zero region. According to the Civil Police Missing Persons Department, Thayla disappeared after leaving for a party.
> 
> According to the PJC, the young woman was a resident of the industrial city. Thayla went out with a friend to a party last Friday (20), but has not responded or contacted her family since then.
> 
> Teams from the Specialized Homicide and Personal Protection Unit (DHPP) are on site investigating the case. Teams from the Official Forensics and Technical Identification Unit (Politec) are also in the region.
