> Thursday, October 10, 2024 · 13:19
> The Buenos Aires town of Ensenada was the scene of a tragic and shocking event during the early hours of Thursday, when a truck driver was arrested for the murder of Florencia Luna Reinaldi, a transvestite between 38 and 39 years old. The body was found after a call to 911 alerted about desperate screams in the vicinity of the Regatas Club, which led to the immediate intervention of the Police.
> 
> Upon arriving at the scene, the officers found a truck stopped in the middle of the road. Inside the vehicle was Jhonatan Ariel Quintana, a 34-year-old man, in an evident state of agitation. Upon searching the truck, the officers discovered a bloody pipe , which is presumed to have been the weapon used to commit the murder.



> A truck driver arranged to meet a trans woman, killed her and dumped her body in a vacant lot in Ensenada
> A call to 911 alerted the police and the 36-year-old man was arrested minutes after committing the crime. An iron pipe with blood stains was found in the vehicle.
> October 10, 2024, 1:09 pm
> 
> 
> A truck driver was arrested early this morning for murdering a trans woman in the Buenos Aires town of Ensenada. The police managed to capture him inside a truck with an iron pipe that - it is believed - he used to kill the victim.
> 
> Buenos Aires police responded to a 911 call reporting a person screaming on the road to the La Plata Regatta Club, a grassland area bordered by the Doña Flora and La Fama streams and the Santiago River.
> 
> The officers arrived at the scene and found a Mercedes Benz truck in the middle of the road, something that caught their attention. Inside the vehicle was a man who looked agitated, according to the police report.
> 
> When they began to question him and search for the truck, the officers found an iron pipe with blood stains and a drag track that led to a pasture. There, among the bushes, they found the victim's body. She was half naked and had bruises on her head.
> 
> The truck driver was identified as Jhonatan Ariel Quintana, 34 years old. He was immediately arrested and initial investigations revealed that he had been reported for aggravated injuries on September 30.
> 
> The case was classified, for the moment, as simple homicide. It was placed in the hands of the Functional Investigation Unit (UFI) No. 16 , in charge of prosecutor Juan Cruz Condomi Alcorta.




> A 34-year-old man murdered a trans woman this morning. The incident took place on the road to the Regatas Club at entrance N6. 
> 
> Two witnesses heard desperate cries for help and notified the police. When officers from the Ensenada Patrol Command arrived at the scene, they found the victim's body on the side of the road with serious head injuries. The murderer was a few meters away from the scene and was identified as Jonatan Ariel Quintana, 34 years old. 
> 
> Quintana was on board a Mercedes Benz truck with license plate AYQ 415, the murdered trans woman was identified as Florencia Luna Reinaldi (39). At the scene the police seized an iron with blood stains and it was confirmed that it was the murder weapon. Prosecutor Juan Cruz Condomí Alcorta arrived at the scene and the case is being investigated as homicide. 
> 
>  A judicial spokesman said that Quintana went to look for his victim in La Plata (1 and 64) and then took her to a remote area in Ensenada. He will be questioned in court in the near future and the autopsy report will also be released. 
> 
> Finally, it was learned that Quintana had been imprisoned last month for gender violence and that is the only criminal record the accused has.
