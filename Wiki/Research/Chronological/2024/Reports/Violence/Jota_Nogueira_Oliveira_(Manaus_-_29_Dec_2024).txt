> Trans woman known as “Jota” is found dead on the Mount of Olives; watch video
> By Redação 3 on December 29, 2024 at 3:46 pm
> 
> Manaus – On the afternoon of this Sunday (29), a trans woman was murdered, in an abandoned lot on Avenida Margarita, in the Monte das Oliveiras neighborhood, North of Manaus.
> 
> At the scene, police found the body of a person thrown next to a tree, in a wooded area and with signs of aggression.
> 
> The victim, preliminarily identified as Jota Nogueira Oliveira, was found wearing women's clothing, with bruises and marks suggesting violence, including neck injuries, as well as signs of rigor mortis.
> 
> The Legal Medical Institute (IML) removed the body to the morgue.



> Trans woman found dead in back of steakhouse in Manaus
> Body was found in a wooded area, on Avenida Margarita, in the Monte das Oliveiras neighborhood, north of the capital
> Published on December 29, 2024 at 1:49 pm
> 
> Manaus- A trans woman was found dead with signs of strangulation on Sunday morning (29), in a wooded area, on Avenida Margarita, in the Monte das Oliveiras neighborhood, north of Manaus.
> 
> According to witnesses, the transvestite's body was thrown in a wooded area behind a barbecue restaurant and was found by the owner of the place who called the police. The victim showed signs of strangulation.
> 
> There is no information about the motive for the crime. The case will be investigated by the Specialized Homicide and Kidnapping Unit (DEHS). The Legal Medical Institute (IML) was called to remove the body.
