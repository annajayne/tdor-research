> Luciana Román was murdered in Bogotá; She is the seventh trans woman victim of homicide in Colombia this year. Prosecutor's Office assumes investigation
> Luciana had arrived in the Colombian capital two weeks ago with the hope of starting a new life. However, she was the victim of homicide in a hotel in Chapinero.
> 
> Nation Editorial
> April 27, 2024
> Luciana Román, trans woman from Medellín murdered in Bogotá. | Photo: Taken from X Jennifer Pedraza (@JenniferPedraz)
> The LGBTIQ+ community and society in general expressed their outrage at the murder of Luciana Román, a trans woman from Medellín, who was found dead in a hotel in the town of Chapinero, in Bogotá, last Thursday, April 25.
> 
> The Attorney General's Office announced this Saturday, April 27, that it is investigating the homicide and whether this event could be linked to a group of serial killers who attack people from the trans population and other members of the diverse community.
> 
> Luciana had arrived in the Colombian capital two weeks ago with the hope of starting a new life. However, her dream was violently cut short.
> 
> His loved ones, immersed in consternation, expressed their concern at not having heard from him for several days, despite his usual activity on social networks. Unfortunately, the worst suspicions were confirmed when his body was found with obvious signs of violence .
> 
> Mauricio Sánchez, a close friend of the victim, spoke exclusively with Noticias Caracol and remembered her as a warm woman, dedicated to her family and with an energy that “radiated joy around her.”
> 
> “She was a very warm woman, very dedicated to her family, an exceptional human being. She radiated joy wherever she went. Her goal was to try her luck in Bogotá. She was apparently going to stay for a few days to see how she moved and well, well, there she found her final destiny, sadly, ” she said.
> 
> Valeria Bonilla, activist and human rights defender of the trans community, as identified in her profile on the social network.

> She is the seventh trans woman to be murdered in Colombia in 2024
> After the violent event, the Attorney General's Office took over the investigation and confirmed that there is a specialized group to address six other homicides and femicides of trans women that occurred recently in different cities in the country, mainly in Valle del Cauca.
> 
> “The Prosecutor's Office assumed the investigation into the death of the trans woman Luciana Román, which occurred on April 25 in Bogotá. The National Working Group for the Investigation of Violence Based on the Sexual Orientation and/or Gender Identity of the Victims will support the activities that allow us to know the truth and find those responsible for this reprehensible fact,” reported the investigating entity.
> 
> He added: “ Special follow-up will be done on six other homicides/feminicides against trans women, which occurred in the last two months in Bogotá, Cali and Pasto. The Attorney General's Office regrets these acts of cruelty and reaffirms its commitment to investigating cases of violence due to prejudice, as well as its duty to prosecute the effects against LGBTIQ+ people."
