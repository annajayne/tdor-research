> ACAPULCO, Gro., August 20, 2024.- A transgender woman traveling on a motorcycle was shot to death on Avenida Cuauhtémoc, in front of the Estrella de Oro bus terminal at the port.
> 
> The homicide occurred a few minutes after 11 a.m. on Tuesday, when gunshots were reported, prompting the response of National Guard police.
> 
> The body was left lying face down with bullet wounds to the head and ribs, on the Y Griega-Centro lanes, while the motorcycle was left two meters away.
> 
> At least eight spent shells were found at the scene; the arrival of the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) is expected to transfer the body.
