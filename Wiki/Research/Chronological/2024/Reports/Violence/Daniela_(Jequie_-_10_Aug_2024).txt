> A transgender woman was shot dead on Saturday night (10), in Jequié, in the South Center of Bahia. According to the Civil Police, the victim is Daniela. According to the Police, teams from the Mobile Emergency Care Service went to the scene and took her to the Pedro Valadares General Hospital (HGPV), but Daniela did not survive her injuries. Units from the 19th Military Police Battalion also registered the incident. The case is being investigated by the Civil Police Territorial Division of Jequié.
> 
> A transgender woman, identified by her first name "Daniela", was shot dead on Saturday night (10), in the city of Jequié, in southwest Bahia. 



> The growing violence in Jequié resulted in yet another tragedy on Saturday night (10). Luiz Henrique, a 26-year-old trans man, died at Hospital Geral Prado Valadares (HGPV) after being shot in an attempted murder on a street in the Cidade Nova neighborhood.
> 
> According to the police, Luiz Henrique was shot in the head at around 8:00 p.m., next to a bar in the area. An ambulance from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) arrived quickly and managed to rescue him, taking him to the hospital alive. Unfortunately, despite the efforts of the medical team, Luiz Henrique did not survive his injuries.
> 
> Military Police officers were sent to the scene shortly after the incident and are searching for the suspect, who has not yet been found. The Civil Police will investigate the case to identify the perpetrator and the motive for the crime.
> 
> The violence situation in Jequié continues to worry both residents and authorities, who are struggling to contain crime rates. This tragic episode highlights the urgent need for effective measures to combat violence and protect the population.
