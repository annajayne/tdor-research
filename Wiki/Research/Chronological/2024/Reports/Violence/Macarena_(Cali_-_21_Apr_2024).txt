> Who killed Macarena? Trans woman was murdered in a motel in the central area of Cali
> The material perpetrators of the crime are being sought after having been recorded on the security cameras of the establishment and the area.
> Who killed Macarena? Trans woman was murdered in a motel in the central area of Cali
> 
> Macarena, a 28-year-old trans woman, 1.80 tall, originally from Santa Marta and who had lived in Cali for eight years, was a victim of gender violence. When she arrived in Cali she worked for four years as a webcam model, then she began working as a sex worker on the streets of downtown Cali.
> 
> When she arrived in Cali she worked for four years as a webcam model, then she began working as a sex worker on the streets of downtown Cali.
> 
>  There she made friends and for four years she remained in the area of 22nd Street and 7th Street, where there is an apartment hotel that they use for sexual encounters with her clients.
> 
> Macarena, who was wearing a miniskirt, a blouse and a black jacket last Sunday, was left lying on the floor of the parking lot of the Expression of Love residence after receiving four gunshot wounds.
> 
> As background, those who work with this LGTBIQ+ population, of which trans women are part, say that in these places in the center, criminal gangs, micro-trafficking, extortion are reappearing and these are the same criminal organizations that have intimidated the trans to not let them work, leaving them in imminent danger.
> 
> In the case of Macarena, it is presumed that the person who took her life would be a regular client who hires trans women, but that, according to them, in recent times, after providing the service, he says that they have stolen it. and forces them to return the money, often by beating them.
> 
> Allegedly, yesterday this person hired one of the trans women and when she wanted her money back he beat her, at which point Macarena intervened to defend her partner.
> 
> As far as some witnesses comment, they say that the man left, but returned with another person and they surprised Macarena with four gunshots, leaving him on the ground without any immediate attention. The police arrived, but they were closed, said one of the trans women who was desperately asking for help.
