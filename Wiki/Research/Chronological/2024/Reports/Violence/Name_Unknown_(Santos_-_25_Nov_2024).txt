> Santos / Police
> Man confesses to murder after killing transvestite with whom he had sex
> November 25, 2024
> A 42-year-old man was arrested in flagrante delicto for the murder of another man, aged 49. The case took place in Santos and according to the Public Security Secretariat of the State of São Paulo (SSP), police were called on Sunday night (24) with information that the victim had been taken by the suspects in a vehicle.
> 
> The car was found and stopped on Rua Antônio Maia, near Avenida Perimetral, around 9 pm. Although the agents did not find any illegal objects inside, there were two occupants, one of whom had traces of blood on his clothes.
> 
> The man who had traces of blood on his clothes confessed to the murder and was taken to the Central Judicial Police Station (CPJ) in Santos. The other man was released, but remains under investigation.
> 
> Another team found the body of the boy who had been kidnapped near this location. The City Hall reported that the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) was called and confirmed the death at the scene.
> 
> There is no confirmation of the motive for the crime, but according to information gathered by the Mais Santos portal, the dead man was a transvestite who had had relations with the perpetrator of the homicide. The perpetrator's wife found out about the betrayal from the transvestite.
> 
> The case was registered as a homicide at the CPJ and investigations are ongoing to fully clarify the facts.
