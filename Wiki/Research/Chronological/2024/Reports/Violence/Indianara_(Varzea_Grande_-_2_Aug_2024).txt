====== Indianara (Varzea Grande - 2 Aug 2024) ======
Created Friday 02 August 2024
@violence


Indianara [Rogério Pereira de Souza]
2 Aug 2024
Rodovia dos Imigrantes (BR-364)
Várzea Grande
Mato Grosso
Brazil
run over



> A trans woman identified only as Indianara was killed in a car accident on Friday afternoon, August 2, on the Imigrantes Highway. The truck driver who ran her over fled the scene without providing assistance. The victim was wearing only underwear at the time of the accident.
> 
> Preliminary information indicates that Indianara was banging on the truck window and was pushed by the driver, who then accelerated, running over her body.



> After a program, a trans woman is thrown from a truck and dies after being run over
> She had her head crushed by another vehicle that left the scene.
> 
> trans woman murdered
> A trans woman died after having her head crushed on kilometer 435 of BR-364, in Várzea Grande, heading towards the municipality of Jangada, after being thrown from the cabin of a truck, this Friday (2). The victim, identified by the name Indianara, was wearing only underwear. The name on the record is Rogério Pereira de Souza.
> 
> Preliminary information indicates that the woman had a sexual encounter with a truck driver and spent the night taking drugs. However, this Friday she was thrown out of the truck and was subsequently run over and killed on the highway. This information was passed on by Indianara's colleagues. 
> 
> According to Nova Rota do Oeste, the company's rescue team arrived at the scene and found the victim already dead. The vehicle that was apparently involved in the accident was not at the scene when the team arrived.
> 
> The Federal Highway Police (PRF), Civil Police and Official Expertise and Technical Identification (Politec) were called to carry out the appropriate procedures. The Civil Police are conducting investigations to obtain access to the security cameras to clarify the crime and locate the driver who ran over the victim.
