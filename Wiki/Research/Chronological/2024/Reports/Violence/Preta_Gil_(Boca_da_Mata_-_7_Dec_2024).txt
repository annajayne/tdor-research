> Trans woman suffers gunshot attack on public road in Boca da Mata
> By: JC Nicácio / AlagoasWeb  Date: 12/08/2024 at 15:13
> Victim told police that he had fired the shots
> 
> A homicide crime was registered late on Saturday night, the 7th, inPeri-Peri District, rural area of the municipality of Boca da Mata, in the interior of Alagoas. A trans woman, known as 'Preta Gil', (baptismal name Eduardo dos Santos), was the victim of a shooting attack.
> 
> Receba as notícias do AlagoasWeb no seu WhatsApp            
> According to information from the Military Police, a squad from the Integrated Public Security Center (CISP) in Boca da Mata was called by locals to respond to the incident.
> 
> At the scene, the PM found a team from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU), providing first aid to the victim, who was taken to the city hospital, but did not survive his injuries and died.
> 
> Preliminary investigation indicated that the victim was hit by five gunshots.
> 
> Before she died, however, 'Preta Gil' told the police who was responsible for the shooting, but the motive for the crime was not clarified. Given the information, the police began searching the area, but so far the suspect has not been located.
> 
> The victim's body was sent to the Estácio de Lima Legal Medical Institute (IML) in Maceió. The Civil Police are investigating the case to identify and arrest the perpetrator.
