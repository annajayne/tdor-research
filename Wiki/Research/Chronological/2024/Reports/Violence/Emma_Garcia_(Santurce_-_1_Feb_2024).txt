> They investigate the first transfeminicide of 2024 in Santurce
> The deceased, who was found lying on the pavement early Friday morning, had several gunshot wounds.
> 
> February 2, 2024 - 10:22 AM
> 
> At the scene where they were attacked, 23 9-millimeter caliber casings, one .40 caliber casing, three projectiles and two bullets were found.
> The body was found on the pavement of Aurora Street. ( david.villafane@gfrmedia.com )
> 
> The authorities are investigating whether the murder of a transgender woman recorded at dawn on Friday on Aurora Street, in Santurce, constitutes the first hate crime of 2024, confirmed Idamaris Morales Laurido , director of the Homicide Division of the Criminal Investigation Corps (CIC) of the Police, to El Nuevo Día .
> 
> “The victim's parents have already been interviewed and we are waiting for them to go to the Institute of Forensic Sciences for identification ,” said Morales Laurido, in a telephone interview, after describing the deceased as “a trans female .” ”
> 
> Although he could not immediately specify whether this is the first hate crime reported so far this year , he assured that this angle is being investigated.
> 
> “ In these cases, it is never ruled out that it was a hate crime .” That angle is always worked. We cannot say with certainty, at this time, that it is a hate crime, but we are working with that angle as well,” the lieutenant stressed.
> 
> “The body had multiple gunshot wounds and several .40 caliber bullet casings were recovered at the scene ,” he explained.
> 
> After the interview and through a press release, the Police reported that they activated the investigation protocol for violent deaths of women and trans people for the investigation of this case.
> 
> El Nuevo Día reserves the right to publish the name of the victim, since the Uniformed Agency released the one assigned to him at birth, not the one with which he identified.
> 
> In fact, previously, the Police had reported that the body of a “man” shot on Aurora Street had been found.
> 
> “The agents of the San Juan CIC Homicide Division continue to interview different people and other relatives in order to collect additional data that contributes to the identification of the victim and the criminal investigation process,” the brief statement states.
> 
> “ We urge anyone who may have knowledge about the victim or who can contribute to the investigation to contact us confidentially at (787) 343-2020 ,” the letter adds.
> 
> According to the initial report of the events, a call to the 9-1-1 Emergency System alerted the Police about a person lying on the pavement. When the agents arrived, they found the lifeless body of a person of approximately 25 years old , who had gunshot wounds in different parts of the body.
> 
> The deceased person was described as having a dark complexion, black hair, brown eyes, 5 feet 11 inches tall and weighing approximately 200 pounds. At the time of the events, he stated, he was wearing a gray shirt, long black jeans, black Adidas sports shoes, long black socks, and long black and white false nails.
