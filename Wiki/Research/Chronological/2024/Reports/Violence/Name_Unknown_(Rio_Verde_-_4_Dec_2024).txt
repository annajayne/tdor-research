> Transvestite found dead on the banks of the GO-174 in Rio Verde
> 
> A transvestite was found dead this Saturday (4) with a gunshot wound to her body on the banks of GO-174, in  Rio Verde , in the southwest of Goiás. The Civil Police will investigate the case.
> 
> According to officers who were at the scene, the transvestite did not have any personal documents. Therefore, the body is being identified by the Civil Police Identification Institute. This department confirmed to  G1  that it has come up with a possible name, but that it will be confirmed.
> 
> The on-duty delegate Luiz Eduardo Silva said that all lines of investigation are open until new investigations are carried out to find out what happened.
> 
> There is still no information as to whether the body was left in the rural area after the crime was committed or whether it was carried out on site.
> 
> Civil and military police were on the highway where the body was found to begin the investigation and preserve the crime scene for forensic work.
