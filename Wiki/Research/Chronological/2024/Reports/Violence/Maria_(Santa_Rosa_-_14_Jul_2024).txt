> MARÍA [1] IS A VICTIM OF CONTRACT KILLING ALONG WITH FOUR OTHER PEOPLE IN SANTA ROSA, EL ORO – ECUADOR.
> July 16, 2024 · from Advertiser · Bookmark the permalink . ·
> – Ecuadorian authorities remain silent regarding our communications.-
> 
> Asociación Silueta X, members of the National Trans Platform Ecuador (CoaliTrans), the GLBTI Observatory, and the National Federation of LGBT Organizations, hereby make the following fact public.
> 
> A tragic event occurred this Sunday, July 14 at 3:10 p.m., five people lost their lives in an armed attack inside a bar located on Camarsa Alley and Sexta Street, in the Reina del Cisne neighborhood of the Santa Rosa canton. Among the victims is María* (Runa Sipiy Coding SX-ORO-1624714), a 40-year-old trans woman, known as “China Mayerly.”
> 
> According to witnesses, four men arrived at the scene on two motorcycles. Two of them got out of the vehicles, entered the bar and opened fire on the people who were there, who were distracted by drinking beer, talking and listening to music.
> 
> The authorities are carrying out the relevant investigations to clarify the motives of the attack and to identify those responsible for this unfortunate incident. We ask the public, if they have any relevant information about the incident, to contact the corresponding authorities.
> 
> We express our sincere condolences to the families and friends of the victims. Violence and insecurity must be firmly combated to ensure peace and security for all citizens.
> 
> For all of the above, we call on the Attorney General of the State, Diana Salazar, the Police Commander, Carlos Cabrera, the Minister of Women and Human Rights, Arianna Tanca, and the Ombudsman of Ecuador, Cesar Córdoba Valverde, to carry out an exhaustive investigation to identify and bring to justice those responsible for this act.

> MASSACREE IN A BAR! Five people riddled with bullets in the 'business' of 'La China', Santa Rosa
> 
> Santa Rosa, El Oro.- Gabriel Alcides Dioses Escalante, 34 years old; Luis Anthony Herrera Romero, 24; Wilson Manuel Peña Cedeño, 30; Ronny Javier Chero Reina, 34; and Edison Rodrigo Zhiñin Sánchez, 40, the latter known as 'La China', are the identities of the five people riddled with bullets in an armed attack inside a bar in the Santa Rosa canton.
> 
> The incident occurred around 3:10 p.m. this Sunday, July 14, in the Reina del Cisne neighborhood, in a building located on Camarsa alley and Sexta street.
> 
> According to witnesses, four men arrived at the scene on two motorcycles, two of whom got off, entered and shot at the people in the place, while they were distracted by drinking beer, talking and listening to music.
> 
> A video posted on social media shows the scene. A man lying on the table, two on the floor, one sitting on a red chair and the last victim, in a corner.
