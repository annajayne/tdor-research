> Transgender person stabbed to death in Surat, partner arrested
> By: Express News Service Surat | Updated: November 28, 2024 02:00 IST
> 
> A 39-year-old transgender person was stabbed to death, allegedly by their partner who wanted to end the relationship, in Salabatpura area of Surat on Wednesday.
> 
> The incident took place at the rented house of the accused that he shared with his mother in Umarwada tenement, police said.
> 
> The deceased was identified as Sanjana Kunwar.
> 
> The accused, Jethwa Kishan Kumar (29), was arrested later in the day.
> 
> Police said the accused and the victim, a resident of Katargam area in Surat, had been in a relationship for over a decade. Kumar often used to stay at Kunwar’s house and vice versa.
> 
> Police said Kunwar, who earned by panhandling and performing at auspicious events, used to financially support Kumar who was jobless. Police said that for the last few months, the accused had been wanting to end the relationship and was avoiding meeting Kunwar.
> 
> When Kunwar could not reach him on phone for the last few days, they went to Kumar’s house on Tuesday and stayed with him, police added.
> 
> On Wednesday morning, when Kumar’s mother went out to run an errand, a quarrel ensued between him and Kunwar following which he repeatedly stabbed his partner before leaving the house, police said.
> 
> Incharge police inspector of Salabatpura police station S N Desai said, “Kumar was staying with his mother in the one-room-kitchen house in Umarwada tenement. Her mother was also aware about her son’s relationship with Kunwar…The incident came to light when Kumar’s mother returned home to find Kunwar lying on the bed in a pool of blood. She alerted her neighbours and then the police about the incident. We arrested Kumar late on Wednesday afternoon.”
