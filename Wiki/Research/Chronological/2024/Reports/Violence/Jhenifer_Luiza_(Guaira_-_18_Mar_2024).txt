> Two men were arrested on suspicion of involvement in the death of a 26-year-old transgender woman, found wrapped in a mattress in a vacant lot in Guaíra (SP) , this Monday morning (18).
> 
> According to the delegate responsible for investigating the case, Rafael Faria Domingos, Jhenifer Luiza's body would have been abandoned at the scene last Saturday (16).
> 
> In the early afternoon of this Monday, after the young woman's body was located by a resident in the Eldorado neighborhood, images from security cameras helped identify one of the suspects.
> 
> Searches were carried out and the 22-year-old man, from the state of Pernambuco, was found. To the police, the man confessed to having participated in hiding the victim's body, but denied participation in the homicide.
> 
> A second suspect, aged 24, was identified by the man and then located by police. He denied any involvement in the crime.
> 
> Open arrest warrants were identified against the two suspects, one for the crime of robbery and the other for the crime of drug trafficking, respectively.
> 
> Following a request from the Civil Police, the Court ordered the temporary arrest of the two suspects.
> 
> The causes of Jhenifer's death are investigated.



> A transgender woman was found dead, this Monday morning (18), in a vacant lot in the Eldorado neighborhood, in Guaíra (SP) .
> 
> The body of the victim, identified as Jhenifer Luiza, 26 years old, was located by a resident. According to the young woman's sister, Thaís Gândara, Jhenifer was wrapped in a mattress located amidst the vegetation on the ground.
> 
> The young woman's family and friends had filed a missing person report on Thursday (14), the last time she was seen alive.
> 
> Recognition of the body was carried out by family members still at the scene. The land underwent forensic examination and the victim's body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML).


> The body of a missing 26-year-old transgender woman was found inside a plastic bag in a vacant lot, this Monday (18), in Guaíra. The body of Jhenifer Luíza, who had disappeared on Thursday night (14), was found by a neighbor inside a big bag, used to transport products and large volumes. The case will be investigated by the Civil Police.
