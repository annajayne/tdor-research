> A trans woman, named Samira, aged 36, was stabbed to death in the early hours of this Wednesday (17), on BR-110, near the Pombalzinho neighborhood, in the Bahian municipality of Ribeira do Pombal.
> 
> According to the Military Police Operations Command (COPPM), military police officers were called to investigate information about the body of a person lying on the ground. When the police arrived at the scene, they discovered the incident.
> 
> Samu's team provided the first assistance, but Samira did not resist. She had several injuries on her body.




> A trans woman identified as Tiago Vieira Santos, 36 years old, was stabbed to death in the early hours of Wednesday (17), in the city of Ribeira do Pombal, in the interior of Bahia.
> 
> According to information gathered by Portal Massa!, the crime occurred on the banks of BR-110, in the Pombalzinho neighborhood. First, the Emergency Medical Care Service (SAMU) was called with information that Samira, as the victim was known, had been run over.
> 
> However, when they arrived at the scene, the health team found that she had died and that there were marks of aggression with a sharp weapon (knife) on her body and the Military Police were called.
> 
> The body was removed by the Technical Police Department (DPT) of Euclides da Cunha. The Civil Police are investigating the authorship and motivation of the crime.
