> Who is Shane Curry? Man Arrested for Murder of 17-year-old Girlfriend Naveah Goddard
> Shane Curry is a 20-year-old from Stow, Massachusetts who has been arrested on suspicion of murdering his 17-year-old girlfriend.
> 
> Stow Police were called to a home on Great Road for a well-being check around 4:30 p.m. Friday to conduct a wellbeing check.
> 
> According to the Middlesex County District Attorney’s office, once officers got to the home, Curry stopped them from coming inside for nearly two hours.
> 
> Eventually, police managed to gain access and found the body of Naveah Goddard, 17, who was the girlfriend of Shane Curry.
> 
> Authorities considered the death of Goddard as suspicious and are investigating it as such.
> 
> Curry has been charged with assault and battery on a household or family member and assault and battery causing serious bodily injury.
> 
> He is expected to be arraigned on Monday in Concord District Court.
> 
> Meanwhile, the Middlesex County District Attorney’s office and Stowe police are still actively investigating Goddard’s death.
> 
> The Chief Medical Examiner’s Office is also set to conduct an autopsy on the body of Naveah Goddard to determine her cause of death.



> Tragedy struck in Stow as police launched a suspicious death investigation following grim discoveries at a local residence. Middlesex District Attorney Marian Ryan and Stow Chief of Police Michael Sallese confirmed the probe after a young woman was found dead Thursday evening.
> 
> Authorities arrived at a Great Road home around 4:30 p.m. for a well-being check, where they encountered 20-year-old Shane Curry. Despite initial resistance from Curry, who spent roughly two hours barring the officers' entry, law enforcement eventually entered to find 17-year-old Neveah Goddard, Curry's girlfriend, dead inside the home she shared with the suspect.
> 
> Curry was apprehended and rushed to a nearby hospital for evaluation. Subsequent charges against him include assault and battery on a household or family member, and assault and battery causing serious bodily injury, as outlined by official statements. Curry is slated for arraignment in Concord District Court this Monday, April 8th.
> 
> The precise circumstances of Goddard's death remain shrouded in mystery pending an autopsy by the Office of the Chief Medical Examiner. Depending on the outcome, additional charges against Curry are a distinct possibility. The small community of Stow waits with bated breath for answers as this tragic story unfolds, their peace shattered by a young woman's untimely demise.
