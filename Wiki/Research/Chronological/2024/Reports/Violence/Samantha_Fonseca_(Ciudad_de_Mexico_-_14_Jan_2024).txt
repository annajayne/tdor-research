> A trans activist is killed aboard a taxi by application in Xochimilco; They shot her 14 times
> The first investigations indicate that the crime occurred after Samanta Fonseca left the Reclusorio Sur, where she held a meeting. For those in charge of the investigations, the murderers were already waiting for Samanta to kill her.
> 
> The first police reports indicate that the crime occurred this Sunday afternoon minutes after 6:00 pm in the surroundings of the Parish of Santiago Apóstol Tepalcatlalpan, on Del Trabajo Street on its corner with Cuauhtémoc in the Xochimilco Mayor's Office.
> 
> The peace and calm of the colony's inhabitants and merchants was interrupted when a series of explosions were heard.
> 
> And, according to the first reports, a woman of approximately 37 years of age requested Didi, who arrived at the point and the young woman climbed into the back of the unit.
> 
> Woman ordered an app taxi and ended up in 'the afterlife' after a direct attack
> Photo: Jaime Llera | ThePress
> It was then that armed criminals approached the oxford gray Nissan Versa and without saying a word opened fire on it.
> 
> One after another, the shots went through the metal and the rear window of the car, hitting the victim's body, who was seriously injured.
> 
> The owner of the taxi by application and residents of the area requested support through 911, so to the point there was a strong mobilization of elements of the Secretary of Citizen Security SSC, who after observing the shot unit and the bloodied woman requested medical support.
> 
> An ambulance assigned to the emergency arrived at the point to provide pre-hospital services but after a quick check they could only confirm that he no longer had vital signs since the multiple injuries caused by the bullets had already ended his life.
> 
> Relatives of the deceased arrived at the cordoned-off site, screaming and crying inconsolably over the death of their loved one.
> 
> They remained at the scene for at least four hours until the arrival of Forensic Investigation and Expert Services personnel who, after completing the procedures, removed the body to transfer it to the corresponding territorial coordination where the investigations would continue to locate the murderers.

> Ciudad de México.- Trans activist Samantha Gomes Fonseca was murdered near the Reclusorio Sur, in the Xochimilco mayor's office, in Mexico City.
> 
> According to the first report, Morena's former Senate candidate had received death threats.
> 
> It was Morena's deputy in the CDMX Congress, Temístocles Villanueva, who announced that activist Samantha Fonseca was murdered this Sunday afternoon.
> 
> "In a cowardly way, the life of our colleague and friend was taken (...) a brilliant woman with a long history in the defense of Human Rights and against discrimination," he said.
> 
> Samantha Gomes Fonseca was decorated by the Mexico City Congress last October, when she received a medal of merit for being a defender of Human Rights in 2022, along with other activists such as Aranxa Bello Brindis and Miguel Concha Bad.
