====== Name Unknown (Tlaquepaque - 13 May 2024) ======
Created Tuesday 14 May 2024

Name Unknown

Avenida Colón & Torres Bodet
El Sauz
Tlaquepaque
Jalisco
Mexico
stabbed




> A trans woman lost her life after being attacked with a knife on Avenida Colón and Torres Bodet in the El Sauz neighborhood, on the borders of Guadalajara and San Pedro Tlaquepaque, where she remained  unconscious on the ground.
> 
> When paramedics from the Leonardo Oliva Green Cross in Guadalajara arrived, they located the trans woman, approximately 30 to 35 years old, who had two stab wounds in the chest, which caused her death.
> 
> It should be noted that 30 minutes passed without any authority coming to the site, neither from the Guadalajara Police Station nor from Tlaquepaque, since they said it belonged to the other department respectively.
> 
> Finally, the Forensic Medical Service went to the scene to transport the victim to the metropolitan morgue, while the San Pedro Tlaquepaque public security police station acted as first responder.
> 
> This case is investigated under the established protocols carried out by the Specialized Human Rights Prosecutor's Office (FEDH) of the State Prosecutor's Office (FE) to apprehend whoever is responsible for the death.
> 
> 
> The Jalisco State Prosecutor's Office announced that they have initiated an investigation file for the murder of a trans woman that was reported on the morning of May 13 in the municipality of Tlaquepaque.
> 
> The victim appeared to be between 35 and 40 years old; at the time of the discovery in the El Sauz neighborhood, his identity had not been revealed.
> 
> Around 5:30 in the morning, reports began to arrive to the 911 emergency number, indicating that there was a person lying on the public road , this on Avenida Colón, near the intersection with Jaime Torres Bodet.
> 
> The first to arrive were the paramedics. 
> 
> Among some planters, on the sidewalk, the Municipal Medical Services personnel found the lifeless body, which had injuries allegedly caused by a knife.
> 
> Moments later, the municipal police arrived; they were in charge of cordoning off the site of the discovery and requesting the presence of the State Prosecutor's Office.
> 
> To date, no witnesses to this attack or the person who perpetrated it have been located.
