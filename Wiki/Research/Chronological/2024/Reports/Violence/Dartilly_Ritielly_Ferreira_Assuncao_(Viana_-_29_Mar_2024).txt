> The Civil Police of Maranhão arrested, on Thursday (27), a municipal guard suspected of murdering the transgender woman Dartilly Ritielly Ferreira Assunção, in Viana, a city located 117 km from São Luís.
> 
> The crime took place on March 29, 2024. Initially, authorities treated the case as a suicide. However, after investigations conducted by the 6th Regional Police Station of Viana, witness statements and the reproduction of the crime scene indicated that it was, in fact, a homicide.
> 
> The main suspect, a municipal guard whose identity has not been revealed, had a preventive arrest warrant issued against him. The motive for the crime has not yet been disclosed. The arrest took place inside the Viana City Council, where the suspect worked. After giving a statement at the police station, he was sent to a prison unit.
> 
> According to the Brazilian Penal Code, the crime of homicide (article 121) carries a sentence of six to 20 years in prison. If qualifying factors are identified, such as a vile motive, cruel means or the victim's inability to defend himself, the sentence can reach 30 years in prison.
> 
> Furthermore, murders motivated by transphobia can be classified as hate crimes and treated within the Racism Law (Law 7.716/1989), according to a decision by the Federal Supreme Court (STF) that equated crimes of LGBTphobia to the crime of racism. In these cases, the sentence can vary from one to five years in prison, and may be aggravated depending on the circumstances.
> 
> The case remains under investigation to clarify the motive for the crime and gather more evidence for the inquiry.

> By: Editorial Team* February 28, 2025
> On the night of this Thursday (27), the Civil Police of Maranhão arrested a man identified by investigations as being the main suspect of committing a homicide crime that claimed the life of the woman Dartilly Ritielly Ferreira Assunção, which occurred in the municipality of Viana, in the Baixada Maranhense region.
> 
> According to the 6th Regional Police Station of Viana, the crime would have occurred on March 29, 2024. After an investigation with several testimonies, simulated reproduction of the crime scene and reports, the Civil Police concluded that Dartilly Ritielly's case was not a suicide, but a homicide.
> 
> The police have requested the arrest of the main suspect of committing the crime. This Thursday, the civil police officers managed to serve the arrest warrant against the man, who is a municipal guard in Viana. The arrest took place inside the City Hall, the place where the suspect works.
> 
> After legal proceedings at the police station, the suspect was taken to a prison unit in the region.
