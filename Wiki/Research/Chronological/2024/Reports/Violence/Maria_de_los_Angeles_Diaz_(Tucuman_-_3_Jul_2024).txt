> The body of a trans woman is found in Villa Muñecas and her 62-year-old partner is arrested
> Thursday, July 04, 2024, 08:46
> 
> Neighbors found the body in a ditch and alerted the police.
> 
> A 47-year-old trans woman was found dead last night in the back of a house located at 2200 Padre Roque Correa passage in Villa Muñecas III.
> 
> This Wednesday, just after 8 p.m., 911 was notified that a person was dead.
> 
> According to the report issued by the officers, they found the body of María de los Ángeles Díaz, a 47-year-old trans woman, in a well. Meanwhile, the main suspect, Osvaldo Martínez, 62, was arrested. Neighbors said that the couple had constant conflicts between them.
> 
> Homicide officers who worked at the scene confirmed that no signs of violence or blood stains were visible.
> 
> According to experts, the body was found around 8 p.m. in a well by a neighbor who alerted 911 personnel. Two people were arrested and the autopsy report is awaited to determine the cause of death.
> 
> During the early hours of Thursday, ECIF personnel continued with the expert tasks. According to initial investigations, the house where the victim and her partner (a 62-year-old male) lived was lent to them by a neighbor because they were both homeless.
