> Violence in Bagé: Trans woman is dismembered in Bagé; teenage boyfriend was arrested.
> By admin-08/31/202401170
> 
> Jullyane Fernandes, 20 years old, was the name given to the victim of the barbaric crime that occurred this Friday, the 30th, in Bagé. She was killed by her boyfriend, a 16-year-old teenager, on the night of Thursday, the 29th, in the Tiaraju neighborhood, and her body was dismembered and her parts thrown in various places. She was a transsexual and was from Dom Pedrito.
> 
> The accused, after being apprehended, confessed to the crime and was unable to identify the victim, as she had only introduced herself using her social name. The investigation department of the Specialized Police Station for Women (Deam) was able to identify the victim, a transgender woman from Dom Pedrito, through information from social media. The confirmation was made with the help of agents from the Dom Pedrito Police Station.
> 
> The investigations were coordinated by Police Chief Carolina Funchal Terres. The juvenile offender was sent to the Bagé Emergency Care Unit (DPPA) for legal procedures and presentation to the Public Prosecutor's Office and the Judiciary.
> 
> Deam agents received information that the 16-year-old had killed his girlfriend on the night of August 29. In response and reinforced by civil police officers from the Police Department for the Repression of Organized Criminal Actions (Draco), they apprehended the minor at the home of relatives, in the Ibagé neighborhood.
> 
> The teenager reportedly confessed to the crime and, as a result, investigations were carried out in the Tiaraju neighborhood: the body was found in two locations in that neighborhood. The torso was found in the courtyard of a residence and the upper and lower limbs were found in a suitcase, on the side of General Malet Avenue.





> Trans woman is dismembered by her boyfriend in Bagé
> A 16-year-old teenager was arrested after admitting to killing and dismembering his girlfriend Jullyane Fernandes, a 20-year-old transgender woman, on the night of last Thursday (29)
> By Kathrein Silva Published 09/01/2024
> 
> A 16-year-old teenager was arrested after admitting to having killed and dismembered his girlfriend Jullyane Fernandes, a 20-year-old transgender woman, on the night of last Thursday (29), in Bagé. The Specialized Police Station for Women's Assistance in the city is investigating a femicide discovered this Friday (30), in a residence in the Tiaraju neighborhood.
> 
> According to the police, the teenager was arrested at a relative's house and admitted to having killed his wife. The suspect allegedly dismembered her using an axe and kitchen knives. Preliminary investigations indicate that the crime occurred due to a disagreement motivated by jealousy.
> 
> The victim's body was found in two parts of the neighborhood, one part in the courtyard of a residence and the other part in a suitcase found on the side of Avenida General Malet.
> 
> Police are seeking to confirm the identity of the victim, who reportedly introduced herself to the man by her social name. The woman reportedly resides in Dom Pedrito.
> 
> The teenager was sent for procedures at the Bagé Police Station and will remain at the disposal of the Public Prosecutor's Office and the Judiciary.
