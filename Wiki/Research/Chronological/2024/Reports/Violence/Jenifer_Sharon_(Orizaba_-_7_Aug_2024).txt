> A transgender woman  with the initials JSRF and/or SRF was the victim of a gun attack on Wednesday afternoon in the magical town of Orizaba , Veracruz.


> A trans woman was shot dead as she got off a passenger bus on the streets of the city of Orizaba, Veracruz.
> 
> According to initial reports, the incident occurred at kilometer 275 near a gas station in the municipality.
> 
> Reports indicate that the woman was ready to get off the bus when a man was allegedly waiting for her at the bus stop.
> 
> The subject then began shooting at him without saying a word and then fled in an unknown direction.
> 
> After the gunshots, the victim's body fell to the pavement and he eventually lost his life.
> 
> Members of the National Guard arrived at the scene and cordoned off the area due to the incident.
> 
> While a criminal expert removed the body and transferred it to the Forensic Medical Service to perform the corresponding autopsy.
> 
> The motive for the murder and the whereabouts of the alleged perpetrator remain unknown.
