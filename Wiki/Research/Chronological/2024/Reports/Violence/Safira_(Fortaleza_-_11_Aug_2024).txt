> A 39-year-old transvestite , known as Safira, reported missing on Saturday, the 10th, had her death  confirmed this Tuesday, the 13th. The victim was  killed on Sunday, the 11th, in the Conjunto Palmeiras neighborhood , in Fortaleza , with blows from sticks and stones .Although the body was collected on the same day as the crime, it was only on Tuesday that his family members identified it at the Legal Medicine Coordination (Comel).
> 
> They even reported the disappearance to the 12th Precinct of the Homicide and Personal Protection Department (DHPP), a unit specialized in incidents of this kind.
> 
> At the time of publication of this article, it was not known what motivated the crime and no suspects had been arrested. The 3rd DHPP is investigating the case, stated the Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS).People who knew Safira said she had no involvement in crime and "did not even have a criminal record." She was known for asking people in the neighborhood for money.
