> Trans man shot dead in metropolitan area
> A 38-year-old transgender man was shot dead on Rua Itamar de Matos Maia, in the Niterói neighborhood , in Canoas, last weekend.
> 
> At the time of the crime, committed last Saturday (7), the victim, whose name was not released, was receiving a package in front of his house. The shooter arrived on a motorcycle and shot the man, who did not survive his injuries and died at the scene.
> 
> Later that day, the Civil Police arrested an individual suspected of having committed the crime.
> 
> According to the delegate of the Homicide and Personal Protection Department (DHPP) of Canoas, Graziela Zinelli , preliminary investigations rule out gender motivation. The investigations remain confidential to determine the circumstances of the crime.




> Criminal arrested after shooting trans man to death in Canoas
> Police arrested the man accused of killing a trans man in Canoas; See more details below
> 
> 09/12/2024 - 16:36
> Updated on 12/09/2024 - 5:48 PM
> canoes
> (Photo: GBC Agency)
> Police arrested a 28-year-old man on Saturday night (7) accused of shooting a trans man to death in Canoas. The crime took place on Rua Itamar de Matos Maia in the Niterói neighborhood.
> 
> According to investigations, the 38-year-old victim was at home when he went out to receive a package delivered by a ride-hailing vehicle. At that moment, the suspect arrived on a motorcycle and opened fire.
> 
> The victim did not survive his injuries and died at the scene.
> 
> The Homicide and Personal Protection Department (DHPP) in Canoas confirmed that the suspect had no criminal record. In addition, the police reported that the victim also had no criminal record.
> 
> The incident also had the support of teams from the Military Brigade, the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) and the General Institute of Expertise.
