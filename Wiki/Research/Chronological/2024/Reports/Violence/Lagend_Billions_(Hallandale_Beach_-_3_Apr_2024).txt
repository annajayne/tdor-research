> Transgender man who died days after being shot in The Village at Gulfstream Park identified
> Hallandale Beach (WPLG)
> HALLANDALE BEACH, Fla. – Hallandale Police have confirmed that a transgender man who was shot last week at The Village at Gulfstream Park has died.
> 
> According to authorities, officers responded to the 800 block of Silks Run near Strike 10 Bowling after receiving reports of a shooting around 1:30 a.m. on April 3.
> 
> Hallandale Beach police said a victim, whom they identified as 36-year-old Latoya Arnold, was transported to HCA Florida Aventura Hospital, where she was later pronounced dead last Sunday.
> 
> Smith told Local 10 News reporter Rosh Lowe that he believes the shooting is considered an “isolated incident” and is not related to a hate crime.
> 
> “We do not believe this was a hate crime,” Smith said. “When we have incidents like this, we go above and beyond and do everything we can to keep our community safe.”
> 
> Police said they are now searching for a female suspect and said the suspect and victim know each other.
> 
> Anyone with information is urged to call Broward Crime Stoppers at 954-493-8477.
> 
> Police did not immediately release further details.




> Transgender man critically injured in shooting at Gulfstream Park in Hallandale Beach
> One victim was transported to Aventura Hospital in critical condition, according to police
> By Lena Salzbank • Published April 3, 2024 • Updated 4 hours ago
> 
> Police are investigating a shooting at the Gulfstream Park shopping center in Hallandale Beach that left a transgender man critically injured. NBC6’s Jamie Guirola reports
> 
> Hallandale Beach Police officials said officers responded around 1:30 a.m. to the 800 block of Silks Run, near Strike 10 Bowling, regarding a shooting.
> 
> The victim, 37 years old, was found and transported to Aventura Hospital in critical condition, according to police. His identity hasn't been released.
> 
> The shooting was an isolated incident. Police said the cause is under investigation but do not believe it was a hate crime.
> 
> Video showed multiple police vehicles including a crime scene unit van at Gulfstream Park, with one area closed off by yellow crime scene tape.
> 
> A tow truck was also seen taking away two cars from the shopping center.
