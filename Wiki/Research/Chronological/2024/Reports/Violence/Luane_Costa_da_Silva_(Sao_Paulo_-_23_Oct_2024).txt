> Luane Costa da Silva, a 27-year-old trans woman from Santo Antônio de Jesus , was found dead in a motel in Santos, São Paulo, last Wednesday (23). The police are investigating the case as femicide, with one of the main lines of investigation indicating that Luane may have been strangled by two men.
> 
> Crime Suspects
> One of the suspects is a man who claims to be a pastor. He is accused of luring Luane to the city with false promises of employment and financial improvements. The man was arrested when he returned to his room to retrieve his cell phone.
> 
> Luane's Life
> According to information from Blog do Valente, Luane worked as an escort and frequently traveled to other states for work. Recently, she was persuaded by the fake pastor to travel to São Paulo, believing that her financial situation would improve. At the time, Luane was accompanied by her partner and had plans to move to São Paulo with a cousin, both of whom live in the São Benedito neighborhood in Santo Antônio de Jesus.
> 
> Ongoing Investigation
> The alleged pastor's involvement became a central focus of the investigation after he left his cell phone in the room where Luane's body was found. Luane's body was sent to the Forensic Medical Institute (IML) and is expected to be transferred to Santo Antônio de Jesus this Thursday (24). So far, there is no information about the wake and burial.

> Luane Costa Silva, 27, originally from Santo Antônio de Jesus, was found dead in São Paulo this Wednesday (23). She is a trans woman and was in the capital of São Paulo after working during the municipal elections in SAJ. The cause of death has not yet been released and should be clarified in the coming days. Luane's body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) and will be transferred to Santo Antônio de Jesus. The information is that Luane lived in the São Benedito neighborhood. The wake has not yet been announced. On social media, many friends mourned her death.
