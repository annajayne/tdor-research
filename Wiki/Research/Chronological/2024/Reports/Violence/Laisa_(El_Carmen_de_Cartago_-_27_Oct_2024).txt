> By Alejandra Morales
> October 27, 2024, 10:32 am
> A person, apparently transgender, was killed with multiple stab wounds early Sunday morning in the Fecosa neighborhood in El Carmen de Cartago.
> 
> The judicial authorities received the alert at 1 am, however the Red Cross received the warning from 12:30 am
> 
> A 56-year-old man with the surname Camacho was killed with multiple stab wounds. He was apparently a transgender man known as Laisa. The attack occurred in the early hours of Sunday, October 27, 2024 in the Fecosa neighborhood in El Carmen de Cartago. 
> 
> When rescuers arrived, they found the victim with cuts to his abdomen and legs and declared him dead at the scene.
> 
> "The body of a deceased man has been reported found in the area, apparently with several stab wounds," the OIJ said.
> 
> Authorities identified the victim as Camacho, 56 years old.
> 
> Locals said that the person was transgender and affectionately known as Laisa, but this version has not been confirmed by the courts.
> 
> The causes of this savage attack are not clear, and no one is currently responsible.
> 
> Cartago is the second province with the fewest homicides, with 57 violent deaths recorded so far this year.
