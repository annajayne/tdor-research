> Body found with disfigured face in Teresina is that of a trans woman
> Information was confirmed to TV Lupa1 by the 9th Military Police Battalion (9th BPM).
> July 22, 2024 at 12:57 pm
>
> The body of hairdresser Aryadina Montenegro, 23, was found on Monday morning (22), on Alfa Street, São Joaquim neighborhood, in the North Zone of Teresina. The information was confirmed to TV Lupa1 by the 9th Battalion of the Military Police (9th BPM).
> 
> Major Tales told TV Lupa1 that the body was found around 6:48 in the morning in a lake area, located behind the victim's residence. The woman's face was bloody and completely disfigured.
> 
> According to information gathered by TV Lupa1 , Aryadina Montenegro had not changed her name and, as a result, an identity document with the name Francisco Thalysson Almeida de Carvalho was found next to her body.
> 
> So far there is no information about how the crime that resulted in the victim's death occurred. The case will be investigated by the Homicide and Personal Protection Department (DHPP).
> 
> The Legal Medical Institute (IML) was called to remove the body.



> Trans woman found dead in her backyard in Teresina
> Hairdresser Aryadina Montenegro, 23, had bruises and facial injuries
> 07/22/2024 2:53 PM - Updated at 3:00 PM
> 
> On Monday morning (22), hairdresser Aryadina Montenegro, 23, was found dead in the backyard of a residence in the São Joaquim neighborhood, in the North of Teresina. Her body had several bruises on the face, with injuries that suggest they were caused by stones. The suspicion is that Aryadina, a trans woman, was a victim of transphobia.
> 
> The 9th Battalion of the Military Police of Piauí (BPM) was called to respond to the incident. Police vehicles were sent to the scene, where the police reported that the body was found near one of the lakes located at the back of the residence.
> 
> Hours before her death, Aryadina shared stories on her Instagram account, showing that she was drinking and having fun with friends. So far, no suspects have been arrested. The body was collected by the Forensic Medical Institute, and the case is being investigated by the State Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP).
>
