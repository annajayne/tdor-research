> The Civil Police are investigating the case of a 27-year-old woman murdered with blows to the head in the Jardim Colonial neighborhood , in Ribeirão das Neves , in Greater BH. The crime occurred in the early hours of Tuesday (23).
> 
> The victim is Marcinha Ribeiro, born in the state of Pernambuco. She was taken to Risoleta Neves Hospital, but did not survive her injuries and died on Wednesday afternoon (24) .
> 
> According to records made by the Military Police , Marcinha was at home when a man hit her in the head. The doctor who treated the woman was unable to identify whether the injuries were caused by a firearm or another object.
> 
> Witnesses reported that the young woman was a sex worker. When the police arrived at the scene, the soldiers tried to talk to the victim, but he had difficulty articulating words .
> 
> The Civil Police reported that a man was taken to the police station and released after being interviewed by investigators.
> 
> For LGBTQIA+ NGO, victim did not have her social name respected
> The Center for the Fight for Free Sexual Orientation of Minas Gerais ( Cellos-MG ) lamented the death of the young woman, who was a trans woman , and said that it had contacted the Human Rights Commission of the Legislative Assembly of Minas Gerais (ALMG) and the Public Ministry .
> 
> According to the entity, Marcinha was identified by the police based on her biological sex , which was no longer identified. Furthermore, Cellos states that the hospital did not notify the State that the death was a case of violence against women, nor did it present the victim as female .
> 
> By note, the State Secretariat of Justice and Public Security (Sejusp-MG) explained that in the police record there is, indeed, the possibility of filling in the sexual orientation and gender identity of those involved, however, as it is a self-declaration field , would only be possible at the request of the victim.
> 
> By note, Risoleta Neves stated that the woman arrived at the scene unconscious and was soon intubated , and that it was not possible to be aware of the victim's social name. However, the report showed that Marcinha's document did indeed contain her social name.
> 
> Regarding this, the hospital said that, when investigating the patient's admission process, it was identified that the data in the system was provided by a companion who came along in the SAMU ambulance. "She signed as responsible for the patient and at no point did she hand over her identity card or mention her social name," said the institution.
> 
> "We also emphasize that the patient received all due assistance and, faced with an extremely serious clinical condition, she unfortunately died. As it was an unnatural death, the body was sent to the IML, responsible for analyzing the cause of death", he added.



> A 27-year-old transvestite died this Thursday (25) at the Risoleta Neves Hospital, after being admitted due to an attack she suffered in the early hours of Tuesday (23), in the Jardim Colonial neighborhood, in Ribeirão das Neves, in the region Belo Horizonte Metropolitan. 
> 
> The information was released by the Center for the Fight for Free Sexual Orientation of Minas Gerais (Cellos), which lamented the woman's death and stated that she was a victim of LBGT+ structural and institutional phobia at the hospital. 
> 
> According to Cellos, the hospital did not identify the victim with her social name but with her “name and biological sex”. Furthermore, the medical center did not notify the State that the death was a case of violence against women, nor did it present the victim as female.
> 
> In a note sent to Hoje em Dia, Risoleta Neves reported that, when investigating the patient's admission process to the hospital, it was identified that the data contained in the health unit's system was provided by a companion who came along in the Samu ambulance.
> 
> "She signed as responsible for the patient and at no point did she hand over her identity card nor did she mention her social name", highlighted the note.
> 
> Risoleta Neves reinforced "that there is an institutional guideline" for the social name to be adopted "whenever it is communicated to the team".
> 
> "Respect for patients' rights and the humanization of care are fundamental values ​​for our Institution. We also emphasize that the patient received all due assistance and, faced with an extremely serious clinical condition, she unfortunately died. to deal with an unnatural death, the body was sent to the IML, responsible for analyzing the cause of death", concluded the note.
> 
> The Civil Police of Minas Gerais highlighted that the fields relating to the sexual orientation and gender identity of those involved in incidents are only completed through self-declaration. In other words, they depend on the opinion of the person involved - and are not mandatory fields.
> 
> Furthermore, the inclusion of this data in the Police Report is the result of the work of the State Secretariat for Social Development (Sedese), in partnership with the State Secretariat for Justice and Public Security (Sejusp) and Prodemge, which developed the LGBTQIA+ Panel at the Integrated Public Security Base, launched in 2023.
> 
> According to the Military Police, the victim was taken to Risoleta Neves Hospital with a wound on the forehead. After examinations, doctors confirmed that the injury was caused by a gunshot. 
> 
> The victim was found by the military in a lot. The place has several houses and a 20-year-old suspect was found with a knife. 
> 
> The man reported that he was with the victim at home when a third person arrived looking for him. The woman allegedly defended him and was shot twice. 
> 
> The suspect's version was refuted by other witnesses, who claimed not to have heard any gunshots. The man was taken to the police station. 





> The transvestite who was shot in the head while protecting a friend in the Jardim Colonial neighborhood, in Ribeirão das Neves, Greater BH, died in the early hours of this Thursday (25). The information was confirmed by Risoleta Neves Hospital, where she was admitted.
> 
> On the day of the crime , the victim was with a young man, aged 20, at home, when a man broke into the place looking for this young man. The author charged a debt of R$300 and wanted to kill the young man.
> 
> The man was armed and, when he fired, the transvestite, identified only as Márcia, tried to stop him and ended up being shot in the head. She was taken unconscious to Risoleta Neves Hospital.




> Transvestite is shot after protecting friend in Belo Horizonte MG
> Police Page — 04/24/2024 - 11:30 By: POLICIAL BH
> 
> A 27-year-old transvestite was shot while trying to protect a friend in the Jardim Colonial neighborhood, in Ribeirão das Neves, Greater BH. The crime took place last Monday night (22).
> 
> According to the Military Police, on the day of the incident, a man invaded a plot of land looking for a 20-year-old young man to collect a debt of R$300. The transvestite was with the young man when the suspect arrived.
> 
> The armed man intended to kill the 20-year-old. To stop it, the transvestite stepped forward and ended up being shot in the head.
> 
> The transvestite was taken to a hospital in Ribeirão das Neves, where she remains hospitalized in a delicate condition. The 20-year-old man, who tried to escape with a knife in his hands, was taken to the police station to provide clarification.
> 
> The shooter has not yet been located and police continue to investigate.
