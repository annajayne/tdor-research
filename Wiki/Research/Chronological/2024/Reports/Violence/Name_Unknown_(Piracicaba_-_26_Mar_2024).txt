> Man confesses to killing transsexual found dead in stream less than two hours after leaving bakery in Piracicaba
> March 22, 2024
>
> As PIRANOT reported yesterday (21), a transgender woman was found dead in a stream in the São Matheus neighborhood, in the Santa Terezinha district, in Piracicaba. According to initial information from the police at the scene, the body was located around 8am, less than two hours after the victim left a bakery in the neighborhood. Images from the establishment's security cameras captured the last record of her making a purchase there at around 6:20 am.
> 
> The man responsible for the crime confessed and was arrested in the act by the Civil Police for qualified homicide. He alleged a disagreement with the victim, which resulted in strangulation and death. Then the body was thrown into the stream. The motivations for the murder are being investigated.
> 
> According to the PM, the accused was in a forest close to where the victim's body was found.
> 
> At first, the first information showed that the victim was a man and the place where the body was found was a lake. Later, the police identified that the victim was a transsexual woman named Patrícia and the body was in a stream.
> 
> The victim, who was 26 years old, was buried at 1pm this Friday (22), at the Vila Rezende Municipal Cemetery.




> Body of murdered trans woman is found in a stream in SP
> The suspect was arrested red-handed in Piracicaba, in the interior of São Paulo
> 
> 
> Body of murdered trans woman is found in a stream in SP / Disclosure/State Government
> 
> A trans woman was found dead in a stream in the São Matheus neighborhood, in Piracicaba, in the interior of São Paulo. The body was found this Thursday (21), after an anonymous report, by the Military Police (PM).
> 
> The crime suspect was found in a forest close to where the body was, and was arrested after confessing to a disagreement with the woman, according to the PM.
> 
> “[...] The police officers came across the man, [...] who raised suspicions due to his actions during the approach. [...] he showed signs of having been in the polluted water of the stream, he provided disconnected versions of what happened. However, after being taken to the Emergency Room, he confessed to the team that he had had a disagreement with the victim, that they got into a physical fight, but he had no idea that the victim would have died,” says an excerpt from the PM's record.
> 
> The suspect will be sent to the State Department of Criminal Investigations ( Deic ) in Piracicaba. He will be charged with qualified homicide, according to information from the Civil Police.
