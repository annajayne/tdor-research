> Trans woman dies
> Sep 6, 2024 | Local

> Her Partner Took Her to Cruz Verde, Where They Reported Her Death
> 
> By Jorge Martinez
> 
> The Jalisco State Prosecutor's Office is investigating, under the femicide protocol, the death of a trans woman who arrived seriously injured and walking to the Cruz Verde Las Águilas in Zapopan.
> 
> According to official reports, at around 1 a.m., a transsexual woman arrived at the first aid station in a flatbed car. The victim was traveling with a man who identified himself as her partner.
> 
> Authorities did not provide further details as to the circumstances of the death, or whether the person had any injuries or bruises.
> 
> Her death was confirmed by municipal authorities, who said that the case is being investigated by the State Prosecutor's Office and that no arrests have been reported so far. The deceased person is around 35 years old.
> 
> Her body was placed under the protection of the Forensic Medical Service and the cause of his death will be determined in the next few hours.
