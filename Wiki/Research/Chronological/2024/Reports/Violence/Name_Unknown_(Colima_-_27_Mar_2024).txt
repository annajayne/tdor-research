> Trans woman executed in Colima 
> By Scandal- March 27, 2024
> 
> In a tragic incident that occurred in the "El Tívoli" neighborhood of the City of Colima, a trans woman was shot dead on calle Belisario Domínguez, near the train tracks.
> Witnesses to the incident reported that the trans woman suffered multiple gunshot wounds to various parts of her body.
> 
> Witnesses reported that armed individuals approached the trans woman and shot her repeatedly before fleeing. 
> 
> Upon arriving at the scene, emergency teams found the trans woman dead.
> 
> Unfortunately, the attackers have not yet been arrested, which has caused dismay in the community and a call to the authorities to clarify this crime and provide justice to the victim and her loved ones.
