> 2024-04-12.- The victim of a run-over accident recorded in the industrial city in Veracruz, at midnight yesterday Thursday, was identified, who was destroyed after being run over by a truck driver who fled.
> 
> Veracruz, Veracruz.- The victim of a hit-and-run accident recorded in the industrial city in Veracruz, at midnight yesterday Thursday, was identified. She was destroyed after being run over by a hit-and-run truck.
> 
> This is Fernanda Rodriguez Lara, a trans woman, who went to the aforementioned area to work.
> 
> The member of the LGBTIQ+ community, Yazz Bustamante, confirmed the identity of the victim of the unfortunate accident on her social networks:
> 
> “They have already identified the trans woman run over in an industrial city in Veracruz, #FernandaRodriguezLara, part of her body was destroyed and unrecognizable; She was run over by a truck driver who fled and dragged Fernanda's body several meters between the tires. To date, there is no official statement from the prosecutor's office regarding the investigation of the truck driver who fled. (Photograph taken from the social networks right here where applicable)”


> Veracruz, Veracruz.—La persona que fue asesinada por un tráiler en Ciudad Industrial en el puerto de Veracruz, la noche del pasado jueves 11 de abril, fue identificada.
>
>  This is a trans woman who responded to the name of Fernanda Rodríguez Lara.
> 
> The civil association of the LGBT + community, Soy Humano, confirmed that the victim's remains were identified and are expected to be delivered to her relatives in the next few hours.
> 
> “Part of her body was destroyed and unrecognizable; “She was run over by a truck driver, who fled and dragged Fernanda's body several meters between the tires,” the association reported.
