> In a tragic incident that occurred in the town of Samayac, Suchitepéquez, the murder of Petronilo Cacoj, popularly known as "La Petra", was reported inside an establishment dedicated to the sale of intoxicating beverages in the Variedades sector, under the jurisdiction of the Canton conception.
