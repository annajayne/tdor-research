> Ciudad Juárez.– The body of a transgender woman of Venezuelan origin was found inside her home in the Libertad neighborhood and in an advanced state of decomposition.
> 
> The discovery was made by neighbors on Tuesday night. They had not heard from her for several days.
> 
> The woman, identified as Estrella, was inside the house located on calle Marfil, near the intersection with Calcopyrita.
> 
> Elements of the Municipal Public Security Secretariat (SSPM) arrived at the site and secured the area.
> 
> It has not been determined whether the death was natural or a result of violence.




> The body of a transgender woman was found in the Libertad neighborhood
> Agents from the Municipal Public Security Secretariat (SSPM) arrived at the scene and were in charge of securing the area.
> 
> Last night, Tuesday, a body was found, apparently a transgender woman of foreign origin, in her home in the Libertad neighborhood.
> 
> It was revealed that the body was already in an advanced state of decomposition, which was found in a house on Marfil Street, near the intersection with Chalcopyrita , in said colony.
> 
> Neighbors made and reported this discovery. They had not heard from this person for days, who was said to be of Venezuelan origin.
> 
> Agents from the Municipal Public Security Secretariat (SSPM) arrived at the scene and were in charge of securing the area.
> 
> Elements of the State Attorney General's Office also arrived to carry out the corresponding investigations regarding this incident.
> 
> The body was transferred to the facilities of the Forensic Medical Service , where the legal autopsy will be performed.
> 
> The authorities will be responsible for determining the identity of this person and the cause of death.
