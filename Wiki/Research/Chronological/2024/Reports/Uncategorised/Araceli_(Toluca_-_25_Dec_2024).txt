> TERRIBLE. Araceli was a trans woman; today she was found dead in the house she was renting
> BY: Christian De Jesus / December 25, 2024
> 
> Araceli was a trans woman who was found dead on the morning of Wednesday, December 25, in a house she rented.
> 
> It was Mrs. María de Jesús, who rented her home to her, who found her lifeless and decomposing in the property located in the Capultitlán district in Toluca.
> 
> The victim, between 40 and 50 years old, was part of the LGBTTTIQ+ community.
> 
> The owner of the house had not heard from Araceli for several days and when she entered the property on Wednesday, she saw the body lying on the floor among clothes and blankets.
> 
> The house is located on Reforma Street and San Pablo David.
> 
> An ambulance from the State of Mexico Emergency Service arrived at the scene; However, paramedics deduced that she had been dead for weeks, although the cause of death is unknown, a source said.
> 
> State and municipal police went to the site to secure the scene, and also placed a cordon around the main entrance, then notified both the Public Prosecutor's Office and experts for removal.
> 
> Through its regional headquarters, the Attorney General's Office of the State of Mexico will be in charge of determining, based on the results of the autopsy, what caused the death in the next few hours, according to reports.
