> Trans woman found dead at Regent Park
> May 24, 2024, 04:19 IST
> 
> Kolkata: A 27-year-old trans woman hailing from Siliguri and living in Kolkata for the past several weeks, was found dead under mysterious circumstances on Thursday. Cops said the victim, Tamoghna Sen, was found lying unconscious on the floor of her rented accommodation at Purba Putiary around 11 am. The body has been sent for autopsy. Cops have not ruled out suicide along with accidental death.
> 
> According to police and local sources, Tamoghna’s family resides in Siliguri’s Uttar Bharatnagar. She lived alone in a flat in Kolkata. On Thursday morning, when the housemaid came to work, she found Tamoghna lying in the room in a bloodied state.Cops recovered a liquor bottle from the room which had been seized to ascertain if it contained poison. Tamoghna was taken to the hospital where doctors declared her dead. Neighbours said that Tamoghna did not socialize with anyone in the neighbourhood and often left home for work. However, she loved dancing and singing, they added.
