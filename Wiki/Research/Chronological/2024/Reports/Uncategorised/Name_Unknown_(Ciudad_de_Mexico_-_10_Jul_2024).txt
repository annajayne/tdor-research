> Trans man found dead in flowerbed on Chapultepec Avenue
> The lifeless body of a man apparently belonging to the LGBT+ community was found half-naked inside a median strip.
> 
> Posted onJuly 10, 2024, 12:40
> 
> CUAUHTÉMOC, Mexico City.- On the morning of this Wednesday, July 10, in the Roma neighborhood , where the lifeless body of a man was found, apparently belonging to the LGBT+ community.
> 
> It occurred on Chapultepec Avenue, at the height of Eje 2 Poniente Monterrey , in the Roma neighborhood, where a man wearing women's clothing, long hair, a white jacket and blue pants, was found unconscious on the median that divides the central lanes from the side lanes.
> 
> It was the windshield wipers working at this road intersection who located the victim and consequently alerted the SSC traffic elements , who confirmed the incident and alerted the authorities of the Attorney General's Office (FGJ).
> 
> The area remained cordoned off and at times, the road was closed to allow the work of the experts, who took samples to begin the corresponding investigations.
