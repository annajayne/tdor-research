> Body of 29-Year-Old Trans Woman Found in Advanced State of Decomposition in Apucarana; Possible Natural Death, Delegate Ricardo Monteiro Speaks about the Case
> On Tuesday afternoon (26), the body of a 29-year-old trans woman was found in an advanced state of decomposition in a residence on Rua Professora Leonina Rezende, in the Cidade Educação region, in Apucarana. The situation mobilized teams from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU), Military Police, Scientific Police and Legal Medical Institute (IML). “She had a history of fainting and other medical conditions that may have contributed to her death,” reported acquaintances.
> YouTube video player
> According to the Operational Delegate of the 17th Police Subdivision (SDP), Dr. Ricardo Monteiro de Toledo, the police were called around 3 pm and found the property locked.
> 
> — “The body is that of a 29-year-old trans person, known as India. Initially, it was in an advanced state of decomposition, but a preliminary analysis shows no signs of violence against the body. We are initially working with the possibility of natural death. The body will undergo forensic examinations to confirm the cause of death,” explained the police chief.
> 
> Witnesses were interviewed at the scene, and other people will be identified to help reconstruct the victim's last moments.
> 
> — “These witnesses indicated other people who may have important information. We will investigate everything to understand exactly what happened. After the examinations of the body, we will have more clarity about the circumstances of the death,” said Dr. Ricardo.
> 
> Circumstances of the Case
> The police chief highlighted that the victim was found naked, with clothes and blankets over her body. A witness reported seeing someone trying to wake the victim through the window, believing she was asleep or passed out.
> 
> — “What is striking is that this person did not call for help. We are going to identify him to understand the reason for this,” he highlighted.
> 
> Health Problems
> According to people close to her, the trans woman suffered from serious health problems, having already fainted at the Emergency Care Unit (UPA) in Apucarana on other occasions.
> 
> — “She had a history of fainting and other medical conditions that may have contributed to her death,” acquaintances said.
> 
> The body was sent to the Apucarana IML for detailed examinations, which will be crucial to determine the cause of death. In the meantime, investigations continue to clarify the facts and rule out any possibility of crime.
> 
> The case caught the attention of local residents, who watched the police teams' movements in the area with apprehension.
