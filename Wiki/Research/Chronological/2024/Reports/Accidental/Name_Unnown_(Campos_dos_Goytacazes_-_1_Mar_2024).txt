drowned


> Transvestite drowns in the Paraíba River, in Campos
> Name Unknown
> 
> The identity of the victim has not been released
> March 1, 2024 | 11:17 am | By: Bruno Rocha
> 
> A transvestite drowned after diving into the Paraíba do Sul River, close to the Saturnino de Brito Bridge, in the central area of Campos dos Goytacazes, this Friday morning (1st). The victim's identification was not released.
> 
> According to initial information, the transvestite took a shower for a few minutes, but then disappeared into the water. The Fire Department team were called and found the victim's body after searching.
> 
> The body was removed to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) in Campos.
