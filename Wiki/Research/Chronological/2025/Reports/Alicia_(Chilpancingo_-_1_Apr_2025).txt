> CHILPANCINGO, Gro., April 1, 2025.- A trans woman was stabbed to death in the early hours of Sunday and was buried by her relatives on Monday in the town of Coscamilla, municipality of Quechultenango.
> 
> This crime was reported by the LGBTI+ Collective Orgullo Guerrero through its social media channels, where it demanded the intervention of authorities to clarify the case. 
> 
> Alicia was a trans woman from Quechultenango. Her body was found at approximately 4 a.m. on Sunday with stab wounds, on the bank of a river in the community. 
> 
> The LGBTI+ Collective Orgullo Guerrero recalled that Monday, March 31, was International Transgender Day of Visibility, and that was the date Alicia was buried on that day. They noted that the commemoration took place in the context of a crime. 
>
