> The reality TV world is in mourning after the death of a star from the LGBTQ+ reality show ' RuPaul's Drag Race ' was announced in recent hours.
> 
> This is Jiggly Caliente , 44, famous for participating in drag television shows such as ' RuPaul's Drag Race ', ' RuPaul's Drag Race: All Stars ' and ' Drag Race Philippines '.
> 
> The news was confirmed by Jiggly's family on their official Instagram account.
> 
> “It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Bianca Castro-Arabejo, known and loved around the world as Jiggly Caliente,” they said.
> 
> In the statement released by the family, they reported that Jiggly's right leg had been amputated due to a severe infection.
> 
> It had even been reported that due to her health problems, her participation in 'RuPaul's Drag Race Philippines' was canceled, as well as any public appearances.
> 
> According to the report, the trans actress died peacefully on April 26 at 4:42 a.m., surrounded by her loving family and closest friends.
> 
> And her family described her as a luminous presence in the world of entertainment and defense; with contagious energy, unique humor, and unparalleled authenticity.
> 
> So far, neither the creator of the concept nor the program's official account have commented on the tragic news.
