> *SHE WAS FOUND DEAD IN HER HOME, APPARENTLY SHE WAS SUFFERING FROM AN INCURABLE DISEASE
> 
> Relatives found Fernanda Hernández, alias Fanny Le Fem, who was an icon of the LGTBIQ+ community, dead in the city of Tlapacoyan, in the Martínez de la Torre region.
> 
> Initial reports indicate that his loved ones called 911, but the ambulance only confirmed his death upon arrival, so forensic services carried out the necessary examination.
> 
> For now, her family has told authorities that Fernanda had been suffering from an illness for several months and was fighting for her life. However, yesterday morning she lost the battle.
> 
> Fanny Le Fem was a well-known person in the citrus-growing region of the state of Veracruz, for her participation in various artistic events, in addition, for being one of the pioneers in fighting for the rights of the gay community.
> 
> The events took place at the height of Ignacio de la Llave Avenue between Juárez and Serdán, where elements of Municipal Public Security attended, followed by rescuers.
> 
> Experts from Forensic Services carried out the corresponding work, where after speaking with relatives they confirmed that the deceased person had a history of illness, which is why the body was not transferred to the SEMEFO, remaining with his relatives to give him a Christian burial.
