>  A 40-year-old man was found dead in his garage on Via Marco Perennio, and the cause of death is believed to have been a narcotic overdose . That is, a dose that was too pure or poorly blended.
> 
> The tragic event dates back to September 18th but was only learned today. An autopsy was performed in Siena in recent days . Today, Wednesday, September 24th , the body was returned to the family.
> 
> The 40-year-old , originally from Alessandria but resident in Arezzo , was found lifeless with classic signs of previous drug use. A syringe was found next to his body .
> 
> The Carabinieri have launched an investigation to identify the drug dealer who supplied him with the lethal dose . The Misericordia has recovered his body, which has been made available to the prosecutor 's office for forensic examinations  on Monday at Le Scotte di Siena.
