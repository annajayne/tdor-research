> Camila Gil, a renowned trans DJ and LGBTI activist, passed away on Thursday, June 5, 2025, at the age of 29, her family confirmed on social media. The news was shared in an Instagram story in which her loved ones asked for respect and sensitivity for the moment they are going through.
> 
> The information was originally published by El Tiempo de Bogotá , an outlet that highlighted Gil's career in the electronic music scene and his fight for the rights of trans people in Colombia.
> 
> “Camila was much more than her talent: she was a symbol of freedom, authenticity, and resilience . Her energy, her light, and her courage transformed lives,” her family wrote.
> 
> Although the family did not provide details on the cause of death, relatives said Camila Gil had been experiencing health problems in recent weeks . According to a recent report from El Tiempo de Bogotá , the young woman suffered a respiratory and lung infection possibly linked to vaping.
> 
> Hours before her death was announced, Camila shared a message on Instagram asking for prayers for her health, which set off alarm bells among her followers.
> 
> The news was picked up by several Colombian media outlets, including websites specializing in music and diversity. Among them, Bogotá's El Tiempo highlighted the impact of her artistic career and her activism on behalf of the trans community.
> 
> Camila Gil was one of the first trans women to achieve notoriety in the world of electronic music in Colombia. With more than 400,000 followers on Instagram, her style combined tech house, circuit, and guaracha, and she performed in cities like Bogotá, Medellín, Cali, and Santa Marta, as well as on stages in Mexico and Spain.
> 
> In addition to her career as a DJ, she was an active voice against discrimination. Her YouTube channel began as a space to denounce the violence she experienced in Medellín and evolved into an audiovisual diary of her daily life.
> 
> His family reported that the wake was held on Thursday in Medellín and that the religious ceremony would take place this Friday, June 6, 2025, at 10:30 am in the Montesacro Chapel.
