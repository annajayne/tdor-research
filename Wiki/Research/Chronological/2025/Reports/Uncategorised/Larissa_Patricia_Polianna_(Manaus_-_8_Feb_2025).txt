> Trans woman dies after being run over by two cars in Ponta Negra; drivers fled
> The victim was trying to cross Avenida Coronel Teixeira when he was hit and thrown; another vehicle then ran over the body without providing assistance. The case will be investigated by the police.
> 
> Joana Queiroz
> 02/09/2025 at 10:06.
> 
> A trans woman identified in the police report as 'Deusimar' and on social media as Larissa Patrícia Polianna , the influencer 'Buiu a Bonita ', died after being hit by two cars while trying to cross Avenida Coronel Teixeira, in Ponta Negra, West Zone of Manaus. 
> 
> The accident occurred on Saturday night (8) and was witnessed by coconut vendors. One of them said that the victim was trying to cross the road when he was hit and thrown by a car of unknown characteristics. Soon after, a white Honda Civic ran over the body, which was crushed and the brain matter spread across the avenue.
> 
> 
> Neither driver stopped to provide assistance. The victim's body was removed to the Forensic Medical Institute (IML), and the case will be investigated by the Specialized Traffic Accident Unit (DEAT).
> 
> The influencer's wake will be held at the Evangelical Assembly of God church in Pará in the Amazon, located at Rua Isarel, nº 55, Fátima I neighborhood.
