> A transvestite was found dead in Caneras de San Manuel Cortes
> Editorial. The body of a transgender person was found lifeless in the sugarcane fields of San Manuel, Cortés.
> 
> The victim was identified as Jorge Alberto Oliva, and known by his gender identity as Kendra, 28 years old. She had been deported from the United States two weeks ago.
> 
> In addition to her National Identity Document (DNI) that allowed her to be recognized, the authorities reported a tattoo she had on his ribs.
> 
> Her relatives reported his disappearance on social media and asked for help in finding her whereabouts.
> 
> Her sister, Elizabeth Oliva, explained that Kendra was in a shelter in Tijuana, then entered the United States and moved from South Carolina to California.
> 
> “During that time she maintained constant contact with her family,” she said.
