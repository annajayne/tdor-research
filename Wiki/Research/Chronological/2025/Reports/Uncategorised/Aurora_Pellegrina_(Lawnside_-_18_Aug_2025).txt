Her name was Aurora Pellegrina, but she also went by Alexa and Luna (she was a system and had DID). She was 36 years old. Aurora used she/her pronouns, Alexa used xe/xem or they/they pronouns, and Luna used fay/faer or they/them pronouns. I've attached the best photo I could find.
