> Hellen Marques, a trans artist who performed at several clubs in São Paulo, has died at the age of 31. The information was released by the Blue Space nightclub, located in the capital of São Paulo.
> 
> "It is with great sadness that we inform you of the passing of this great artist Hellen Marques, who brought us so much joy with her art. We will miss her greatly," the statement said.
> 
> The iconic performer of the national LGBTQIA+ scene would have celebrated her birthday on May 18. Speaking to Quem magazine, Hellen's mother, Francisca Auristênia Teixeira Moura, thanked everyone for their displays of affection and asked that the moment of mourning be respected.
> 
> “We hope that, with the information that my daughter was with us at all times, it will be enough to comfort the hearts of all those who are suffering with us and thus avoid unnecessary speculation and interference in a situation that, in itself, is already very challenging,” he said.
> 
> In addition to her mother, the artist leaves behind two siblings, a brother and a Shih Tzu dog. The wake is scheduled for Tuesday (4/15), at the Dom Bosco Cemetery, in São Paulo, at 9 am. The burial will be at 11 am.
