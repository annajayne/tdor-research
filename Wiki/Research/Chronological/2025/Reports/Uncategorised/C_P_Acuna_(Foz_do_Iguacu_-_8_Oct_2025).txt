> Trans woman found dead in front of motel in Foz do Iguaçu
> October 8, 2025, at 10:03 AM | Catve
> Photo: Oops News Foz na Hora
> 
> A trans woman was found dead in the early hours of Tuesday (8) on Avenida Costa e Silva, in front of a motel, in Foz do Iguaçu.
> 
> According to information gathered, locals located the body and called the Military Police. The road was cordoned off until the arrival of the Scientific Police and the Forensic Medical Institute (IML), who conducted an investigation at the scene.
> 
> The body was collected and sent to the IML in Foz do Iguaçu for further examinations and subsequent release to family members.
> 
> The victim was identified as Celino Portillo Acuña, 35, from Paraguay.
> 
> Celino had previously gained prominence after being arrested for setting fire to her own home in the Caacupemí Settlement in Presidente Franco. At the time, neighbors reported that she was drunk and out of control.
> 
> The circumstances of the death are being investigated by the Foz do Iguaçu Homicide Division, which has not ruled out any hypothesis at this time.
