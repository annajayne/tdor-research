> Last Friday (04) the body of a trans woman was found wrapped in a tarpaulin on the sidewalk of a state school in the Jatiúca neighborhood, in Maceió-AL. The victim was identified as Marcela Valentina, 20 years old. Marcela lived in the Salgado neighborhood, in Caruaru, and worked as a makeup artist.
> 
> Marcela's body was found in an area where garbage is usually dumped. According to witnesses, close to where she was found, Marcela was with a man the night before the crime, in the same place where she was found.
> 
> On Monday (7), the Scientific Police of Alagoas confirmed that the woman died as a result of a traumatic brain injury (TBI). The expert report indicates that she was raped with a blunt instrument, which was confirmed by the IML. The young woman had bruises on her face and punctures in her head from an unidentified object. Personal objects such as a purse and cell phone were found next to the victim's body, but there were no documents. Even though it was broken, the cell phone was collected and will be examined by experts. There was a receipt indicating that she had lunch two days earlier at a restaurant inside a shopping mall.
> 
> Marcela's body was released by her mother this Sunday (6), after the completion of the autopsy examination, which will be sent to the Homicide and Personal Protection Division (DHPP) of the Civil Police, which continues investigating to identify the person responsible for the crime.




> The Civil Police of Alagoas (PCAL) reported that the woman found dead on the morning of this Friday (5), on the sidewalk behind a state school located in the Jatiúca neighborhood, is a trans woman, not yet officially identified.
> 
> The victim was wrapped in a tarpaulin and already dead when the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu) arrived at the scene.



> A woman was found dead with signs of violence on Friday morning (04), on the sidewalk of the Rosalvo Lobo State School, in the Jatiúca neighborhood, in Maceió. The suspect of the homicide remains at large. 
> 
> According to information passed on to TNH1 , the woman was covered in blood and wrapped in a tarp, which was also stained with blood. In addition, the victim's face had injuries. Because the images were graphic, the report chose not to release them. 
> 
> Residents of the region reported that the victim was homeless, which is yet to be confirmed by the police during the investigation. The woman's identity is not yet known. 
> 
> The Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) was called and confirmed the death. The Criminalistics Institute (IC) and the Legal Medical Institute (IML) went to the scene. The expert will determine the cause of death. 
> 
> Any information regarding possible suspects and the motive for the crime can be provided to the police by calling Disque Denúncia (telephone 181).
