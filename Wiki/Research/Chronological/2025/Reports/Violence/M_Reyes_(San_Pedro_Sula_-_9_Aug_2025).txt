> A member of the Lesbian, Gay, Trans, Bisexual, Intersex, and Queer (LGBTQI+) community was murdered Saturday morning in the Bográn neighborhood, in the northern part of the country.
> 
> The victim was identified as Moisés Elías Reyes, 51, who made his living selling lottery tickets. According to the preliminary report, Reyes was attacked with a knife in a public street. Seriously wounded, he managed to get to his apartment, where he ultimately died.
> 
> The incident has caused alarm among neighbors and family members, who said they pay for security services to ensure their peace of mind, but this attack has highlighted their vulnerability.
> 
> According to the Violence Observatory of the National Autonomous University of Honduras (OV-UNAH), an average of six people die violently every day in the country.
> 
> Police authorities are investigating the motive for the crime and have not ruled out hate crimes.




> A trans man was murdered inside a home in the Bográn neighborhood of San Pedro Sula Cortés.
> 
> The man was 52-year-old Moisés Reyes, who was taken to the San Pedro Sula morgue after the crime.
> 
> The victim's neighbors and family are alarmed by the incident, as they cannot explain how the criminals entered the area when they pay for security and there is a roadblock to their homes.
> 
> Police officers arrived at the scene to conduct the necessary investigations.
