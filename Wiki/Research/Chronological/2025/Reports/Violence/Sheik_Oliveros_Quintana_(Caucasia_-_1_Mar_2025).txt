> In the early morning of March 1, 2025, a homicide was reported in the La Colombianita invasion sector , in the rural area of the municipality of Caucasia .
> 
> The victim was identified as Sheik Quintana, 30 years old, a member of the LGTBIQ+ community.
> 
> According to citizen reports, a man forced his way into the residence and shot the victim, then fled without any problems.
> 
> Authorities cordoned off the area and gathered information in the area. The technical inspection of the body was carried out by the CTI of Caucasia, while investigations continue to find the person responsible for the crime.
