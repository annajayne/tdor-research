> Trans woman murdered inside a home in Vista Esmeralda II, León.
> Free Trade Zone
> August 15, 2025
> 
> León, Gto . Early Friday morning, a trans woman was shot and killed inside a home in the Vista Esmeralda II neighborhood.
> 
> The incident was reported at 1:27 a.m. on the corner of callesVista Grulla and Vista Alta. Police and paramedics confirmed the death upon arrival.
> 
> The victim was wearing blue jeans and black and white tennis shoes.
> 
> The name of the deceased is yet to be confirmed following investigations conducted by the Deceased Persons Identification Unit .
> 
> Inside the home, several packages containing substances such as crystal meth, cocaine, and psychotropic pills were found, which were seized by authorities.
> 
> The State Attorney General's Office, through the Specialized Homicide Investigation Unit , continues its investigation to clarify the case and find those responsible.
> 
> It's worth mentioning that a few blocks from the home, last Wednesday a 19-year-old man was shot and taken to a hospital by his relatives.
> 
> So far there are no arrests.
