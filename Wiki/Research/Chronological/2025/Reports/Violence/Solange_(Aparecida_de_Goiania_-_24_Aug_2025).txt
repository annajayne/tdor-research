> A transvestite was murdered on Sunday morning (24), in the motel region, in Aparecida de Goiânia. The Military Police (PM) of Goiás confirmed the death, but did not give details about the case, which remains under investigation. The Scientific Police were at the scene to carry out an expert analysis and informed Mais Goiás that the victim's social name is Solange (PDR), 23 years old, hit with a shot in the dorsal region.
> 
> According to a witness, also a transvestite, the crime allegedly occurred after the victim stole a man's cell phone and demanded R$50 for its return. Upon discovering the theft, the suspect attempted to retrieve the device but was refused. He then allegedly pulled out a revolver and shot the transvestite, who died at the scene.
> 
> After the crime, teams from the Fire Department and the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) were called, but found the victim dead at the scene. According to reports, the suspect is still at large. Mais Goiás is trying to gather more information about the case. The suspect's name has not been released.
