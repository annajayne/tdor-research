> Transvestite Sofia is shot dead on a rural road in Betim
> Body was found with marks of at least four shots
> From Inside Minas EditorialFebruary 15, 2025
> 
> A transvestite, known as Sofia, was shot dead on a rural road in the Charneca neighborhood, in Betim, in the Metropolitan Region of Belo Horizonte, on the night of this Friday (14).
> 
> According to the Military Police, the victim was shot at least four times in the legs, torso, neck and head.
> 
> Police believe the victim tried to flee from the attacker, but was shot in the legs and then executed. The body was on a dirt road.
> 
> The technical experts collected four 9mm ammunition capsules. The body was sent to the Betim Forensic Medical Post.
