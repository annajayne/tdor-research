> Hyderabad: Body of transgender person found in Kukatpally
> Hyderabad: An unidentified transgender person was found dead under suspicious circumstances in a secluded spot at Kaithalapur in Kukatpally on Saturday afternoon. The police are investigating if it is a murder.

> According to the police, the body of the victim who is aged in thirties was found lying in a highly decomposed state amidst bushes. It was found by the local residents, who alerted the police.
> 
> The Kukatpally police booked a case and took up investigation. They suspect that the victim was accompanied by some unknown persons to the spot.
> 
> More details on the cause of death will be known after an autopsy, police said.
> 
> The body was shifted to the Gandhi Hospital morgue.
