====== Jimena C. (Apatzingan - 13 Jun 2025) ======

Michoacán

shot


> JImena C., a woman belonging to the trans community , was murdered this Friday night at the Spartan Gym in the municipality of Apatzingán, after receiving at least ten gunshot wounds from armed individuals who entered the establishment.
> 
> The crime against the victim from the LGBTTTIQ+ community was confirmed by the Apatzingán Human Security Observatory (OSHRA), which reported that the attack took place at the building located at the intersection of avenidas Francisco I. Madero and Joaquín Amaro in the Lázaro Cárdenas neighborhood.
> 
> According to reports, the alleged criminals entered the gym where they shot Jimena , who was 30 years old, leaving her lying on the floor and then fled without any information about their whereabouts.
> 
> After the incident, the victim was taken by paramedics to the Apatzingán General Hospital , attached to the Michoacán Health Secretariat (SSM), where she sadly died a few minutes later due to the bullet wounds she received.
> 
> Municipal police officers were dispatched to the crime scene to cordon off the area, while personnel from the State Attorney General's Office (FGE) began the corresponding investigation to clarify the victim's murder and arrest those responsible.
> 
> The motive behind the murder is currently unknown; whether it was due to hate speech perpetrated because of her gender identity or other unrelated circumstances will be determined during the investigation.
> 
> Jimena's death comes during LGBTTTIQ+ Pride Month, where traditional marches led by diversity groups are held in various municipalities. In Apatzingán, this will be on June 28.
> 
> Finally, studies by the Michoacán es Diversidad collective indicate that trans women are the most vulnerable segment of the gender-diverse community, with 27 deaths reported in the last 10 years . Apatzingán ranks fourth in the state for the highest incidence.
