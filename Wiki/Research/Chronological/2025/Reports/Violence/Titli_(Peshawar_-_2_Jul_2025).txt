> A transgender person lost [her] life when unidentified persons opened fire on [her] in Peshawar, reported 24NewsHD TV channel on Wednesday.
> 
> The tragic incident occurred in Siddique Colony in the jurisdiction of Gulbahar Police Station in Peshawar. The deceased identified as Farman was a resident of Swat.
> 
> Soon after the incident, all the accused escaped from the scene. Police established barricades at many places and started a search operation to apprehend the accused.



> The funeral of a Manzil Foundation focal peprson Titli was held at Manzil Foundation office after the family refused to receive the dead body.
>
> KP Transgender Community President, Madam Arzoo Khan, said:
>
> This is not the first time a family has refused to accept the body of their transgender child. Even after death, transgender persons are not accepted. IG Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Police should take notice of the violence and attacks against transgender community.
