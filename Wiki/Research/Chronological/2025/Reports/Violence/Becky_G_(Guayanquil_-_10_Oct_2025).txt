> Trans woman Becky G murdered for refusing to pay vacuna in Guayaquil
> The crime occurred in the Oro Verde Hotel area and exposes the vulnerability of trans sex workers in Ecuador.
> By Nicole ProañoOctober 11, 2025 at 11:06 a.m.
>
> In the early hours of Friday, October 10, 2025 , violence shook Guayaquil once again . A 25 -year-old transgender woman , known on social media as 'Becky G' , was brutally murdered after refusing to pay a "vacuna", a form of extortion that affects merchants, transporters and sex workers in different parts of the country.
>
> According to the Silueta X Association , an organization that defends the rights of the LGBTIQ+ population, the crime occurred in the early hours of the morning , in the vicinity of the Oro Verde Hotel , in the 1° de Mayo sector.
>
> According to the statement, several men allegedly linked to extortion networks approached Becky G and demanded payment to allow her to continue working in the area. When the young woman refused to hand over the “vacuna,” the attackers shot her at point-blank range, leaving her dead on the road.
> 
> Witnesses reported that her body was in a fetal position, a reflection of the violence with which she was attacked.
> 
> Becky G was known for her active presence on social media , where she shared her daily life and spoke openly about her sex work, becoming a visible voice within a constantly threatened community.
