> Ciudad Juárez.— LGBTTTIQ+ community organizations and activists called for a peaceful demonstration this Saturday, May 11, in front of the Specialized Women's Prosecutor's Office, located on Zaragoza Boulevard in Rincón del Sur, to demand justice for the murder of a trans woman this morning, whose identity has not yet been confirmed by authorities.
> 
> The protest, scheduled for 6:00 p.m., sought to highlight the growing violence against trans women in the region and demand clarification of the case, as well as the eradication of transphobia and misogyny, which disproportionately affect this community.
> 
> "Enough trans femicides!" is the call at the top of the campaign spread on social media.
> 
> Attendees are invited to carry flags, candles, and banners as symbols of memory, protest, and demand for justice.

> 
> CHIHUAHUA.- A transgender woman was the victim of a brutal attack early Saturday morning on a vacant lot in the Chaveña neighborhood.
> 
> But it wasn't until Saturday afternoon that she was located by passersby. Authorities received a report of the woman's presence, who had multiple stab wounds and blows to the head from a large rock.
> 
> Municipal Rescue paramedics responded to the scene and transported the victim to the General Hospital, where her death was sadly reported hours later.
> 
> Members of the Forensic Medical Service responded to the hospital to transport the body to their facilities where forensic tests could be performed and any clues found that might lead to those responsible.
