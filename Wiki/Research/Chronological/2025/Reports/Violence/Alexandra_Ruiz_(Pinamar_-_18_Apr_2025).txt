> Trans groups are calling for a rally on social media this Monday at 6:30 p.m. in Plaza Cagancha to protest the murder of a 51-year-old trans woman who was found shot in the chest in her home on 60th Street in Pinamar Norte, Canelones. The incident occurred around 8:00 p.m. on Friday.
> 
> A friend of the deceased, a 57-year-old man, tried repeatedly throughout the day to contact her through calls and WhatsApp messages, but received no response. He alerted 911, who went to the home and found her lifeless upon entering. They found she had a gunshot wound to the right side of her chest.
> 
> According to sources familiar with the case, the woman had a long criminal record. No arrests have been made so far, and several lines of investigation are underway. No video surveillance cameras have been detected in the area that could provide any information.



> Asesinaron a una mujer trans de 51 años en Pinamar Norte; la encontraron en su casa con un disparo en el pecho
> The case has now been transferred to the Prosecutor's Office and the Homicide Department.
> 
> A 51-year-old trans woman was shot in the chest in her home in Pinamar Norte.
> 
> According to information released by the Canelones Police, a 57-year-old man called 911 in the last few hours to report that he had been trying to contact a friend several times but that she hadn't responded.
> 
> “Personnel from Salinas Precinct 22 went to a home located on 60th Street in the Pinamar Norte area, where they interviewed a 57-year-old man. He stated that he tried to contact his 51-year-old friend (a trans person), and when he didn't get a response, he called 911,” the police report states.
> 
> Once they entered the house, the woman was found "lifeless." "It was determined that she had a gunshot wound to the right side of her chest," the police added.
> 
> The case has now been transferred to the Prosecutor's Office and the Homicide Department.
