> Rosa, a 27-year-old transgender woman, was tortured and murdered in the Daniel Lisboa neighborhood of Salvador, Bahia. The victim was known for being hardworking and well-liked, with no involvement in crime. The police are investigating the case, while Rosa's family is seeking answers and support so she doesn't become just another statistic.



> Salvador: Mulher trans é encontrada morta com mãos amarradas
> Salvador: Trans woman found dead with her hands tied
> The body was on a staircase, with several gunshot wounds.
> 
> A woman was found dead on Saturday morning (2), on a staircase in Engenho Velho de Brotas, in Salvador. The victim, not yet identified, was transgender and had her hands tied, hit by several shots, with exquisite torture and execution.
> 
> The crime took place on Travessa Wilson Sanches. Agents from the 26th Independent Company (CIPM/Brotas) arrived at the scene and found her already dead, lying on the ground. The area was cordoned off for forensic examination, and the body was then taken to the city's IML.
> 
> The case will be investigated by the 1st Homicide Division (DH/Atlântico). There are currently no hypotheses surrounding the murder.
