> A transgender woman was found dead inside  her home , located at the intersection of Calamar and Vetano , in the El Mármol neighborhood , in Ciudad Juárez.  The body was found by the victim's daughter , who reported that, upon entering the house , she found her mother in one of the bedrooms , next to the bed, and with visible signs of violence , so she immediately reported it to the authorities through the emergency number 911.
> 
> Members of the Municipal Public Security Secretariat arrived at the scene and confirmed the death of the woman , identified as Nubia IT, 34 years old. In addition, experts gathered evidence to begin the corresponding investigation , which could be related to the victim's sexual orientation.
> 
> So far, authorities have not released any further information related to the incident ; more details are expected in the coming hours .




> Ciudad Juárez.– A trans woman was found dead, allegedly murdered inside a home.
> 
> The discovery occurred Saturday night at the intersection of Calamar and Vetano streets in the El Safari neighborhood.
> 
> According to initial reports from authorities, the victim's son made the discovery and reported it through the 911 emergency service.
> 
> The victim showed signs of beatings and was found lying at the entrance to the bedroom.
> 
> Members of the Municipal Public Security Secretariat (SSPM) responded to the scene and, after verifying the events, proceeded to cordon off the scene.
> 
> The State Attorney General's Office (FGE) and the Specialized Prosecutor's Office for Women (FEM) arrived to begin the corresponding investigations into the 54th homicide of the month.
