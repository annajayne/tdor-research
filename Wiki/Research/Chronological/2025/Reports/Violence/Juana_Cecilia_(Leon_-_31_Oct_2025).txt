> LLeón, Gto. - This Friday morning , a woman was shot and killed in the Ampliación San Francisco de Asís neighborhood . Neighbors in the area stated that armed men in a sedan were responsible for the crime .
> 
> This shooting was reported at 9:45 a.m. this Friday on Frailes Menores Street, between Fray Román and Fray León, in the aforementioned neighborhood.
> 
> Some witnesses mentioned that the victim was walking home when armed men in a sedan began chasing her . She realized they were being pursued and quickly ran toward her house.
> 
> But just steps before entering her home, the assailants caught up with her and shot her several times, leaving her seriously wounded. Once they had accomplished their goal, the men quickly fled the scene.
> 
> Upon hearing the gunshots, residents of the area immediately called 911. Municipal police officers were the first to arrive, immediately cordoning off the scene and requesting an ambulance.
> 
> Fire department paramedics arrived and attempted to provide first aid, but unfortunately, the victim had no vital signs.
> 
> Residents of the area indicated that the victim's name was Juana Cecilia, approximately 37 years old. She was of slender build, approximately 1.63 meters tall, and was wearing a gray t-shirt with green sleeves, jeans, and white sneakers. She apparently lived alone in that neighborhood.





> León, Guanajuato - Juana Cecilia was shot to death after being caught by her attackers at the entrance of her home in theAmpliación San Francisco neighborhood.
> 
> The victim worked collecting cardboard and PET
> 
> The crime occurred at 9:45 a.m. this Friday, October 31, at the corner of Fray Román and Fray Sabatino streets.
> 
> Witnesses said that "Ceci," as she was affectionately called, was overtaken by two men on a black motorcycle.
> 
> "They were following her; she didn't make it inside the house, and they shot her six times," a neighbor recounted.
> 
> The assailants escaped, while witnesses rushed to help "Ceci" and called 911.



> León, Guanajuato. A trans woman , identified by neighbors as Juana Cecilia , was shot and killed Friday morning in the Ampliación San Francisco neighborhood .
> 
> The attack occurred around 9:45 a.m. at the corner of calles Frailes Menores and Fray Sabatino, where the victim was attacked by the occupants of a sedan, who fled after firing several shots.
> 
> An unidentified person was found at the scene . She was of slender build, approximately 1.63 meters tall, and was wearing a gray t-shirt with green sleeves, jeans, and white sneakers.
> 
> Neighbors identified her as Juana Cecilia, the name by which she was known in the area.
> 
> Municipal authorities secured the area and confirmed the discovery of spent shell casings, while the Guanajuato State Attorney General's Office opened an investigation with a gender perspective to clarify the crime.
> 
> So far, no arrests have been reported.
