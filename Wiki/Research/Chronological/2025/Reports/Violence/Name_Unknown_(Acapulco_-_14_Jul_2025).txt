A trans man and a trans woman were reportedly killed in a shooting in the La Sabana area of Acapulco, Guerrero (Mexico) on 14 Jul 2025.

Both victims are unidentified as yet.



> CHILPANCINGO, Gro., July 14, 2025.- A transgender man and woman were murdered in an armed attack at a convenience store in the entrance arch of the town of La Sabana, in Acapulco.
> 
> According to a report from neighbors who heard gunshots, the incident occurred around 8 p.m. on Sunday.
> 
> The victims were at the aforementioned location when they were surprised by armed men who shot them multiple times.
> 
> Security authorities arrived at the scene and confirmed the double homicide, securing the area to allow forensic experts from the State Attorney General's Office (FGE) to conduct the necessary investigations.
> 
> The bodies were transferred to the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) for legal autopsies.
