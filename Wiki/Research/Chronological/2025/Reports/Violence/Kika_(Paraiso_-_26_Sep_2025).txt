> LGBT murder
> A hairstylist and member of the LGBT+ community was murdered in the municipality of Paraíso. Photo: El Heraldo de Tabasco
> 
> A direct attack took the life of a stylist, a member of the LGBT+ community in the municipality of Paraíso , on the night of Friday, September 26.
> 
> The murder occurred when Kika , as she was known, was riddled with bullets while she was inside her business located on calle Francisco Gurría.
> 
> According to initial reports, the victim was in the company of another person when armed men entered the beauty salon, leaving a second person seriously injured.

> 
> It is worth noting that several acts of violence have been reported in the municipality of Paraíso, where several banners and posters signed by alleged criminal groups have been found hanging in various locations, including schools. 




> A transgender woman, known as "La Kika," was murdered yesterday in the Quintín Arauz neighborhood of the municipality of Paraíso, Tabasco.
> 
> According to initial reports, the victim was working at his beauty salon when armed men entered and shot him repeatedly.
> 
> The "Casa de las Muñecas Tiresias " association condemned the attack and reported that she is the third victim of transfemicide in the state. It should be noted, however, that this crime is not classified as such in the Tabasco penal code.
