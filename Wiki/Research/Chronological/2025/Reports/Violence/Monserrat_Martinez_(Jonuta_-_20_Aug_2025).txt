> Exige asociación civil justicia para mujer trans asesinada en Jonuta
> Civil association demands justice for trans woman murdered in Jonuta
> They urged the authorities to properly investigate the case.
> 
> Francisco Bautista Villegas
> August 21, 2025 - 9:30 pm
> The Tiresias Dolls' House Association demanded justice for Monserrat Martínez, a trans woman murdered last Wednesday in Jonuta.
> 
> In a post on their social media, they stated that "Monse" worked at the bar "El Campesino," where the crime was committed .
> 
> They pointed out that she is the second victim of transfeminicide in Tabasco and the 24th in Mexico .
> 
> In this regard, they urged the authorities of the Jonuta municipality and the State Attorney General's Office to conduct a proper investigation into the case.
> 
> Finally, they pointed out that Tabasco is not a safe state for transgender people, as there is no legislation regarding their rights .



> Transfemicide in Jonuta: "La Monse" killed in a bar
> Motorcycle riders murdered a member of the LGBT community identified as "La Monse" in a bar in Jonuta.
> 
> According to witness accounts, the incident occurred around 8:50 p.m., when the victim, known by the nickname "La Monse," was socializing inside the bar. / El Heraldo
> 
> 
> The municipal seat of Jonuta was the scene of a transfemicide on the night of August 20, 2025, when a member of the LGBT+ community was murdered in a bar.
> 
> According to witness accounts, the incident occurred around 8:50 p.m., when the victim, identified by the nickname "La Monse," was socializing inside the bar.
> 
> Suddenly, men riding a motorcycle burst into the scene and, without saying a word, headed straight for "La Monse" to shoot him dead. Once they had accomplished their mission, they fled the scene.
> 
> Municipal Police officers arrived at the scene and cordoned off the area to protect the crime scene, awaiting the experts from the State Attorney General's Office (FGE ), who will be in charge of carrying out the respective investigations, in addition to ordering the removal of the body for its transfer to the Forensic Medical Service (Semefo) , where the legal autopsy will be performed.
