> On the afternoon of last Friday (12), a trans woman was murdered in an adult film cinema, located on Rua Princesa Isabel, in the Santo Amaro neighborhood. The victim was identified as Raquelly Letícia, who was turning 24 on the date of the crime.
> 
> According to the Pernambuco Civil Police, she was found dead and suffering from stab wounds. "Investigations are ongoing until the crime is solved," the police said in a statement sent to Diario. The crime is being investigated by the Capital Homicide Task Force.
> 
> The report found that Raquelly had been involved in a street fight in downtown Recife a few days ago and had been avoiding the area because of the confrontation. Yesterday, friends and family were expecting her for a surprise birthday party.
> 
> In 2024, 105 trans people were killed in Brazil. Although the country recorded 14 fewer cases than in 2023, it remains, for the 17th consecutive year, the country with the highest number of trans people killed in the world. The data comes from the Dossier: National Registry of Transgender Deaths in Brazil in 2024: From Expectation of Death to a Look at the Living Presence of Trans Students in Brazilian Basic Education, by Rede Trans Brasil.
> 
> The majority of cases, 38%, were in the Northeast Region, which continues to be the one with the highest number of deaths of trans people since 2022.
