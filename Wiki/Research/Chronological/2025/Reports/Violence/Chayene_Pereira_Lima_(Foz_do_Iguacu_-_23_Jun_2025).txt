> A trans woman was found dead on Monday morning (23) in a vacant lot near the Foz do Iguaçu International Bus Station, in western Paraná.
> 
> Trans woman found dead in vacant lot
> The location is known for sheltering homeless and socially vulnerable people. According to the Military Police (PM), the body was found after reports from local residents.
> 
> The victim was identified as Chayene Pereira Lima, 36 years old. During the initial investigation, a perforation in the head was found, but it is not yet known whether it was caused by a firearm or a blunt object.
> 
> The body was collected and sent to the Forensic Medical Institute (IML), where it will undergo autopsy examinations, which should clarify the cause of death.
> 
> According to reports from residents, it is common for the land to be used by wanderers and people living in precarious conditions, who build improvised shacks there.
> 
> One of the witnesses said he had seen Chayene the day before, while she was setting up a makeshift structure to protect herself from the cold. The case is still being investigated by the Foz do Iguaçu Homicide Department.
