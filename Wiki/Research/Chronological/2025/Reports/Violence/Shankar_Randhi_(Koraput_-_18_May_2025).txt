> Transgender Priest Beaten to Death in Koraput; Uncle-Nephew Duo Detained
>
> In a shocking incident from Sunabeda area of Koraput district, a transgender temple priest named Shankar Randhi, residing in Paraja Sahi of Kaki village, was allegedly beaten to death on Sunday evening. The accused, identified as Deb Badaligudia and his uncle Lakshmidhar Nayak, are said to have attacked Shankar fatally near his residence.
> 
> According to preliminary reports, Shankar served as a priest at a local temple. The accused duo has been detained, and police are interrogating them under murder charges. Law enforcement authorities suspect past enmity might have been the motive behind the attack, though the investigation is ongoing.
> 
> The incident has raised serious questions about safety and discrimination faced by transgender individuals, even within sacred spaces like temples. Police have initiated a full investigation at the site of the crime.
