> Summary of this article: A 30-year-old transgender woman was stabbed to death at Posto Cinquentão, in Uberaba , on Monday night (6). The victim, after being attacked, tried to seek help and ran towards her acquaintances, but did not survive. The crime was registered by the Military Police , who are investigating the case. The body was taken to the Forensic Medical Institute and the Civil Police expert carried out the necessary procedures at the scene.
> 
> A 30-year-old transgender woman was murdered at Posto Cinquentão, located in the Distrito Industrial III neighborhood, in Uberaba. The crime occurred on Monday night (6) and the victim was stabbed.
> 
> Military Police (PM) reported that the woman had a deep wound in her chest. SAMU teams were called, but found the victim already dead.
> 
> Witnesses reported that after being attacked, the woman ran for help, bleeding. She fell to the ground, and several people gathered at the scene. Information gathered by the police indicates that the woman had been to a barbecue restaurant before the attack.
> 
> After the crime, two trucks were seen leaving the gas station, raising questions about security at the location. A Civil Police forensic expert performed the necessary investigations, and the body was sent to the Forensic Medical Institute (IML).
> 
> This case highlights the importance of safety and protecting the rights of LGBTI+ people in Minas Gerais . The community and authorities must unite to combat violence and seek justice.




> The Civil Police are investigating the death of a 30-year-old transgender woman, identified as Dandara, who was stabbed in the left side of her chest on Monday night (6), at a gas station located in the Distrito Industrial III neighborhood, in Uberaba.
> 
> Preliminary information indicates that the woman had been at a nearby barbecue restaurant until shortly before the attack. The Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) was called, but upon arrival, they could only confirm the victim's death.
> 
> After being injured, the victim tried to run for help, but fell near other women who were there. Several people then gathered at the gas station.
> 
> The Civil Police's Forensic Department carried out technical procedures at the scene and the body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML) in Uberaba.
> 
> The case remains under investigation. So far, no suspects have been arrested, and the motive for the crime has not yet been confirmed.
