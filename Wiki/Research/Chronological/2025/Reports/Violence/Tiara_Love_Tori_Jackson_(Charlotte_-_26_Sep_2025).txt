> Trans Woman Gunned Down at Low-Budget Charlotte Motel — Suspect Identified and Charged
> Published on September 27, 2025
> 
> A trans woman was tragically shot and killed Friday morning at, WoodSpring Suites, a low-budget motel in Charlotte, according to the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Police Department (CMPD).
> 
> On Friday, officers with CMPD responded to a call reporting an assault with a deadly weapon with injury at around 9:21 a.m. in the 1600 block of Deboer Avenue.
> 
> Upon arrival, they found 37-year-old Tiara Love Tori Jackson, a member of Charlotte’s LGBTQ+ community, dead at the scene.
> 
> As CMPD’s Homicide Unit took over the investigation, officers quickly identified a suspect — 33-year-old Darris Dee Marshall. Just after Jackson’s body was found, Westover Division officers were called to a separate shooting where Marshall was located with a non-life-threatening gunshot wound.
> 
> He was taken to a local hospital, treated, and then interviewed by detectives at CMPD’s Law Enforcement Center (LEC).
> 
> Based on that interview and gathered evidence, Marshall was formally charged with first-degree murder and possession of a firearm by a felon.
> 
> Darris Dee Marshall, 33, is charged with first-degree murder and possession of a firearm by a felon after allegedly killing trans woman Tiara Jackson in Charlotte.
> 
> Following his release from medical care, Marshall was transferred into the custody of the Mecklenburg County Sheriff’s Office, where he is currently being held without bond.
> 
> Marshall is scheduled to appear in court on Monday, September 29. No motive has been publicly released, and investigators have not confirmed the relationship, if any, between Jackson and Marshall.
