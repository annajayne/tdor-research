> Trans woman is executed with five shots to the head when leaving a bar to get a blouse at home, in CIC
> The criminals called her name and, upon realizing they were armed, the victim tried to run.
> By Editorial Staff on September 21, 2025 at 8:33 am .
> A trans woman was executed with at least five shots to the head, on Saturday night (20), on Rua Leony Medeiros Guimarães, in Vila Sabará, in the Industrial City of Curitiba (CIC). The crime took place around 8:30 pm.
> 
> According to witnesses, Rayane was at a bar with friends when she decided to go home to get a sweater because she was cold. On the way, when she reached the street corner, she was surprised by two men on a motorcycle .
> 
> The criminals called her name, and upon realizing they were armed, the victim tried to run. However, she was caught and shot dead. This information comes from Ric RECORD reporter Kainan Lucas.
> 
> Rescuers were at the scene to provide assistance to the woman identified as Rayane, but she did not survive.
> 
> Local residents reported that Rayane was known in the neighborhood and had no ongoing disputes. The motive and perpetrator of the crime remain unknown.
> 
> After the murder, the criminals fled. The case is being investigated by the Civil Police.
