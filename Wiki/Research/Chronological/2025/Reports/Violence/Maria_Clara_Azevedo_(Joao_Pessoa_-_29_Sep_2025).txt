> The head of the Institute of Legal Medicine (IML) of João Pessoa, Flávio Fabres, confirmed this Tuesday (30) that the death of the trans woman found in a residence in the capital presents clear signs of brutal violence and an attempt to defend the victim, during an interview with the program Ô Paraíba Boa, hosted by communicator Fabiano Gomes.
> 
> According to Fabres, a full forensic autopsy revealed six facial wounds, a cervical injury, and a cut on the right hand. The latter, he says, is characteristic of a defensive wound, indicating the victim attempted to fight back.
> 
> "The body was in an advanced state of decomposition. After a complete forensic autopsy, facial, cervical, and right hand injuries (defense injuries) were identified, resulting from a sharp object. Further tests have been requested. The medical report will be ready within the ten-day legal deadline," stated the head of the IML.
> 
> The body was released for funeral arrangements. Confirmation of multiple injuries and signs of a struggle reinforces the suspicion of a violent crime, which had already been suggested by remains found at the victim's home.
> 
> The Paraíba Civil Police (PCPB) is monitoring the case and should use the detailed IML report as a central piece in the investigation to identify the perpetrator and circumstances of the crime.



> Former Miss Paraíba Trans was killed with stab wounds to the neck and face
> September 30, 2025
> 
> The cause of death of Maria Clara Azevedo, 32, a former Miss Paraíba Trans, was six stab wounds to the face, neck, cervical region, and right hand (a self-defense wound). One of the wounds was deep and hit a blood vessel in the neck. The information was released this morning after a full forensic autopsy was performed by the João Pessoa Institute of Forensic Medicine.
> 
> Maria Clara was found dead late yesterday morning at her home in the Trincheiras neighborhood, central João Pessoa. She had last been seen on Friday the 26th, and because of her absence, friends went looking for her. There, they found the front door open and her body, in an advanced state of decomposition, wrapped in sheets.
> 
> Maria Clara was elected Miss Trans Paraíba in 2017 and 2018 and also won the title of Miss Trans Juripiranga. She was also a dancer with the Lageiro Seco Quadrilha.




> Miss Trans Paraíba is found dead inside her home in João Pessoa
> Maria Clara Azevedo was Miss Trans Paraíba 2017 and 2018, as well as Miss Trans Juripiranga and Miss Trans Pérola. She was also a dancer with the Lageiro Seco June square dance competition in João Pessoa.
> By Lucas Isidio Published in:September 29, 2025, at 5:50 PM
> 
> Miss Trans Paraíba of the 2017 and 2018 editions, Maria Clara Azevedo, was found dead inside her home on Rua das Trincheiras, in João Pessoa. Her body was found in a state of decomposition on the afternoon of this Monday (29).
> 
> Friends noticed Maria Clara 's absence in recent days and went to her house on Rua das Trincheiras. They found the gate open and Maria Clara wrapped in sheets.
> 
> Friends mourned Maria Clara's death. "I can't believe it. She was a beautiful person in every sense of the word and didn't deserve this sad end to her life. May justice be served and may the police investigate this crime quickly and clearly. When one of us leaves, we also take a piece of those who remain," commented Miss Nordeste Gay host Karina Espínola.
> 
> Maria Clara Azevedo was Miss Trans Paraíba 2017 and 2018, as well as Miss Trans Juripiranga and Miss Trans Pérola. She was also a dancer with the Lageiro Seco June square dance competition in João Pessoa.
