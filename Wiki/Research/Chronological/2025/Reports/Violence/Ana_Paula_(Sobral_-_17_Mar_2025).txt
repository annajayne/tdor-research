> Trans woman found dead in Sobral; police investigate the case
> 
> A trans woman was found dead on Monday afternoon (17) in a property on Rua Coronel Adeodato, in the center of Sobral, near the municipal cemetery. According to preliminary information, the place operated as a bar. The victim had head injuries and was covered in blood. The Military Police were called around 3:30 pm and isolated the area until the arrival of the Civil Police and Forensic Experts.
> 
> According to the police, there were blood stains in some rooms of the property, suggesting that the body may have been dragged. Investigators are seeking more information to clarify the case and identify suspects. The victim's identity has not yet been released. The body will be sent for forensic examination, and the case will remain under investigation by the Sobral Civil Police.



> A trans woman, whose identity has not yet been revealed, was brutally murdered inside her own home, located in the center of Sobral, on the afternoon of this Monday (17).
> 
> The victim was allegedly killed by a man who broke into her residence and stabbed her with a sharp object, possibly a piece of glass.
> 
> The victim's body was sent to the Forensic Expertise of the State of Ceará (Pefoce).
> 
> So far, no one has been arrested.
>

> TRANS WOMAN FOUND DEAD INSIDE APARTMENT IN DOWNTOWN SOBRAL/CE.
> 
> A trans woman identified as Verissimo Paula Alves, known as Ana Paula, 57 years old, was found dead on Monday afternoon (17) in her apartment in the central market of Sobral/CE.
> 
> According to information from family members, the victim's body had a cut on the head probably caused by bottle caps, given that there were also bottle caps.
> 
> Ana Paula was a well-known trans woman in Sobral and worked in the beverage trade in Sobral. The civil police opened a police investigation to investigate the crime.
