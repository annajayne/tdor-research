> Delhi police have arrested two persons in connection with the murder of a transgender person whose body was found with a slit throat in Delhi’s Madhu Vihar area on Monday, officials said.
> 
> According to officials, a PCR call was received regarding the stabbing of a transgender person on Monday afternoon. Upon reaching the spot, police found the victim’s body in a pool of blood with a slit throat.
> 
> The deceased was identified as Karan alias Annu, a resident of Khichripur who was currently residing in Village Chilla.
> 
> During sustained interrogation, it was revealed that Rehan alias Ikka (19), a resident of MCD School, Ghazipur Village, Delhi, was in a live-in relationship with the victim for the past four months. Over time, disputes arose as Rehan allegedly kept extorting money from Karan. When Karan began to distance herself, Rehan grew possessive and saw the move as betrayal, police said.
> 
> Enraged, Rehan conspired with his associate, Md Sarver (20), a resident of Rajeev Colony, Yadav Chowk, Khoda Colony, Ghaziabad, UP, to eliminate the victim, they said.
> 
> Officials said that multiple raids were conducted in the Trans-Yamuna area, as well as Ghaziabad and Meerut, to track down the suspects.
> 
> Acting on specific intelligence, a police team reached the Laxmi Nagar Metro Station area around midnight. They conducted surveillance in the adjoining parking zone and under the Shakarpur flyover near Kishan Kunj/SDM Office.
> 
> They said that at around 12:45 AM, two suspects matching the given descriptions arrived on foot and stood under the flyover, seemingly waiting for someone. After confirmation by the informer, the police team apprehended both suspects at approximately 1:00 AM.
> 
> During interrogation, both suspects confessed to their involvement in the murder of Karan alias Annu earlier the same day, they said.
> We are further looking into the case, they added.



> Transperson found murdered in Patparganj; Cops question youth handed over by locals
> 
> 
> A 25-year-old transgender person was allegedly murdered under a bridge near Telco T-point in east Delhi's Patparganj on Monday. The victim, identified as Karan alias Annu, had reportedly suffered a deep stab wound to the neck, and was declared dead at the scene.According to police, a PCR call was received around 1.32 pm at Pandav Nagar police station, informing that a boy had been apprehended by locals after allegedly stabbing a person. "Since the crime location fell under the jurisdiction of Madhu Vihar, the SHO and police team from Madhu Vihar police station rushed to the spot, located beneath the bridge towards Hasanpur," a senior police officer said.
> 
> "Upon arrival, the officers discovered the body of the victim lying in a pool of blood with a slit throat.
> 
> The victim—a transgender— was identified as Karan alias Annu, a resident of Khichripur who had been living in Chilla village," the senior officer added. Locals had caught hold of a 19-year-old boy at the scene and handed him over to the police. During initial questioning, the youth alleged that Annu's ex-husband, Rehan, a resident of Ghazipur, had planned and executed the murder. Police said the boy is being interrogated further to determine his exact role in the crime.
> 
> Whether he was directly involved in the murder or was present as a witness remains to be established, said police. A case under Section 103 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), which pertains to murder, has been registered at Madhu Vihar police station. The body was sent for post-mortem examination, and police teams are now working to trace Rehan and verify the sequence of events that led to the killing.Further investigation is underway.
