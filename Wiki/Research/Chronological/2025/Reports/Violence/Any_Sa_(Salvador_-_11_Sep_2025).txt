> A 29-year-old trans woman was shot dead on Thursday afternoon, the 11th, on Rua Almeida Brandão, known as Escada, in the Itacaranha neighborhood, Subúrbio Ferroviário de Salvador .
> 
> The victim was identified as Jefferson Sampaio Neri, who also used the social name Any Sá. She was shot multiple times but succumbed to her injuries and died at the scene. The perpetrator and motive remain unknown.
> 
> According to the Military Police, agents from the 14th CIPM responded to the incident and called the Technical Police Department (DPT) to carry out the investigation.
> 
> The Civil Police reported that it has issued expert and autopsy guides and is already carrying out investigations to identify those responsible for the crime




> A trans woman, identified as Jefferson Sampaio Neri, was shot to death in the Itacaranha neighborhood of Salvador, Bahia. The crime occurred in the afternoon, near the VLT construction site.
