> Mulher trans é executada a tiros após invasão de casa em Belo Jardim
> 
> Criminoso arrombou a residence, soprendeu a victima e fugiu sem dechir pistas
> Gabielly, 25 years old, was shot dead in his own home, in the neighborhood of Santo Antônio, in Belo Jardim, in Agreste de Pernambuco. The crime happened na manhã desta quinta-feira (2) and left the vizinhança em shock pela forma como foi commeto.
> 
> The victim, registered as Marlon Israel Souza da Silva, but using the social name Gabielly, was surprised when a homem broke into the door of the residence. With no chance of reaction, the victim was hit by several gunshots while he was in the living room.
> 
> O autor do crime fugiu apos a execución e até o momento não foi localização. The Military Police was not local and isolated the area until the arrival of the Instituto de Criminalística, responsible for the investigation. The body was removed and taken to the Instituto de Medicina Legal (IML) de Caruaru.
> 
> The Civil Police assumed the investigations and worked to discover who was responsible for the homicide and what would have been the motivation for the invasion followed by the execution.





> Trans woman murdered in Belo Jardim; two men are the main suspects
> According to reports, the victim shared a house that was frequented by drug users and was alone when everything happened.
> 
> A trans woman was murdered on Thursday afternoon (2), inside her home in the Santo Antônio neighborhood, in Belo Jardim.
> 
> According to reports, the victim shared a house that was frequented by drug users and was alone in the residence when everything happened.
> 
> Two men arrived on a motorcycle, and one of them went to the woman's door, calling out someone else's name. When she opened the door to understand what was happening, she was brutally shot.
> 
> Both military and civil police were on site to conduct investigations. The woman had previously been the victim of a stabbing attempt, but she survived.
> 
> "We have already collected some witness statements and we are looking for other elements to determine the perpetrator of this crime that occurred," said delegate Fabrício Pimentel.
> 
> The body remains at the IML in Caruaru and tomorrow it should be released for a wake in Belo Jardim.



> A transgender woman is shot dead inside her home in Belo Jardim; Pernambuco has now recorded 8 murders of trans people.
> The crime occurred on the morning of last Thursday (2) and is being investigated by the Civil Police; the state is among the most lethal for the trans population in Brazil.
> 
> last update October 3, 2025
> 
> The city of Belo Jardim, in the Pernambuco Agreste region, was the scene of yet another crime with characteristics of transphobia. A transgender woman was shot dead inside her own home on the morning of last Thursday (2). The victim's identity was not released.
> 
> According to information provided by the Pernambuco Civil Police, an investigation has already been launched into the case. The force is seeking to determine the perpetrator and motive for the homicide. So far, no details have been released about possible suspects or the circumstances surrounding the murder.
> 
> The case comes amid an alarming scenario of violence against trans people in Brazil. Data released by the National Association of Transvestites and Transsexuals (Antra) indicates that 122 trans people have been murdered in the country. Among these victims, 117 were transvestites or transgender women.
> 
> Pernambuco ranks fifth nationally in murders targeting this vulnerable population, with eight deaths recorded so far—a number equivalent to the cases recorded in Bahia and Mato Grosso. These statistics highlight a worrying scenario of insecurity and vulnerability faced by trans people in the state.
> 

> The Civil Police continue with the investigations and reinforce the importance of the public's collaboration with information that can help identify those involved in the crime.
