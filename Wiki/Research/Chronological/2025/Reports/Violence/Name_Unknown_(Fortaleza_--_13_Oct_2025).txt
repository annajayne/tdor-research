> Transvestite killed with wooden blows and body found half-naked
> Residents reported that the place is dark and often used for sexual activities during the early hours of the morning.
> Updated on October 13, 2025
> 
> A transvestite was killed with extreme cruelty in the early hours of this Monday (13), in the Montese neighborhood, in Fortaleza. The victim was murdered with blows from wood and found half-naked during the morning, in an area known for being frequented by drug users and sex workers.
> 
> According to the Military Police, the body was found in a vacant lot on Rua João Melo, near Avenida João Pessoa. A piece of wood stained with blood, possibly used in the crime, was found next to the victim.
> 
> Residents reported that the location is dark and often used for sexual encounters during the early hours of the morning. The body was discovered by a worker passing by around 6 a.m. "I thought she was injured and in need of help, but when I got closer, I saw that she was already dead," said a witness.
> 
> The Civil Police, through the Homicide and Personal Protection Department (DHPP), began the investigation. Experts collected materials found at the scene, including condoms, torn clothing, and traces of drugs.
> 
> The victim's identity has not yet been confirmed. So far, there is no information about suspects or the motive for the crime.
> 
> The Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS) reported that the Civil Police of the State of Ceará (PCCE) is investigating the circumstances of a homicide registered this Monday (13). According to initial information, the victim, not yet formally identified, was found lifeless and with signs of violence.
> 
> Teams from the Ceará Military Police (PMCE), the Ceará State Forensic Science Office (PEFOCE), and the PCCE's Homicide and Personal Protection Department (DHPP) were called to the scene. The DHPP's 5th Precinct is in charge of the investigation, aiming to clarify the information surrounding the case and identify the perpetrator.




> Body with signs of violence found on public road in Fortaleza
> Ceará Civil Police are investigating a homicide reported Monday, the 13th, in the Demócrito Rocha neighborhood. The victim had signs of violence on her head.
> 16:27 | Oct. 13, 2025
>   
> A body showing signs of violence was found on Monday morning, the 13th, on a public street in the Demócrito Rocha neighborhood of Fortaleza . The Ceará Civil Police (PC-CE) is investigating the case as homicide. The victim has not yet been formally identified.
> 
> Images circulating on social media show the victim with marks of violence, mainly on the head. It is not yet clear whether the injuries were caused by gunshots or other weapons.
> 
> Preliminary information indicated that the victim was a transvestite, but this information has not yet been officially confirmed. At the scene, the body was found wearing only underwear.
> 
> At the scene, teams from the Ceará Military Police (PMCE) and the Forensic Science Department (PEFOCE) gathered information that will aid in the investigation. As of this writing, no suspects have been arrested.
> 
> In a statement, the Secretariat of Public Security and Social Defense (SSPDS) reported that the Civil Police of Ceará (PC-CE) is investigating the case through the 5th Police Station of the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP).
> 
> Work continues with the aim of clarifying information about the case and identifying the perpetrator of the crime.
