> Firefighters search for drowning victim in Piracicaba River
> February 27, 2025
> in Piracicaba - RMP
> The Piracicaba (SP) Fire Department is searching, this Thursday (27), for the body of a woman who is transgender, according to acquaintances of hers, and who would have fallen into the Piracicaba River this Wednesday (25) when she went to cool off.
>
> This Thursday morning, two acquaintances of the victim were watching the work firefighters, who were diving near the Deck where the boat trips depart. It was nearby that the victim disappeared.
>
> According to her friends, the victim lives alone in the city of Rio das Pedras (SP). They said they hope their friend is alive. The search continues this afternoon.




> Firefighters search for transvestite who jumped into the Piracicaba River
> by Junior Cardoso
> February 26, 2025
> in Piracicaba (SP) , Security, Society and Well-Being
> 
> It is now 6:50 pm. PIRANOT reports that the Fire Department is currently searching for a transvestite who jumped and drowned in the Piracicaba River, near the boat pier.
> 
> According to initial reports, the transvestite's friend said they were smoking a marijuana cigarette on the banks of the river, on Rua do Porto, when she got up and jumped into the water.
> 
> Firefighters were called and searched for her, but the work must be completed within the next few minutes due to nightfall.
