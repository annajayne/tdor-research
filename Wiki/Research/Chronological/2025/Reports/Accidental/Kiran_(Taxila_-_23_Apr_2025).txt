====== Kiran (Taxila - 23 Apr 2025) ======

Kiran [Ahsan]
23 Apr 2025
Taxila
Pakistan
electrocuted


> TAXILA: Two persons were killed in different incidents in the jurisdiction of Taxila and Hazro police stations on Wednesday, police sources said.
> 
> In the first incident, a transgender person died of electrocution in the limits of Taxila police station.
> 
> According to police sources, Ahsan alias Kiran was standing at a bus terminal when he accidentally stepped on the live wire and suffered a fatal electric shock, and died on the spot. His body was shifted to Tehsil Headquarters Hospital for autopsy.
> 
> Separately, a man gunned down his nephew over a family dispute in Mullah Kalan village in the limits of Hazro police station.
> 
> Police sources said the man married the daughter of his uncle and moved to Swabi, however, the relationship between the couple became strained, after which the girl returned to her parents’ house.
> 
> On Wednesday, when the man came to his in-laws’ house to take his wife back, his uncle, a hotel owner, after a heated exchange, took out his pistol and shot him dead and fled away. Respective police stations registered cases and launched further investigations.
> 
> Published in Dawn, April 24th, 2025
