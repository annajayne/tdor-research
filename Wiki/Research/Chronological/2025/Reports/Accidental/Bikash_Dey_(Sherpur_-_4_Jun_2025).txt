> Transgender among 3 killed as truck rams easy bike in Sherpur
> Published : Thursday, 5 June, 2025 at 9:53 AM  Count : 336
> Observer Online Report
> Submit   
> Three passengers, including a transgender, were killed after a truck smashed their easy bike in the Mobarakpur Mahishaband area of Sreebardi-Longaorpara road in Sherpur on Wednesday.
> 
> The accident occurred around 7:30PM when the three were heading towards Sherpur town from Sreebardiriding on the easy bike.
> 
> The deceased were identified as Golam Hossain Imran, 22, son of former UP member Ilias Hossain from Yoginimura Namapara in Sherpur Sadar, Al-Amin, 17, son of Billal Hossain; and transgender Bikash Dey, 40, son of Dhiren Dey from Singpara in the town.
> 
> Golam Hossain Imran and Al-Amin died on the spot, while Bikash Dey was taken to Sherpur District Sadar Hospital, where he was declared dead by the doctors, said Zubaidul Islam, officer in-charge (OC) OF Sherpur Sadar Police Station.
