> Gabriella is survived by her loving parents, Lorri and James Savarese; her two older sisters, Leigha and Angelina; her younger sister, Ariana; and her maternal grandparents Helen and Robert Davidson. She also leaves behind an extended family of aunts, uncles, and cousins, along with countless friends and members of the community who were blessed to have known her. Gabriella has now joined her beloved paternal grandparents, Michael and Catherine Savarese, in heaven, where their love will surround her eternally.

Beyond the stage, Gabriella had a profound love for reading, art and children. She was an avid fan of horror movies and Marvel, with a special affection for Spider-Man, often drawing inspiration from these stories in her own creative endeavors.
