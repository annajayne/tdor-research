> Lily-Dawn Harkins "Chi/L Nguyen" (She/Her) was a transgender woman who recently graduated with her degree in supply chain management. Recognized as a genius for her IQ of over 140, she loved Magic the Gathering, cooking (and burning) food for her loved ones, volunteering for local organizations, and giving back to others in any way she could. She was fluent in German, and known for her absurdist humor and infectious smile and laugh. She passed by suicide on June 22nd, 2025, in Colorado.
