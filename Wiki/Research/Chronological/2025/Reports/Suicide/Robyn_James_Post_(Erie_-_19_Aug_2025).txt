> Robyn James Post, 48, of North East, passed away on Tuesday August 19, 2025 in Ohio.
> 
> He was born on August 5, 1977 in Erie, Pennsylvania to Gary and Patricia (Vance) Post.
> 
> Robyn, "Bird", graduated from North East High School and Mercyhurst University earning a Liberal Arts Degree in Graphic Design. He also obtain his LPN Degree and RN Degree in Nursing from Cuyahoga Community College in Ohio.
> 
> He began his nursing career at the Cleveland Clinic and was a traveling nurse in Tucson Arizona. Robyn was currently employed at the Erie County Health Department as a public health nurse.
> 
> Robyn loved his "fur babies" Maddy and Bosley and will always be remembered for his wonderful sense of humor, adventurous spirit, and kindness towards others.
> 
> His close friends which nicknamed him Buzz were very important to him. Throughout life Robyn enjoyed art culture especially Dia De Los Muertos; working out at the gym, productive yard work, traveling, studying keyboard and drums, and living life to the fullest.
> 
> Robyn is survived by his parents; brother, Randy L. Post (Carolyn); niece, Samantha Post; nephews, Alex and Caleb Post; several aunts, uncles and cousins; and his 18 year old cat, Maddy.
> 
> Private funeral arrangements have been entrusted to the William D. Elkin Funeral Home, 65 South Lake Street, North East. Memorials may be made to NAMI, 1611 Peach Street, Suite 218, Erie, PA 16501. A Celebration of Life will be announced at a later date. Please send condolences to elkinfh.com.
