> Derek Moody (he/him), 23, died by suicide, on August 13, 2025 at his apartment in Red Bay, Alabama. Derek was an active participant with the TRANS Program, who considered him family. He participated in weekly TRANS Tuesday Support Calls and made an immensely positive impact on many transgender and non-binary lives, in Alabama, Mississippi and beyond. Derek will be remembered for his brilliant mind, concern and kindness for others, passion for music production and for being the worlds greatest frog enthusiast.
