> Social activist Hannah Fernanda Cervantes died in Puebla
> November 4, 2025 | 5:44 PM CST
> LGBT+ activist Hannah Fernanda Cervantes left a farewell message on social media.
> 
> Hannah Fernanda Cervantes, Social Activist and Fighter in the LGBT Community of Puebla, has died
> The activist was a candidate for city councilor in Cholula, Puebla.
> 
> 
> 
> On Tuesday, November 4, the death of an activist woman , defender of the rights of the LGBT+ community , was confirmed ; Hannah Fernanda Cervantes lost her life in recent hours in Puebla .
> 
> The above was confirmed by the Government of the State of Puebla , as well as various political figures who worked closely with Hanna Cervantes , who recognized her work and issued a message to honor her death.
> 
> Hanna Fernanda Cervantes was a candidate for City Councilor in San Andrés Cholula , Puebla. She studied Public Administration at the Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla ( BUAP ).
> 
> She was a social activist who described herself as a hardworking, loyal, humble, noble, courageous, and committed woman.
> 
> On her personal social media accounts, she posted a story with a farewell message , where she affirms that she always fought for her ideal, and looked out for others before her own personal well-being.
> 
> In her post, she referred to her mother as her only loved one, hoping that nothing happens to her and asking her loved ones to take care of her for her sake.
> 
> Many people transcend, some leave a mark, others leave a path full of courage, joy, and teaching.
> 
> The cause of Hanna Fernanda 's death has not yet been revealed ; family, friends, and fellow activists have spoken out about her passing as a regrettable loss.
