It is unfortunate to hear the news of a young and beautiful LGBT celebrity who has achieved success in the art world.

On September 15, 2019, May Thitsar Maung realized her dream of becoming a beautiful woman. She underwent transgender surgery. May Thitsar Maung was one of 13 nominees for Miss International Queen Best Talent 2020.

Miss May Thitsar Maung died in a car accident near the American Center on the night of June 7, when the Probox taxi taken by May Thitsar Maung and her friends was hit by a traffic light at the corner of University Avenue and Inya Road.

The taxi driver and his wife and four friends, including May Thitsar Maung, were riding in the taxi. The taxi driver and his wife, May Thitsar Maung, died and the other three injured were taken to North Okkalapa Hospital. The crash is being assisted by the Non-Formal Aid Society

According to his friend, May Thitsar Maung went out to apologize for renting a taxi because his puppy was giving birth while he was out. May Thitsar Maung pray for a good life.
