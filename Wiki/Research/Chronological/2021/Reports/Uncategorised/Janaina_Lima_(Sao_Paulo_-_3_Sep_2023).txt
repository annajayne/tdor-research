> On September 03, 2021 we lost Janaína Lima, one of our country’s greatest trans activists.
> From Rio Grande do Norte, registered in Paraiba, arrived in São Paulo as a child. Transvesti, pedagogue and sex professional, Janaína was proud of herself, she was the first transvesti president of the Municipal Council for Attention to Sexual Diversity in São Paulo.
> Janaína left us a legacy of achievements and was part of the Rede Trans Brazil family.
