The body of Jerry Whitewater, in their late 20s or early 30s, laying face down in a pool of blood, and with serious head injuries, was found beneath a viaduct at 7th Street over the Kansas City, Kan., railroad yards . They had bled to death.

Police stated that body, dressed as a woman, was far enough beneath the viaduct that they could not have fallen from it, or from a passing train.
