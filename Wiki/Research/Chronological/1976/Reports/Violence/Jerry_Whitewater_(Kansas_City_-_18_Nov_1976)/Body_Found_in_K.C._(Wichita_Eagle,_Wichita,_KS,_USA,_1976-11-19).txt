===== Body Found in K.C. =====

KANSAS CITY Kan (AP) — The body of a man dressed in women's clothing was found by police Thursday lying face down in a pool of blood beneath a viaduct over the Kansas City, Kan., railroad yards.

Through fingerprints police Identified the victim as Jerry Whitewater in his late 20s or early 30s. There was no identification on Whitewater when he was found.

Police said the victim was wearing blue jeans, a plaid shirt, a brown cloth coat with an imitation fur collar and tan women's shoes. He was wearing facial makeup and wore rings on his fingers.

Police said Whitewater had serious head injuries and apparently had bled to death.

Police said Whitewater was found under 7th Street Viaduct and the body was far enough under the crossing that he could not have fallen off the bridge. Authorities also said the victim had not fallen from a passing train.
