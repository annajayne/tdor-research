===== Slain Man Identified As Poser =====

A female impersonator whose body was found early Sunday in northwest Atlanta was identified Monday as James Earl Williams, 31, of 5 Houston St., Room 104.

Police said the man recently moved here from Strawberry, Tenn.

His body was found lying' face down in a gravel driveway at 255 Ashby St NW at 6:30 a.m. Sunday. Investigators believe he was shot two hours earlier.

Williams was known to walk Hunter Street, seven or eight blocks from where his body was found, as a female impersonator, police said.

There were no suspects in the slaying Monday night.
