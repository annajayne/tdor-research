====== Knifing Suspect Admits Guilt (The Indianapolis Star, Indianapolois, IN, 1968-08-14) ======
Created Sunday 23 May 2021

Knifing Suspect Admits Guilt

Raymond A. Craig Jr., 25 years old, 2005 Ruckle Street, yesterday pleaded guilty, to manslaughter In Criminal Court, Division 1.

Craig's Dlea came after a jury had heard one day of evidence in the state's attempt to convict Craig for first degree murder in the fatal knifing of Freddie B. Jordan Jr., 26, 2029 North College Avenue, on Dec. 25. 1964.

Judge John T. Davis set 1:30 p.m. Aug. 22 for sentencing.
