====== Anthony Stephans Jr (Lackawanna - 8 Mar 1964) ======

After a night of drinking with companions Johnny P. Fields, 23, and Terrance Wilson, 29, in Lackawanna, New York, on the evening of 8 March, 1964, Anthony Stephans Jr, 22, dressed as a woman, was found dead, stabbed once in the neck, after having walked off with a strange man who had approached while Stephans and Wilson were waiting for Fields to bring their car around.

Fields and Wilson were charged with masquerading in women's clothing and were held as material witnesses.
