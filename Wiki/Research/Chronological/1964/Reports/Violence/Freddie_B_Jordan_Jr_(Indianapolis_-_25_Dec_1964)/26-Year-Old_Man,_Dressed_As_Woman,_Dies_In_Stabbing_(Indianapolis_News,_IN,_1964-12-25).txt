===== 26-Year-Old Man, Dressed As Woman, Dies In Stabbing =====

A 26-year-old man died en route to General Hospital today after police found him bleeding from stab wounds in the stomach and chest in the hallway of a near-Northside apartment.

Police said the victim, Freddie B. Jordan Jr., 26, 2029 N. College, was dressed in women's clothing and was wearing makeup. He was known as a female impersonator.

Police went to the apartment at 1:12 a.m. James Lee Green, 19, who shares the apartment with Jordan, said two men came to the apartment and argued with Jordan.

Police found a straight razor and a woman's purse containing $17.30. Wigs and dresses were found in the apartment
