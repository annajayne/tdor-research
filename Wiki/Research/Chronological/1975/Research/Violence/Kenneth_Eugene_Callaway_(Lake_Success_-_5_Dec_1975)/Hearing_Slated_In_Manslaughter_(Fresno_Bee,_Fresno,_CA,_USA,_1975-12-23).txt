===== Hearing Slated In Manslaughter =====

PORTERVILLE — A preliminary hearing has been set for Dec. 31 at 10 a.m. in Municipal Court for Manuel Carrasco Jr. 20 who was arraigned on a charge of manslaughter stemming from the death of Kenneth Eugene Callaway, 64, of Camp Nelson on Dec. 5 at Lake Success.

Carrasco was also charged with the theft of Callaway's pickup that was found in the Gorman area.

Callaway had been found beaten to death. He was dressed in women's clothes.
