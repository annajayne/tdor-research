===== Body Found at Lake Success =====

PORTERVILLE (UPI) — Tulare County sheriffs investigators said today the body found near Lake Success before dawn was a man dressed in women's clothing.

Deputies at first reported it was the body of a woman but they said further investigation revealed it was a male dressed in womens evening clothes.

The body was discovered by two campers in the parking lot at Vista Point near the lake which is east of Porterville.

Officers said the body was dressed in an evening gown and womans cape. Investigators said the victim carried no identification.

The body appeared to be badly beaten and deputies said it appeared to be a case of homicide but that an autopsy would be held to determine the exact cause of death.

Authorities said the victim was about 40 years old. The body was discovered about 3:15 a.m.
