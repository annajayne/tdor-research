On Dec. 5, 1975, Kenneth E. Callaway, 64, of Camp Nelson, California, was brutally beaten to death with a shovel in the parking lot at Vista Point, Lake Success, California. 

The bloody body was found lying near the south wall of Vista Point dressed in a long black evening gown and wearing a blonde wig. A black and white checkered cape, two bracelets, and earrings were found nearby. Police believed the items were torn from the victim's body.

Manuel Carrasco Jr., 20, of Porterville, was arrested on Dec. 15 and charged with manslaughter as well as the theft of Callaway's pickup truck.
