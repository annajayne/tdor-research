On New Years Eve, 1975, transgender women Claude Jean, 22, and Robert Morin, 22, were stabbed to death in an apartment at 7595 Viau Boulevard in St. Leonard, Montreal.

One of the victims telephoned a neighbor for help, but when police arrived both women were dead.
