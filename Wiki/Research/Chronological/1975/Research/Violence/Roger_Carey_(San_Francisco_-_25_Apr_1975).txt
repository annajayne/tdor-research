Transgender woman Roger Carey, 42, was stabbed to death in their room in their room at 180 Turk St., San Fransciso, on 25 April, 1975. Their killing was one of multiple related knife murders of transgender women in their rooms that happened in 1974 and 1975 in the Tenderloin District of San Francisco.

On Apr. 30, 1975, Raushee Onez, 32, was arrested and charged with the murder of Roger Carey, and in the murder of Francis Nelson, 76, on Nov. 11, 1974.
