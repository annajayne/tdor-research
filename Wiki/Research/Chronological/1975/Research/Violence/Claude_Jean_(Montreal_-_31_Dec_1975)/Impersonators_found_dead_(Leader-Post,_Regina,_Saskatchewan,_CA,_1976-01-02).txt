===== Impersonators found dead =====

MONTREAL (CP) — Two young men, described by police as female impersonators, were found stabbed to death late New Year's Eve in an east-end Montreal apartment.

Police said one of the men telephoned a neighbor, asking that police be called. Both were dead when police arrived.

The victims were initially identified as young women, but police later Identified one of the victims as Claude Jean, 22, and said they were trying.to identify the other man.

Their deaths brought the number of homicides in the Montreal Urban Community in 1975 to 104 by police calculation.
