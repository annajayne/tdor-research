===== Scene Of Fatal Stabbing =====

A man wearing woman's clothing was stabbed to death at Herrison and Fayette streets, across from police headquarters, at 2 A.M. today. James Reid, 27, of the 1700 block Harford avenue, was pronounced dead on arrival at Mercy Hospital. Central district police were unaware of the murder until three of the victim's companions reported it.
