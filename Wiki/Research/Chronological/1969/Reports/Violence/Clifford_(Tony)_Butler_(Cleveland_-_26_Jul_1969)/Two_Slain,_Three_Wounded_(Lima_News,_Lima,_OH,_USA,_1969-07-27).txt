===== Two Slain, Three Wounded In Cleveland Shooting Spree =====

CLEVELAND (UPI) — An attempt by an armed gang to shake down an after-hours drinking spot early Saturday ended in a shooting spree that saw two killed and three wounded. Police arrested four persons as material witnesses in the incident, but released no names.

Police said that about 20 men carrying weapons surrounded the east side house where the speakeasy was located on the second floor.

Three men, armed with shotguns and a submachine gun, entered the jammed apartment, ordered 40 persons to stand against the wall and demanded money from the proprietor, Clifford (Tony) Butler, 38.

Butler, a 250-pound female impersonator, known to carry as much as $3,000 on his person, refused, and the shooting started.

Killed were Butler and a man still unidentified. A 16-year-old youth was critically wounded and two other men were also hospitalized.

Police said the believed the gang, who wore black nationalist uniforms of levis, black berets and green shirts, had tried to shake down the place, and when refused, at turned to outright robbery.
