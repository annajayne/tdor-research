===== Man in Women's Clothes Found Dead =====

NEW PHILADELPHIA, Ohio (AP)—The body of William S. Wright, 45, of New Philadelphia, was found on a street corner Thursday night. Police believed at first they had found Willie Gooch, 42, who escaped four days ago from Tuscarawas County jail.

The cause of Wright's death was not determined Immediately.

The body was clad partly in women's clothing, and the man had been bleeding slightly at the nose, police said.

Wright was employed In a Dover factory. His identity was confirmed by relatives, city police reported. Deputy Sheriff John Blake said the sheriff's office still is looking for the missing escapee.
