===== Suspects Arraigned In Common Pleas Court =====

A man was arraigned on a charge of murder Tuesday in Common Pleas Court 14 in connection with a fatal stabbing in Hartford last Saturday.

Angel L. Espinosa, 53, of 104 Edwards St., received a continuance of his case to June 30 and Judge Joseph F. Morelli set bail for his release at $100,000.

Espinosa, who surrendered to police Monday night, was charged with killing Roberto Amaro, 20, also of 104 Edwards St., after an argument.
