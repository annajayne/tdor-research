===== Man Is Slain In Argument =====

A Hartford man dressed in women's clothing was fatally stabbed Sunday evening during an argument with another man, police said.

Roberto Amaro, 20, was found in the street outside his apartment at 104 Edwards St., police said.

He was rushed to St. Francis Hospital and Medical Center, where he was pronounced dead on arrival.

Police said Amaro was stabbed once on the left side of his chest. The weapon an eight-inch kitchen knife was recovered by police.

Police were called to the scene at about 8 p.m., when a resident telephoned to say a woman was lying in the road. Police later learned the victim was male.

Hartford detectives say the fight that resulted in Amaro's death was apparently the result of a dispute with a male acquaintance. Police said they have a suspect in the slaying, but refused to release his name.
