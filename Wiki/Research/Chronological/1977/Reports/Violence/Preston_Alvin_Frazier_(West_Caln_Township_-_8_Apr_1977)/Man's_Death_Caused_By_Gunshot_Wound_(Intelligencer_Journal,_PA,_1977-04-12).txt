===== Man's Death Caused By Gunshot Wound =====

State police at Embreeville said Mon day that the body found in West Cain Township, Chester County, Sunday afternoon was identified as Preston Alvin Frazier, 28, of 752 Coates St., Coatesville.

An autopsy at Coatesville Hospital disclosed that a gunshot wound of the neck was the primary cause of death. Two horseback riders discovered the body in a rural area about five miles west of Coatesville.
