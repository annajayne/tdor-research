> Tragedy: Firefighters find the last three bodies in the rubble of a building in Janga, totaling 14 deaths
> July 8, 2023
> 
> The Fire Department found, early this Saturday afternoon (8), the last three bodies of the disappeared in the rubble of the building that collapsed in Conjunto Beira-Mar, in Janga, in Paulista. The bodies belong to a mother, Marcela, aged 40, and her two children, Maria Flor, aged six, and Wallace, aged 10. According to the Fire Department, the three were found hugging each other in a double bed.
> 
> In all, there were 21 victims, three rescued alive from the rubble, four found alive outside the building and 14 deaths. Fire Department teams are still on site, now looking for three animals that were on the top floor of the building.
>
