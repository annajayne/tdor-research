> The official profile of makeup artist and influencer Juliana Rocha brought the news of her death. The message is short and does not contain the causes of the 25-year-old professional's death.
> 
> “Note of clarification. It is with deep regret and sadness that Juliana Rocha's family informs you, her audience and people who loved her, that she has died. The funeral will be tomorrow for family and friends. Close friends, get in touch with Marllon”, says the post on Instagram Stories .
> 
> Juliana Rocha had 155 thousand followers on Instagram and more than 230 thousand on TikTok. Her last post on Instagram, for example, came more than eight weeks ago.
> 
> She was known for her makeup and fashion tips. On their social networks, fans showed all the affection they had for the digital influencer.
> 
> “Ju, appear. So making up that you died, please come out”, wrote one. “My God, I'm in complete shock, I've always loved Ju's content. May she rest in peace,” commented another.
