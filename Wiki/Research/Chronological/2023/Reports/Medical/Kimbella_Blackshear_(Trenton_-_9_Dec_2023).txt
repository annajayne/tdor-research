> Craishon Jahbari Blackshear affectionately known as Kimbella peacefully went home to be with the Lord on Saturday, December 9, 2023. He was born on December 21, 1992, in Trenton, New Jersey to Nichole Blackshear. Craishon is proceeded in death by his grandparents Shirley Ann Blackshear, Theodore Wilson, and an aunt Wanda Hunt all of Trenton, NJ.
> 
> Craishon attended the Trenton Public Schools system and graduated from Rabino Academy High School in Ewing Township, NJ in 2011. She was an entrepreneur with a passion for fashion and cosmetology. She loved doing hair, make-up and modeling for various brands. Kim was a social media influencer boasting over 160,000 followers on Instagram. Kim also had a thrill for fast cars with tinted windows and loud music. Kim was very comical and had her own language that those who knew her could only understand. She loved her siblings as is they were her own and took very good care of her mother.
>  
> She was very respectful and well liked in the entire community where she lived for the past 25 years. Kim traveled all across the world and was very well known. She was so courageous, outgoing, and very fearless. Kim had a light that would brighten any room she stepped in. Kim had a heart of gold and would do anything for anyone whom she loved.
>  
> Craishon is survived by her mother Nichole Blackshear, two brothers, Shabir Walker and Mi’Quel Blackshear of Trenton NJ, a sister Nikiyah Blackshear of Trenton NJ, and niece Gabriella Blackshear of Philadelphia, PA, six uncles, Charles, Chris, Troy and Todd Blackshear, Rodney Hunt all of Trenton NJ, and Bienvenido Blackshear of Philadelphia, PA, four aunts, Nina and Bridgette Blackshear of Trenton NJ, Nannette Blackshear of Lawrenceville NJ, and Tarah Blackshear of St. Thomas, USVI, a great-uncle, Melvin Chapman of Edgewater Park, NJ, a great-aunt, Barbara Jean Yates of Oakland, CA, Godsons, Ay’on Ewings and Jason Parker, Godsisters, Quinnesha Thomas and Anastasia Edwards, and a host of cousins and friends.
>
