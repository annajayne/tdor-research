> CIUDAD VALLES.- A transgender person with the identity of a woman died as a result of a drug overdose.
> 
> The deceased called herself Mía Yenislei C., she was 24 years old, and was staying in a hotel room located on avenida Juárez, between Madero and Porfirio Díaz streets in the downtown area. 
> 
> He was in the company of another transgender, who, when he saw that Mia was in poor health and was beginning to convulse, decided to ask for help from the 9-1-1 Emergency System. It was around 5 in the morning when the Red Cross paramedics came to help, and the person accompanying Mía admitted that he was drugged, but since he was in serious condition, he was taken to the General Hospital in an emergency.
> 
> Hours later, the hospital staff notified the State Attorney General's Office of the death of said person.
