> The trans influencer La Gaga de Torreón dies from a terrible illness
> They report the death of Roxana Martínez "La Gaga" , who was an icon of the LGBT+ community.
> December 30, 2023 · 12:07 pm
> 
> Great sadness is experienced on social networks after the sudden and unfortunate death of Roxana Hernández “La Gaga” , known in the digital world for her appearances and charisma, in addition to being a prominent representative of the trans and LGBT+ community, was announced. in general.
> 
> According to information from Telediario, the influencer originally from Torreón, Coahuila saw her health deteriorate due to a bacteria that was located in her intestines , which is why she was hospitalized in an emergency on more than one occasion.
> 
> Unfortunately, she died on the morning of Friday, December 29, and was laid to rest at her home to be buried this Saturday afternoon in the Jardines del Tiempo municipal pantheon. Her funeral was attended by family, friends and other loved ones, and she was also allowed access to her followers, to whom she was eternally grateful.
> 
> You may not know but “ La Gaga de Torreón ” or “La Gaga Lagunera”, the name by which she was known in the world of social networks, was a member of Las Chisquiadas, a group of influencers who shared comedy videos to entertain. to her loyal followers.
> 
> Her charisma and wit helped her accumulate more than 100,000 followers on TikTok , in addition to becoming a reference in the LGBT+ community . His death takes netizens by surprise, who have begun to say goodbye and remember the best videos of him.
