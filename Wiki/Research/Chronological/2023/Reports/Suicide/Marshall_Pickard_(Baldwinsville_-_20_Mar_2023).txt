Marshall Pickard, 14, died of suicide on Mar. 20, 2023 in Baldwinsville, NY. 


> Maya Marshall Pickard March 20, 2023 Maya Marshall Pickard, 14, of Baldwinsville, passed away unexpectedly on March 20, 2023.
> 
> A lifelong resident of Baldwinsville, Maya was very interested in art and had a great love for animals, the outdoors and being in nature were very important as was any time spent creating cherished memories with family and friends.
> 
> Surviving is mother, Karen Pickard; grandparents, Tom and Phyllis Pickard; uncle, Tom (Karen) Pickard; cousins, Ryan and Luke Pickard. Calling hours will be held from 4-6 PM, Friday, March 24, 2023 at Baldwinsville First United Methodist Church, 17 W. Genesee St., Baldwinsville. A Funeral Service will follow at 6 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Friends of Beaver Lake or Friends of Rosamond Gifford Zoo. Online at FalardeauFH.com
> 
> Published by Syracuse Post Standard from Mar. 22 to Mar. 23, 2023.
> 
> The Baldwinsville Central School District announced in an e-mail to families in the district that a student, Maya "Marshall" Pickard, died unexpectedly Monday.
> 
> Pickard was an 8th grader at Durgee Jr. High School, the district said.
> 
> Those with connections to the junior high school told CNY Central that Pickard was a transgender student.
