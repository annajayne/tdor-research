"La Pajarita" died by suicide in Santa Cruz de Yojoa, Cortés (Honduras) on or shortly before 29 Oct 2023.

She was 20.



> Member of the LGTBI community took his own life inside his home in Santa Cruz de Yojoa
> 09:57 AM 29 Oct 2023
> A 20-year-old young man known as 'La Pajarita', a member of the Lesbian, gay, transsexual, bisexual and intersex (LGTBI) community, was found dead this Sunday inside his home.
> 
> The young man identified as Miltón Joel Vásquez Sánchez was found hanging from a beam in his bedroom, in the El Cajón sector, in the municipality of Santa Cruz de Yojoa, in northern Honduras, according to information from La Prensa Santacruzana .
> 
> Members of the National Police arrived at the young man's house to carry out investigations and determine whether or not it was a suicide.
> 
> Milton's relatives did not want to provide details of why the young man would have made the fatal decision.
> 
> Some residents of the sector, through social networks, lamented the death of "La Pajarita" since he was someone known in the area.
> 
> In the last few hours, another suicide was recorded in Comayagua, central Honduras, a 20-year-old girl made the decision to take her own life with an electricity cable, leaving her one-year-old son alone.
> 
> According to data from the Ministry of Security, from 2013 to June of this year, 3,911 people took their lives.
