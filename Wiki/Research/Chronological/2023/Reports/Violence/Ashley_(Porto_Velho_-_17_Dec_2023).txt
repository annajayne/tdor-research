> Transvestite is strangled to death in front of a motel near Avenida Rio Madeira
> A 34-year-old transvestite, identified as Jeferson de Freitas, known as Asheley, was strangled to death in the early hours of this Sunday (17/12). The crime took place in front of a motel located on Rua Caparari, close to Avenida Rio Madeira and BR-364, in Porto Velho (RO).
> 
> According to information gathered by the Jornal Eletr么nico Portal de Rond么nia team, the transvestite was in front of the motel, when a man on a motorcycle arrived, stopped her and dragged her to a nearby bush.
> 
> Witnesses reported that they heard the victim screaming for help and then saw the man leaving the scene on the red motorcycle.
> 
> Rescuers from the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu) were called, but when they arrived at the scene, the victim was already dead.
> 
> Military police officers recorded the incident. The crime took place during the early hours of the morning and until 9am this Sunday morning, forensic experts had not yet arrived at the scene of the crime.
> 
> Only after the forensic work has been carried out will the body be removed to the Legal Medical Institute (IML). The case will be investigated by the Specialized Crimes Against Life Police Station.
