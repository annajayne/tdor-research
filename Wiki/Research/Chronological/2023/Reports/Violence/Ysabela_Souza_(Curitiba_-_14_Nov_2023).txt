> Mother of trans woman who died after having her throat cut at the Center reveals last conversation with her daughter
> The crime may have been committed during a romantic encounter
> By Francielly Azevedo and Djalma Malaquias on November 16, 2023 at 10:37 am .
> In topic:Grief
> The mother of Isabely Moreira, a trans woman murdered with a stab wound to the neck in the center of Curitiba, revealed to Banda B what her last conversation with her daughter was like. Elza Souza da Silva said, this Thursday morning (16), that the victim was a calm person and that the crime may have been committed during a romantic encounter.
> 
> isabely trans murdered in the center
> Isabely was murdered with a stab wound to the neck (Personal Collection)
> Isabely, 38 years old, was killed last Tuesday night (14) on the corner of Alameda Cabral and Avenida Princesa Isabel . Even with the deep cut in her throat, she ran a few meters in search of help.
> 
> Dona Elza, who lives in São Vicente in the state of São Paulo, received the news from a friend of her daughter.
> 
> “She just said: Isa was stabbed by someone, Mrs. Elza. And then I asked: is she alive? And she replied: no, Dona Elza. She is dead,” she said.
> 
> The mother said she spoke to her daughter on Tuesday afternoon and that she seemed happy in the messages.
> 
> “I had talked to her during the afternoon about a bag of mine that was with her and she ended the conversation with 'kkk'. It was the last message […] She was a kind person to everyone, she respected me a lot as a mother, she had her life like everyone else has, she was a very loving person with her brothers”, she lamented.
> 
> According to Elza, her daughter had other visits to Curitiba, but had been living here permanently for two weeks.
> 
> “She came here calmly, she was happy. She had been here for two weeks, but she had been there other times years ago,” she said.
> 
> According to her mother, Isabely had not reported any problems or threats.
> 
> “”I never said I had received a threat or said to me 'mother, someone is bothering me' [...] Nobody knows if she had met anyone. No one knows if she was in a relationship with anyone. No one knows if she had an appointment at that time. She always told me: Mom, I found someone and I'm dating. But she hadn’t said anything,” she detailed.



> The trans woman who died  after being  stabbed in the throat in the center of Curitiba has been identified. The case happened after the victim had an encounter with a man on Tuesday night (14). Ysabela Souza, 35 years old, worked in a beauty salon.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, witnesses stated that she had gone out with a man hours before the attack. After being  stabbed in the throat, she tried to run to escape: “There are some drops of blood approximately 100 meters from here, which indicates that she had been attacked in a location other than where she fell,” say the police.
> 
> The crime was recorded on Rua Alameda Cabral on the corner of Avenida Princesa Isabel. After the attack, employees of a bar in the region called the Military Police (PM). Siate was also called to help the victim.


> Trans woman dies after being stabbed in the throat after encounter with man
> November 15, 2023 at 08:33
> A trans woman died after being stabbed in the throat in the center of Curitiba . The case happened after the victim had an encounter with a man on Tuesday night (14).
> 
> According to the Civil Police, witnesses stated that she had gone out with a man hours before being attacked. After being stabbed in the throat , she apparently tried to run to escape: “There are some drops of blood approximately 100 meters from here, which indicates that she was attacked in a location other than where she fell,” say the police.
> 
> The crime was recorded on Rua Alameda Cabral on the corner of Avenida Princesa Isabel. Subsequently, employees of a bar in the region called the Military Police (PM). Siate was also called to help the victim.
> 
> “I ask that the community here in the center of Curitiba engage in this investigation, call 0800643121 and tell us what you know. Mainly people here in the neighborhood who want to get rid of aggressors here,”
> 
> The police stated that what is known so far is that the victim had claimed that she was going out to meet a man and was later found dead. She was stabbed in the throat.
> 
> “Residents here in the region who see any dynamics in their monitoring system that could contribute to clarifying this fact, please call the division or hand over the files that are necessary for clarification”, concludes the delegate.
> 
> The woman lived close to the crime scene and worked in a beauty salon in Curitiba. The victim was already unconscious when security teams arrived and, a moment later, she succumbed to her injuries.
