stabbed


> Transvestite identified only as Tieta, was found dead inside the residence where she lived on Rua José Simplício da Costa Marques, in Bairro Primavera 4, in Bela Vista, 310 kilometers from Campo Grande.
> 
> According to information from the Jatobanews website, on Wednesday night (25), she was accompanied by her boyfriend, when, at around 10pm, he stabbed her body and then locked Tieta in the room and disappeared. . 
> 
> Tieta's body was only found this Thursday (26), by family members.
> 
> Police authorities were at the scene and began investigations in order to elucidate the case and discover the motivation for the crime.
