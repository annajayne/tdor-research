> PRESS RELEASE: TRANSFEMICIDE TAKES THE LIFE OF HIS TRANS PARTNER, AFTER STABBING HER IN BUENA FE, LOS RÍOS, ECUADOR
> April 7, 2023 
> The Quito Trans Psycho Center of the Silueta
> 
> Ceci (Sipiy Rune Coding CPTSX002311146), better known as “La Cebollita”, 31 years old, was murdered with two stab wounds early this Thursday, April 6, 2023 in the sector known as “October 19” of the Buena Fe canton. , in the province of Los Ríos.
> 
> The incident occurred inside his apartment, where the victim's transfemicide arrived with a knife to stab him twice at the height of the neck and arm. His body was left lying lifeless, while the culprit fled. Unofficial sources affirm that it would be his ex-partner who would have taken his life, since he had a history of assault that was recorded on video and circulated on networks.  
> 
> For what has been described, we call on the Attorney General of the State, Diana Salazar, the Commander of the Police, Carlos Cabrera, the Minister of the Ministry of Women and Human Rights, Paola Flores, the Undersecretary of Diversities Alexander Guano, the defender of People of Ecuador (e), Cesar Córdoba Valverde, to carry out the necessary coordination to find the culprit or perpetrators of this fact that mourns our community.
> 
> The Runa Sipiy Report of the Silueta
> 
> Quito, Friday, April 7, 2023
