> Transvestite is killed with a knife in the chest in Cidade de Deus
> By Redação 7 on September 7, 2023 at 12:34 | Updated September 7, 2023 at 1:45 pm
> 
> Manaus – A transvestite, who was not identified, was thrown dead on Thursday morning (7), on Rua Goiás, in the Cidade de Deus neighborhood, in the north zone.
> According to police, the victim was killed with a stab wound to the chest and was thrown from a car with an unidentified license plate.
> 
> The victim's identity has not yet been discovered, but according to police, she had long, dark hair and was wearing a pink dress when she was found.
> 
> The police were at the scene and, after examination, sent the body to the IML. The investigation team must check if there are security cameras that may have recorded the criminals' actions.
