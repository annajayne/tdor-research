CHILPANCINGO, Gro., October 15, 2023.- A transgender woman was shot to death in a taxi near the El Tomatal community in the municipality of Iguala.

The crime occurred around 9 a.m. on the Mexico-Acapulco highway, when a call to 911 reported an armed attack on the passenger of a taxi with economic number 0046.

The victim was traveling as co-pilot in the white Tsuru-type Nissan vehicle and suffered gunshot wounds to the head and body.

Military and state police cordoned off the crime scene.
