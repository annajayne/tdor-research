> Saturday morning in the Cariri region was marked by the murder of two women within an hour. Around 3:30 am Thamires da Silva, 30 years old, “Tiago”, was killed with several shots to her head near her home on Rua Mariana Dantas de Medeiros (Bairro Triângulo). The crime took place in the vicinity of Praça do Prourb and she even tried to run to escape the line of executioners who lured the transsexual into a conversation and executed her.
