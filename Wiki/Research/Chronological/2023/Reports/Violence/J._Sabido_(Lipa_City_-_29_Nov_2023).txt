====== J. Sabido (Lipa City - 29 Nov 2023) ======
Created Thursday 17 October 2024
@violence


J. Sabido [Julio Sabido]
63
29 Nov 2023
Lipa City
Batangas
Philippines
stabbed


> Tragedy struck a [63]-year-old member of the lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and queer (LGBTQ) group when his body was found with stab wounds inside his room in their house in Lipa City, Batangas on Tuesday.
> 
> The victim, Julio Sabido, 63-years-old, was bathing in his own blood when his relatives discovered his body inside his room in their house in Purok 3, Barangay Tangway in Lipa City, Batangas at around eight o'clock in the morning.
> 
> According to the Lipa City police report, his relatives went to the victim's room before he came out to have breakfast.
> 
> They were alarmed when the victim did not answer so they forced open the bedroom door.
> 
> They were surprised by the body of the victim lying on the bed with stab wounds in different parts of the body.
> 
> During the police investigation, it was discovered that it was robbed by an unidentified assailant because money, jewelry and some belongings were missing.
> 
> The police investigation into the crime continues.
