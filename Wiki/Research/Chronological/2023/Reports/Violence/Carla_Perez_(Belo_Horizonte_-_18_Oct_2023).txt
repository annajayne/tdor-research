====== Carla Perez (Belo Horizonte - 18 Oct 2023) ======
Created Thursday 19 October 2023
@violence


Carla Perez
30
18 Oct 2023
Padre Eustáquio
Belo Horizonte
Brazil
shot


> A 30-year-old transvestite was shot dead in the early hours of Wednesday (18) in the Padre Eustáquio neighborhood, Northwest region of Belo Horizonte. The crime occurred in front of the Horácio de Miranda trench.
> 
> The first information released by the Military Police (PM) shows that three men participated in the crime. One of them fled in a silver Pálio car, heading towards the Center. The other two on foot.
> 
> Residents of the region said that the victim was accompanied by another transvestite and was murdered with three shots. She would be known in the region as Carla Perez and worked during the day in a supermarket on Avenida Dom Pedro II, and at night she would also perform sexual programs in the region.
> 
> The body was sent to the Legal Medical Institute (IML).

> The death occurred in a place known as a prostitution spot where trans and transvestite women stay. There is a suspicion that the crime occurred because of a debt from drug trafficking. The transvestite was shot to death
> 
> A transvestite was shot to death in the Padre Eustáquio neighborhood, in the Northwest Region of Belo Horizonte, in the early hours of this Wednesday (18).
> 
> The crime took place in a place known as a prostitution spot where trans and transvestite women stay.
> 
> According to the Military Police (PM), a silver car with three men passed by the scene, one of them got out and shot the victim three times. He fled on foot and the other two in a car.
> 
> There is a suspicion that the crime occurred because of a debt from drug trafficking.
> Civil Police expertise was called and the body was taken to the Legal Medical Institute (IML).
