Paola Portillo was shot in Baracoa, Cortés (Honduras) on 30 Dec 2023.


> On a tragic night in Baracoa, Cortés, Juan Iraeta Mejía, known as Paola Portillo, a member of the LGTBIQ+ community, lost his life in a shooting attack carried out by unknown persons.
> 
> She lived in Barrio Las Flores, Baracoa sector, and was recognized for her charisma and active participation in the community.
> 
> The victim was on the detour that leads to the village of Baracoa when she was violently attacked.
> 
> The police arrived at the scene to cordon off the area and wait for forensic medicine to carry out the removal of the body in accordance with legal procedures.
> 
> According to preliminary reports, Paola was attacked by armed individuals who, after committing the violent act, fled without leaving a trace.
> 
> The community, shocked by the tragic event, notified the National Police to arrive at the scene of the crime.
> 
> Inspection of the body confirmed that Paola no longer had vital signs, presenting multiple gunshot wounds.
> 
> The Forensic Medicine team proceeded to remove the body to perform the corresponding autopsy before being handed over to his relatives.
> 
> So far, the motive for the murder and the whereabouts of the perpetrators are unknown.
> 
> The authorities are carrying out the corresponding investigations, while the victim's relatives are crying out for justice.
