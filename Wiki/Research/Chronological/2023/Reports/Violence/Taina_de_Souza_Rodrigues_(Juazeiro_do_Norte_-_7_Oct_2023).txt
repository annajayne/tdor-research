> 21-year-old trans woman is murdered in Juazeiro do Norte
> 7/10/2023
> 
> Henrique Rodrigues de Souza Neto, whose social name was “Katarina”, died after being shot. The crime took place in Praça do Giradouro, in the Triângulo neighborhood.
> 
> A trans woman was murdered in the early hours of this Saturday (07) inJuazeiro do Norte. According to the Military Police,Henrique Rodrigues de Souza Neto, whose social name was “Katarina“, lost her life after being shot.
> 
> Preliminary information indicates that “Katarina” may have been a victim of transfeminicide. At around 4 am, the victim was in one of the bars close to Praça do Giradouro, in the Triângulo neighborhood, where the suspect was also present, a short, dark-skinned, bearded man, who was riding a white Bros model motorcycle with details. reds.
> 
> Shortly afterwards, they left the place and went to the square, where they began to argue. According to witnesses, shots were heard being fired. “Katarina” was hit in the armpit and back, dying at the scene. Authorities are analyzing images from security cameras near the crime scene in an attempt to identify the suspect.



> A transgender woman was shot dead in the early hours of Saturday (7), in Juazeiro do Norte, 491 km from Fortaleza. No one was arrested.
> 
> According to reports from residents, the victim Tainá de Souza Rodrigues (Catarina), aged 21, was drinking in a bar. The young woman went out to Praça do Giradouro, where she argued with a man.
> 
> Witnesses also stated that the victim tried to escape, but was hit by at least two shots. The shooter fled on a motorcycle.
> 
> Regarding the case, the Public Security Secretariat (SSPDS) reported that teams from the Military Police and Forensic Expertise (Pefoce) were called to respond to the homicide. According to preliminary investigations, the victim, who has not yet been formally identified, was hit by gunshots on a public street. The victim did not survive his injuries and died at the scene.
> 
> The case was registered at the Juazeiro do Norte Regional Police Station.
