> Man who stabbed and beaten trans people to death in Pedra Azul is arrested by police
> The suspect had been in a relationship for about a year with the 22-year-old woman; After being arrested in a bar, he said he killed her out of "excessive love"
> By José Vítor Camilo Published on December 22, 2023 | 02:07 am – Updated on December 22, 2023 | 02:07
> Suspect used a piece of wood and a knife to kill the victim - Photo: PC/DIVULGAÇÃO
> 
> Last Wednesday (December 20), a 25-year-old man was arrested on suspicion of stabbing and beating his own partner, a 22-year-old trans woman, in the city of Pedra Azul, in the Jequitinhonha Valley. The suspect had been in a relationship with the victim for about a year.
> 
> According to the Civil Police, the suspect was arrested red-handed for the crime, which took place in the Inconfidentes neighborhood in the city of around 25 thousand inhabitants. Investigations showed that, using a piece of wood and a knife, the young man had struck several blows to the head and also pierced the victim's neck.
> 
> After receiving the information, the Civil Police team went to the couple's residence, where they found the victim's body with signs of violence. Official forensics were called, carried out examinations at the crime scene and collected the piece of wood with blood stains found in one of the rooms.
> 
> Arrested in bar, suspect said he killed because he “loved too much”
> According to the police institution, after finding the victim's body, agents ended up locating the man in a bar during searches in the city.
