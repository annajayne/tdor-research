> Trans woman is murdered in the Canton of Huaquillas with multiple gunshots
> November 14, 2023 · from Advertiser · Bookmark the permalink . ·
> PUBLIC COMMUNITY : Trans companion is murdered in the Canton Huaquillas with multiple shots
> 
> With her transfemicide we surpassed figures from 2022 to date in 2023
> 
> The Quito Psycho Trans Center of the Silhouette X Association, members of the National Trans Revolution Platform (which covers rural, community and peripheral areas with its collectives) and the Ecuadorian Federation of LGBT+ Organizations, hereby make the following fact public.
> 
> María José (Runa Sipiy Code CPTSX0025121123), 30 years old, was the victim of a violent act perpetrated by individuals traveling on a motorcycle. The attackers shot the victim directly in the head, who, despite trying to flee, was hit by the shots.
> 
> The National Police of Ecuador, through the National Directorate of Crimes against Life, Violent Deaths (Dinased), informs the public about the unfortunate event that occurred in the early hours of Sunday, November 12 in the Huaquillas canton at 00:30 that day, at the intersection of 24 de diciembre and Huancavilca streets.
> 
> Based on the above, we call on the Attorney General of the State, Diana Salazar, the Police Commander, Fausto Salinas Samaniego, the Minister of the Ministry of Women and Human Rights, Paola Flores, the Undersecretary of Diversity, Alexander Guano, and the acting Ombudsman of Ecuador, Cesar Córdoba Valverde, to make the necessary arrangements to find the person(s) responsible for this event that has brought mourning to our community.
> 
> The Runa Sipiy Report of the Silhouette X Association, on transfemicides, LGBT+ murders, unsolved violent deaths or suspected criminality, records this case as the twenty-fifth of 2023, against our populations in Ecuadorian territory.
> 
> Guayaquil, Tuesday, November 14, 2023
