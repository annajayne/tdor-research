> The body of the transsexual known as Valentina, shot dead in Agudos do Sul (PR), was identified. This is Jian Carlos Felipe Guedes, 22 years old. The information was confirmed by family members to Portal Fazenda, in Fazenda Rio Grande (PR), where the case will be investigated by the Civil Police.
> 
> The brutal crime was committed late this Wednesday afternoon (15), in a nightclub on the banks of PR281, which connects Agudos do Sul and Piên, both in Paraná.
> 
> According to initial information, two individuals invaded the place carrying large-caliber weapons, and fired several shots at the victim. They then fled, taking the device that stores security camera images.
> 
> The young woman's family demands justice in the case. Valentina worked in a nightclub in São Bento do Sul (SC) and recently came to work at this nightclub in Agudos do Sul.

> A homicide was recorded around 6pm, this Wednesday, 15th, in a nightclub on the banks of PR 281, in the city of Agudos do Sul.
> 
> According to information, two men armed with a 12-gauge shotgun and a 9mm pistol entered the establishment and shot a transsexual who worked there.

> The transsexual identified as Valentina did not survive her injuries and entered death while still at the scene. The military police were called and took steps to capture those involved who fled the scene of the crime.

> The nightclub is the same one that suffered an arson attack some time ago. The victim's body was sent to the IML in Curitiba. The civil police investigate the case to discover the motivation for the crime.
