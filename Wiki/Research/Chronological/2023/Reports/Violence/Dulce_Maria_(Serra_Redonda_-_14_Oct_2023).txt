Serra Redonda
Paraíba
Brazil
shot



> A trans woman was murdered with a gunshot to the face inside her home, on Friday night (14), in the city of Serra Redonda, in the interior of Paraíba . According to the Military Police (PM), the crime took place on Rua Francisco Clementino de Andrade.
> 
> To Portal T5 , the Itabaiana sectional delegate, Eraldo Barbosa, informed that the victim was identified as Dulce Maria, aged 47 . According to the Civil Police, the woman died from just one gunshot that also hit her neck.
> 
> According to the PM, the suspects were in a car when they committed the crime. In images posted on social media, one of the men gets out of the vehicle and, carrying a long gun, enters the residence and fires a shot. 
> 
> The authorship and motivation of the crime are unknown. The Civil Police will investigate the case.
