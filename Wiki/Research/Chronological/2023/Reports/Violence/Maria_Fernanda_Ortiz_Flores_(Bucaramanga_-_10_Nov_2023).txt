murdered


> A woman from the LGBT community was found dead in an apartment in Bucaramanga
> Authorities are investigating the death of a 44-year-old woman who was found dead inside an apartment in the Diamante II neighborhood of Bucaramanga.
> 
> A sister of the woman found dead was the one who notified the police that she had been found dead at 3:45 p.m. on Friday, November 10. She lived at Carrera 24 and Calle 87, on the fourth floor of an apartment in the Diamante II neighborhood.
> 
> The woman who belongs to the LGTBI community was identified as María Fernanda Ortiz Flórez, 43 years old, single and dedicated to various jobs and on December 29 she would turn 44 years old.
> 
> The sister told the officers that the last time they spoke was last Monday, November 6, at 9:30 p.m.
> 
> "As he did not answer his cell phone in the last few days, today the sister decided to go to the apartment, but when she knocked, no one opened the door. So she hired a locksmith and when they entered the apartment they found the lifeless body in one of the rooms," said the police.
> 
> The body was transferred by Sijín to the Institute of Legal Medicine of Bucaramanga in the Campo Hermoso neighborhood and the causes of death are yet to be established.
> 
> On social media, her friends and loved ones have expressed their sadness upon learning of the death of Maria Fernanda Ortiz Flórez.
> 
> “May God welcome you into his holy kingdom. Thank you for all the advice and beautiful things you have given me. I am an example of a trans woman. I am proud of you,” said one of her friends.
