> The Quito Trans Psycho Center of the Silueta
> 
> Giyo (Sipiy Rune Coding CPTSX00231361), 28 years old, from Guayaquil, Guayas province, Ecuador, was in the north of the city, specifically in Sauces 6, apparently delivering as a way to make a living. According to statements from the authorities, in that sector she would have started a conversation with a person who takes care of cars in the sector. During that conversation, they were intercepted by motorized vehicles who shot at both of them, killing Giyo on the spot. The motive for the murder is unknown and whether it was directed at Giyo or the person he had conversations with.
> 
> For what has been described, we call on the Attorney General of the State, Diana Salazar, the Commander of the Police, Fausto Salinas Samaniego, the Minister of the Ministry of Women and Human Rights, Paola Flores, the Undersecretary of Diversities Alexander Guano, the defender of the People of Ecuador (e), Cesar Córdoba Valverde, to carry out the necessary coordination to find the culprit or perpetrators of this fact that mourns our community.



> A 'delivery' and a guard were murdered in a parking lot on Sauces 6, in Guayaquil

> A 'delivery' and a guard were murdered in a parking lot on Sauces 6, in GuayaquilNone of the deceased had a criminal record. ( Pixabay )
> A woman and a man were murdered on the night of this Wednesday, May 31, after being shot at in a parking lot in Sauces 6 , in the north of Guayaquil .
> 
> According to police officer Yuri Narváez, head of the Model District , two subjects traveling on a motorcycle opened fire on the female, who worked as a delivery worker and was traveling in a similar vehicle; however, the projectiles also hit the parking guard.
> 
> The woman was identified as Giannina León Castillo and was approximately 28 years old. Her body was left lying on the road. Instead, the man, who responded to the name of Jordy Roque Escalante, was transferred to a health home, but did not survive.
> 
> None of the deceased had a criminal record. Criminalistics and Dinased agents went to the parking lot and the hospital to remove the bodies and collect evidence and testimonies.
