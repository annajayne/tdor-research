> The friends of this 22-year-old girl do not know anything about Meli's family, as they only knew that she and her family are originally from the Sierra Norte de Puebla
> Fernanda Escobedo
> @mf_escobedo
> 
> At the time of being found by the authorities, inside her apartment in a neighborhood of the Historic Center on November 11 , Melani's body had already been lifeless for seven days; Currently, the remains of this trans woman are still kept in the Semefo and for this reason they have not been able to say goodbye to her properly , since no one from her family has come forward to claim her, so a search was started on networks to find them.
> 
> This was made known through social networks by friends close to Melani N, the trans woman who was found lifeless in her apartment in the Historic Center , who only count as information that she and her loved ones are from Zacapoaxtla or Teziutlán.
> 
> “Help to find the relatives of the companion known as Meli or Melani, her body remains unknown and could go to the mass grave,” her friends wrote on social networks.
> 
> Melani N, 22 years old or "Meli" was found dead inside her apartment in a neighborhood of the Historic Center located on 10 Oriente and 6 Norte, although her body was in an advanced state of decomposition, it was detected that she was He had signs of violence.
> 
> Now it was revealed that those signs of violence that the young woman had on her body were some ropes around her neck and wooden boards on top, according to testimonies from her close friends.
> 
> It should be noted that her case continues with the latent suspicion that it could have been a hate crime and transfemicide, the Prosecutor's Office has already opened an investigation folder to clarify her death, which the governor of the state of Puebla Sergio Salomón Céspedes asked that Everything possible is done so that this alleged homicide is investigated to the fullest extent.
> 
> In addition, the Puebla Human Rights Commission, CDH, issued a complaint letter due to the alleged violation of Melani's human rights, which is why it also demanded that her case be investigated with a gender perspective. In turn, the agency is in search for his family to grant him the corresponding protection measures.




> A transsexual person is killed inside a neighborhood in the Historic Center of Puebla, this Saturday the authorities located the putrefied body. However, they did not provide information on the cause of death or the injuries on the body.
> 
> Email Address
> Around noon this Saturday the discovery occurred, in a property located on 10 Oriente Street, between 4 and 6 North of the capital of Puebla.
> 
> Neighbors of the property called the emergency number 911 to report a foul odor coming from an apartment in a neighborhood. Likewise, they revealed that they had smelled this smell for two days, so municipal police and paramedics came.
> 
> Municipal Civil Protection (PC) medical emergency technicians entered the property and confirmed that there was a lifeless person in an advanced state of decomposition.


> The victim was identified as "Melani"
> Although the motive for the murder of the trans woman who was found dead inside her apartment in the city center last Saturday has not yet been determined , investigations with a gender perspective will be carried out .
> 
> Gaby revealed that the victim has been identified as “Melani” and apparently had no family, so the community will be responsible for receiving the body in the coming days to give it proper burial, trusting that the State Attorney General's Office will agree to hand it over.
> 
> The trans activist added that it is still not known if it was a hate crime or was due to a violent home robbery, since on the one hand the body was tied hand and foot and showed signs of violence; But on the other hand, her apartment was in disarray and Melani's clothes, documents, and other belongings were missing from the place.
