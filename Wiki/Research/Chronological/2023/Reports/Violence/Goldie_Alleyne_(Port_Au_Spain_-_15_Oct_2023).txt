> A man identified as Marvin Alleyne—also known as "Goldie" and "Marva"—was stabbed to death and his body left at the side of the road at Lady Chancellor Hill in Port-of-Spain.
> 
> Police said around 4 am yesterday a passerby saw Alleyne, who was clad only in women’s garment, lying on the ground in the vicinity of the Horticulture Centre.
> 
> The man appeared to be dead.
> 
> The police were called in and arrived on the scene shortly thereafter.
> 
> Police said Alleyne's body bore several stab wounds.
> 
> Investigators are working on the theory that Alleyne was a “cross-dresser” and his killing may have been a “hate crime.”
> 
> His body was removed from the area and taken to the Forensic Science Centre for identification pending an autopsy.
> 
> Investigations are continuing.
