Shiirya was stabbed in Iguala, Guerrero (Mexico) on 25 Aug 2023.


> Iguala, Guerrero.— A transgender woman was stabbed to death in the center of Iguala, in the north of Guerrero.
> 
> Shiirya, would have been attacked during an alleged assault. Police reports indicate that the crime occurred early this Friday morning.
> 
> Information from security authorities indicates that the crime against Shiirya occurred while he was walking between Maya and Bravo streets, in the center of Iguala.
> 
> The woman was stabbed several times, then the attacker fled and has not yet been arrested.
> 
> Paramedics attended the scene and transferred her to the Jorge Soberón Acevedo General Hospital, where she died.
