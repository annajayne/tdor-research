Pirapora do Bom Jesus

Brazil
murdered



> Anna Luisa Pantaleão, a trans woman from Alagoas, was found dead on the side of a highway, located in Pirapora do Bom Jesus, in São Paulo, this Tuesday (19).
> 
> She had been missing since September 4th. The suspect, according to the São Paulo press, is a ride-hailing driver and confessed to the crime. He is 21 years old, confessed to the crime and pointed out where he left the body.
> 
> The victim was 19 years old and was born in Pão de Açúcar, in the Sertão de Alagoas, where his family lives. However, she lived in São Paulo.
> 
> As shown by the São Paulo press, Anna Luisa's family was at the police station investigating the case, where they met the suspect, creating confusion at the scene.
> 
> Friends and family mourned the young woman's death. "My little girl. So sweet, always smiling, I can't believe they did such cruelty to you. You will always be alive in my memories and in my heart", declared a friend of Anna Luísa on social media.
