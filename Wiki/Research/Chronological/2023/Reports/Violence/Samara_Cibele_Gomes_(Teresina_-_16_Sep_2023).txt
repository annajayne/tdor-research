====== Samara Cibele Gomes (Teresina - 16 Sep 2023) ======
Created Monday 18 September 2023
@violence



Samara Cibele Ferreira Gomes (also identified as Samara Amaral in one report)
31
2023_09_16_Samara-Cibele-Gomes.jpg
https://cidadeverde.com/noticias/399524/travesti-morta-a-tiros-e-identificada-e-estava-com-saida-temporaria-de-presidio
16 Sep 2023
Rua Pedro Brito & Rua Canindé
Parque Alvorada
Teresina
Piauí
Brazil



> A transvestite, still unidentified, was murdered with at least four gunshots on Saturday night (16) in Parque Alvorada, north of Teresina.
> 
> A10 learned from the 9th Battalion of the Piauí Military Police that the victim was in the company of another person when the criminals, who have not yet been identified, arrived and fired several shots. The transvestite was shot in the head.
> 
> The victim died at the scene. People in the region did not know the victim. The Institute of Legal Medicine (IML) was called and removed the transvestite's body. The motivation for the murder is still unknown.
> 
> Police teams also carried out investigations in an attempt to locate and arrest the perpetrators of the crime, but were unsuccessful. The case will be investigated by the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP).
