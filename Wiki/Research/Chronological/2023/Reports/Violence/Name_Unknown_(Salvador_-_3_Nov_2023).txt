> Two women, one of whom was transsexual, were shot dead in Salvador, on Avenida Aliomar Baleeiro, known as Estrada Velha do Aeroporto. The bodies were located on Friday (3).
> 
> According to the Civil Police, no one has yet been arrested and none of the victims have been identified so far, as they were without documents. There are no details about the motivation and authorship of the double homicide.
> 
> The crime is investigated by the Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP). According to the Police, the women's bodies were sent to the Technical Police Department (DPT) for examination.
