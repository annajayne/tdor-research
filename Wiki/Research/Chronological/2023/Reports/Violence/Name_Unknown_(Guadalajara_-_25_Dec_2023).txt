> Security in Jalisco: Man kills his trans sister for defending his parents in Tlaquepaque
> SECURITY. The crime took place in the Francisco I. Madero Colony in Tlaquepaque. Photo: Reforma Agency
>  
> Monday, December 25, 2023 · 18:49
> Guadalajara.- Seeing that his trans sister was trying to attack her parents with a knife, the older brother disarmed her and stuck it in her neck, causing her death.
> 
> The deceased suffered from schizophrenia and had not taken her medication to control herself, which could have influenced the conflict.
> 
> The events were recorded this Monday in a home located in the Francisco I. Madero Colony, in Tlaquepaque.
> 
> According to the authorities, the trans woman arrived at the house in the morning and began to argue with her parents.
> 
> At one point, he took a knife about 20 centimeters long and tried to attack them both.
> 
> The incident was seen by the brother, who intervened and struggled with her for a moment in order to defend the parents, both over 60 years old.
> 
> The sister, around 25 years old, gave him a superficial wound in the chest, however, he lost control of the situation later and died with the same weapon.
> 
> The tragedy occurred at Juan José Castillo 293, near the intersection with Santa Lucía.
> 
> The parents said that their daughter suffered from schizophrenia and that she had not taken the prescribed medication when she should have, to control the episodes. The son was presented before the State Prosecutor's Office while it is determined whether he is susceptible to the benefit that would exclude him from criminal responsibility.
