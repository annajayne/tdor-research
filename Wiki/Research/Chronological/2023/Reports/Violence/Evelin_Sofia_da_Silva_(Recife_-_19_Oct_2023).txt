> Trans woman is shot dead in her home
> Crime happened in Afogados, in the West Zone of Recife
> By: Wilson Maranhão
> Published on: 10/19/2023 09:57 | Updated on: 10/19/2023 15:21
> She was killed by several gunshots, in the neighborhood of Afogados, in the North Zone of Recife. (Photo: Reproduction/Personal archive)
> 
> The Pernambuco Civil Police (PCPE) opened an investigation to investigate the murder of a 24-year-old trans woman. She was killed by several gunshots, in the neighborhood of Afogados, in the North Zone of Recife. The case happened on Wednesday (18), but confirmed by the corporation this Thursday (19).
> 
> The Department of Homicide and Personal Protection (DHPP) will conduct the investigations. Although the police did not reveal the name of the victim, preliminary information suggests that she is Evelin Sofia da Silva. 
> 
> According to the corporation, she was found by military police officers lifeless and with several gunshot wounds. The crime took place inside the victim's residence, in the community of Caranguejo Tabajaras, on the border between Ilha do Retiro and Afogados. 
> 
> The victim worked as a trader and produced hot lunches to sell in the locality. The Capital Homicide Task Force and the Criminalistics Institute (IC) were at the scene to carry out the first investigations into the crime and the examination of the victim's body. So far, there is no information about the author or motivation of the crime. 
> 
> The woman's body was sent to the Institute of Legal Medicine (IML), in the Santo Amaro neighborhood, in the Central area of ??Recife. 
> 
> Statistics
> 
> Pernambuco led the ranking of murders of trans people across the country in 2022. The data comes from the National Association of Transvestites and Transsexuals (Antra), which, through a dossier, showed that the state recorded 13 cases of Intentional Lethal Violent Crimes (CVLIs) against trans people. 
> 
> According to the entity, at the time, Pernambuco moved from 5th position in the ranking to take first place, followed by São Paulo which fell from 1st to 2nd position and Ceará which went from 4th to 3rd in 2022, with 11 cases each. Minas Gerais maintained the nine cases from the previous year and Rio de Janeiro, which had 12 murders in 2021, closed 2022 with eight. 
>  
> Amazonas had eight cases and Bahia 7. Paraná, Pará and Espírito Santo had six murders each; Goiás, Mato Grosso, Mato Grosso do Sul and Sergipe with five cases each. Followed by Alagoas, Paraíba and Maranhão with four murders in each state and Rio Grande do Norte with 3. We also had two cases in Rio Grande do Sul, Santa Catarina and the Federal District. A murder in Rondônia, Piauí and Roraima.
