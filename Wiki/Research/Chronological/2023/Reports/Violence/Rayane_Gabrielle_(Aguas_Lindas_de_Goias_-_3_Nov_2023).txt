> Rayane Gabrielle died after being attacked with a tire iron — Photo: Disclosure/Civil Police 
> 
> A young man was arrested on suspicion of killing a transvestite with a tire iron after a program, in Cristalina, on the outskirts of the Federal District. According to the Civil Police, the crime occurred on November 3, in Águas Lindas de Goiás.
> 
> g1 Goiás was unable to locate the suspect's defense until the last update of this report.
> 
> Delegate Vinícius Máximo, from the Civil Police, reported that the case happened when the victim Rayane Gabrielle, aged 23, and another friend, performed sexual programs and, on the day of the incident, the investigated DRN, aged 27, together with another man, hired the two professionals.
> 
> Máximo said that after finishing the job, the men refused to take the women back to where they were. Then, the four started an argument and Rayane stated that they would leave on foot, but that she would tell the young men's wives everything that happened, since she knew them.
> 
> At this time, the suspects went towards the victims, but only managed to reach Rayane. The two violently attacked her with a tire iron. The other woman managed to escape.
> 
> At the time, the victim had been rescued and taken to a local hospital in serious condition. Due to the seriousness of the case, the transvestite was taken to a hospital in Goiânia, but she did not survive her injuries and ended up dying.
> 
> The suspect was arrested on Saturday (9). The deputy reported that he has a conviction for robbery. The man who helped the suspect commit the crime is on the run. The Civil Police are investigating the case.
