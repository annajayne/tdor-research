> In a shocking incident from Guwahati, a body of a transgender person was found under mysterious circumstances in the city's Chandmari locality today.
> 
> The body was discovered inside a sack at a canal close to the Assam Engineering Institute hostel. 
> 
> Following the discovery, a team of Chandmari police led by Additional Deputy Commissioner of Police Nandini Kakati reached the spot to investigate the matter.
> 
> It is being suspected that miscreants may have killed the transgender person in a planned manner and later dumped the body inside a sack. 
> 
> Meanwhile, a team of forensic and CID Assam also reached the site and sent the body for post-mortem. The identity of the victim has not yet been ascertained.
> 
> On the other hand, the body of a youth was recovered in Guwahati's Uzan Bazar area today as well. 
> 
> The deceased youth has been identified as one Rupam Das, a resident of Beltol near Kharghuli area. While further details are awaited in the matter, the victim's family has alleged murder.
