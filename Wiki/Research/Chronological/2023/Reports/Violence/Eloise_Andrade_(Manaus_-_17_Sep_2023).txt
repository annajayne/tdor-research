> Trans woman and cousin are shot dead in the east of Manaus
> The cousins had just left a family member's house when they were surprised by the criminals
> Published on September 17, 2023 at 09:25
> 
> Manaus- Trans woman Eloise Andrade and her cousin Lucas Paiva were shot to death on Saturday night (17), on Rua Comandante Lacerda, Cidade Alta community, Jorge Teixeira neighborhood, east of the capital of Amazonas.
> 
> According to information, the cousins had just left a family member's house when they were surprised by armed suspects who fired several firearms at them. The victims did not survive their injuries and died instantly.
> 
> According to the information, the criminals used a 16-gauge weapon to murder the victims. Brain masses were found scattered across the floor.
> 
> There is no information about the motivation for the crime. The case must be investigated by the Specialized Homicide and Kidnapping Police Station (DEHS).
> 
> The victims' bodies were removed by the Legal Medical Institute (IML).
