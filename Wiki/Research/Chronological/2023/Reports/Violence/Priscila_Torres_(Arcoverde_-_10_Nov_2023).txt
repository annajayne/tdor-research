> MISSING HOMOSEXUAL FOUND DEAD IN ARCOVERDE
> A homosexual was found murdered this morning Friday (10), near a daycare in the municipality of Arcoverde, in Sertão de Pernambuco.
> The body of Josenildo Bispo da Silva, 40-year-old Priscila Torres, as she was known, was located in a field with a neck injury.
> THE LAW OF SILENCE
> At the crime scene, no one wanted to provide any information that would help police identify the perpetrator.
> Security cameras showed when the victim left the house around 02:30 last Wednesday (08) and did not return. Making relatives arrive until they announce their disappearance in the
> social networks
> After a thorough investigation carried out by the Criminal Investigation Institute (IC), the body was sent to the Institute of Forensic Medicine (IML) of Recife.
> INVESTIGATE
> The case is being investigated by the City Civil Police.
