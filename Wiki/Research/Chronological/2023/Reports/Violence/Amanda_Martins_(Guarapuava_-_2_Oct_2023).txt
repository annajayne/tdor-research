> Transvestite Amanda Martins was found stabbed to death in the early hours of Monday (2) in the Morro Alto neighborhood, in Guarapuava , in the central region of Paraná.
> 
> Amanda's body was found by a young man, who heard the victim's dog making a lot of noise and went to see what was happening. She was lying near the door and was covered in blood.
> 
> The Civil Police are investigating the motive for the death. The body will be buried this Tuesday (3), in the Santo Antônio cemetery. The time has not yet been announced.
