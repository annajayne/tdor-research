> Transvestite is found dead in VG's residence
> 
> A transvestite, known as Lorraine, was found dead, on Saturday afternoon (25.11), in a residence in the Santa Izabel neighborhood, in Várzea Grande.
> 
> According to the incident, the police were called by the victim's neighbor, who went to Lorraine's residence, due to the strong odor of the place, and found the victim's body in a decomposed state.
> 
> According to the witness, the transvestite had come from Pernambuco and had no family in the city.
> 
> The military police called the Mobile Emergency Care Service (Samu), which confirmed the death and reported that the victim showed no signs of violence.
> 
> The team from the Specialized Homicide and Personal Protection Police Station was called to take over the incident.
> 
> The Civil Police investigates the case.
