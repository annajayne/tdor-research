> Operations Center - COPOM of the 10th BPM, in the early hours of October 30, 2023, in the Parati 2000 neighborhood, in Assú, where a transvestite had been found dead inside a house, was concluded after official identification by the Instituto Técnico Cientifico de Perícia - ITEP de Mossoró, which was called to the scene of the incident together with the civil police, to carry out the necessary procedures, even though the body did not show signs of violence.
> 
> The ITEP team identified the body, and it is Denisval Brilhante de Souza, 28 years old, born in Fortaleza, Ceará. The body was found on Manoel rua Albano Sobrinho in the Parati 2000 neighborhood. It is likely that death was due to a sudden illness.
> 
> After exchanging information with the forensic institute in Fortaleza, it was possible to obtain the official identification of the transvestite, who had no documents / or were not found at the scene.
