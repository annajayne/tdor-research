> This Monday, November 13, family and friends confirmed, through social networks, the death of Chenoa Vázquez, a trans woman from Potosí, who was reported missing since November 7 , the last time she was seen was in the housing unit. Azaleas from the municipality of Soledad de Graciano Sánchez.
> 
> So far, state authorities have not issued information in this regard and her family and friends have not made known the causes of Chenoa's death; however, they have expressed outrage about hate crimes against people from the LGBT+ community in the country. country.
> 
> During the search process, the LGBTTTIQAP+ community condemned the State Attorney General's Office (FGE) for issuing a search file for the young woman with her name José Ernesto Vázquez Puente and male photographs, which did not identify her .
> 
> This death was announced on the same day that the murder of Judge Ociel Baena in Aguascalientes was announced.
