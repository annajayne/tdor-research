Name Unknown
31 Oct 2023
Tobias Barreto
Sergipe
Brazil
not reported


> On Tuesday afternoon (31), the Sergipe Military Police received a report of a death in a residence in the municipality of Tobias Barreto, close to Espaço Águia.
> 
> Upon arriving at the scene, the Mobile Emergency Care Service (SAMU) was called, which confirmed the death of a trans woman, who was already without vital signs and with a stiff body.
> 
> The complaint was made by the victim's friend, who reported that she had been feeling unwell for a long time due to her high intake of alcoholic beverages.
> 
> The case was forwarded to the Integrated Public Security Operations Center (CIOSP), which contacted the Criminalist and the Legal Medical Institute for further investigations.
