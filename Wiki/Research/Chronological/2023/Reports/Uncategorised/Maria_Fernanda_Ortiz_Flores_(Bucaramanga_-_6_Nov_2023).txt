> Woman from the LGBTI community was found dead in Bucaramanga
> This is María Fernanda Ortiz Flores, 44 years old and member of the LGBTI community. She was found dead in her apartment, located in the Diamante Dos neighborhood, in Bucaramanga.
> Investigators analyze the causes of the death of this woman member of the LGBTI community in Bucaramanga -
> Updated: November 11, 2023 07:47 AM
> What happened to María Fernanda Ortiz Flores? That's what her family and friends are wondering, after this woman, a member of the LGBTI community , was found dead in her apartment in the city of Bucaramanga.
> 
> Blu Radio reported that, according to what the victim's sister told the Police, “Monday, November 6, around 9:30 at night, was the last time she had contact with her and, taking into account that María Fernanda did not return to answer “The phone or chat over the course of several days, he decided to go to the apartment where he lived, at which point he discovered that his sister was dead.”
> 
> María Fernanda Ortiz Flores was 44 years old, part of the LGBTI community, single and dedicated to various jobs. Her apartment was located on Carrera 24 and Calle 87, in the Diamastes Dos neighborhood, in the south of Bucaramanga.
> 
> The authorities have had a group of agents to investigate what happened to this woman and what was the cause of her death. The Blu Radio station assures that her body had no visible injuries or indications that she suffered a physical attack.
> 
> The neighbors of the apartment where María Fernanda lived, on the fourth floor, said they had not heard or seen anything unusual that would have alerted them that a serious situation was occurring in the home. They hope that security cameras in the area will be analyzed, among other elements.
> 
> The body of this woman, a member of the LGBTI community, was taken to the National Institute of Legal Medicine for the respective autopsy, which could accurately establish the details of this case.
