> COLUMBIA, S.C. (WACH) — Richland County deputies have confirmed that the body of missing transgender woman Shandon Floyd has been found.
> 
> Authorities say Floyd’s body was found just before midnight on November 14th around the 10000 block of Two Notch Road in Columbia.
> 
> The initial investigation found no signs of foul play.
> 
> According to family, Shandon was visiting Columbia from Mullins and was staying with friends at the Rodeway Inn hotel on Berkshire Drive.
> 
> Floyd was last seen in Columbia on November 8.
