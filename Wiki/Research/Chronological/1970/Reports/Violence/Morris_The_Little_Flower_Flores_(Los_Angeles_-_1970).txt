Morris Flores, known as ”The Little Flower”, was stabbed in the heart and killed by Nathaniel William, 23, in a LA gay bar in the 9500 block of South Main, Los Angeles, California. Their friend,  Gregario Garcia, known as ”The Model”, was also stabbed (by Nathaniel's twin brother, Lee Daniel Williams) and fighting for their life as the the report date.
