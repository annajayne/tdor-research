===== L.A. Drag Queen Killed in Brawl (a newspaper transfer) =====

In an L.A. gay bar in the 9500 block of South Main, two transvestites were stabbed, one fatally in a fight that reportedly began when someone attempted to "cut in” on a pair of dancers.

Morris Flores, known as ”The Little Flower” was dead of stab wounds, and his friend Gregario Garcia, ”The Model” was near death. Both were well known in the south-central Temple Street and Main Street areas for their fancy drag outfits.

The Little Flower was lavishly costumed in velvet and satin. He was dancing with a man when Nathaniel Williams cut in.

Flores called Garcia and the two went to a table occupied by Williams and his twin brother, Lee Daniel Williams, both 23. After an angry argument Nathaniel drew a knife and allegedly sank it into Flores’ heart, Garcia, also lavishly costumed turned and ran, reportedly pursued by Lee Williams.
