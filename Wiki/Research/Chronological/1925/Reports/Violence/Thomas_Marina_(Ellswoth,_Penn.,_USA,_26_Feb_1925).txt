===== Clad in Masquerade Dress, Man Found Slain =====

WASHINGTON, PA., Feb. 25—(Special.)—Clad in the woman's apparel he had worn to a costume ball last night, the body of Thomas Marina, aged 27, of Ellsworth, near here, was found in a ditch near his home early today. He had been shot twice through the chest. County detectives left here this afternoon to investigate the murder, for which no cause has been ascertained.
