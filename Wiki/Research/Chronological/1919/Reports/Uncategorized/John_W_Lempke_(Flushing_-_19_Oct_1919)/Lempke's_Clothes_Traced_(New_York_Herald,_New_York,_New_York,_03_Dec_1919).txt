===== LEMPKE'S CLOTHES TRACED. =====

==== Prosecutor Finds Where Woman's Costume Was Bought. ====

Assistant District Attorney William U. Morris of Queens announced yesterday that he had traced all the woman's clothing worn by John W. Lempke, at the time his body was found hanging In his motor boat; who the jobbers were, when and where the goods were sold, and that his Investigation has now narrowed down to two stores, one in College Point and one In Flushing. Mr. Morris stated that he was unable as yet to say at which one of these stores the goods were purchased.

The assistant prosecutor announced also that he has examined a number of new witnesses in his investigation into the death of the young man.
