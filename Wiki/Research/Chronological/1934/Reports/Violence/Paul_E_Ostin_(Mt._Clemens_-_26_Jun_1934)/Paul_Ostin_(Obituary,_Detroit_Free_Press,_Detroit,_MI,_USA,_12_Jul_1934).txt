====== Paul Ostin (Obituary, Detroit Free Press, Detroit, MI, USA, 12 Jul 1934) ======
Created Sunday 02 May 2021

Paul Ostin

Funeral services . will be held Friday at 10 a. m. in the A. H. Peters Funeral Home, 12057 Gratiot Ave., for Paul Ostin, of 14661 Hazelridge Ave.

Born 36 years ago In Hoboken, he had lived here 18 years and was employed by the Chrysler Corp. Hi. widow Estelle and two sons, Robert and Donald, survive.
