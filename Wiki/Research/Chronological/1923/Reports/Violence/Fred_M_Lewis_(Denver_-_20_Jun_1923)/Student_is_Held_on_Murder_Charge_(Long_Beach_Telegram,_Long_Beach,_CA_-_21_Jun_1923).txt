===== Student Is Held on Murder Charge =====

DENVER, Colo., June 21. Thomas Glyn, 20 year old college student, is held in jail here today of a charge of murder, following the stabbing of Fred Lewis, a former female impersonator in vaudeville. According to police. Glyn admitted the stabbing.

Glyn declared, police say, that Lewis lured him to a room in the latters downtown lodging house, where a "wild orgy" of dancing girls in diaphanous gowns was in progress.

According to Glyn's story to police, a "drinking bout" was being staged in the kitchen of Lewis rooming bouse. Mrs. Glyn, seven weeks' bride, is reported to have danced in flimsy garments.
