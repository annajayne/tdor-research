===== ON TRIAL FOR KILLING FEMALE IMPERSONATOR =====
DENVER, Colo, Oct. 8.—Impaneling the jury to try Thomas B. Glynn, Jr, accused of the murder here June 21 of F. M. Lewis, female impersonator, in a rooming house, was begun in district court here today. With Glynn, helping him to fight the charge, are his mother, Mrs. Mary Glynn Collins, of Colorado Springs, and his father, Thomas B. Glynn, Sr, said to be a prominent office holder in Louisiana.
