===== Dope, Guns Seized; 10 Are Arrested =====

**STAR STATE REPORT**

Gary, Ind. — Police confiscated a quantity of narcotics and three guns and arrested 10 persons yesterday in a raid on a room in the M & M Motel here. 

Twenty Gary policeman accompanied by Federal narcotics agents, used sledgehammers to break into the room.

Confiscated were 143 caps of suspected heroin, valued at between $1,500 and $2,000, a small quantity of marijuana, two shotguns, a pistol, some ammunition and $720 in cash.
