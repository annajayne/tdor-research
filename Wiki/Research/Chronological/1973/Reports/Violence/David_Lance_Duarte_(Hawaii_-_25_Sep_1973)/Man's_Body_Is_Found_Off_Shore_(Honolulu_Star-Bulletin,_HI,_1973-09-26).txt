===== Man's Body Is Found Off Shore =====

The body of Lance Duarte, 20, of 1475 Linapuni St., was discovered a short distance off shore at a recreation area in Barbers Point Naval Air Station yesterday.

Navy officials are investigating.

Duarte's body was discovered by a Navy wife who was horseback riding in the area.
