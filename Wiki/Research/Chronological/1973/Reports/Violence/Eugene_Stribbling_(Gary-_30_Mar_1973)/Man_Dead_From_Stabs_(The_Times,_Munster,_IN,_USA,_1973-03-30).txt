Man Dead From Stabs

GARY — The nude body of a 26-year-old man was found early this morning in his bedroom.

Police said Eugene Stribbling of 1517 Washington was wound with a six-inch butcher knife impaled in his midsection.

He had been stabbed 11 times.

According to police the body was dispovered at 3:30 .a.m. by a friend who became suspicious after failing to arouse anyone after two attempts at the door.

William Trice of 555 Durbin St., told police he stopped at the murdered man's apartment Thursday afternoon around 4:30 p.m. knocked on the door and left after not getting an answer.

Trice returned this morning and climbed-through a window after knocking on the door.
