The partially decomposed body of Charles E Dixon, 32, dressed in women's clothing and shot through the head, was found on 21 Feb, 1973, in the basement of their northwest Detroit home. They had last been seen on the 19th of Feb., 1973.

A friend, Curtis Hamell, 17, who had discovered the body, was held in the investigation.
